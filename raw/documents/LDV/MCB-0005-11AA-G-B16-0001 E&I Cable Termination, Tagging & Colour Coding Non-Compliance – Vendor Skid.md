All 28 items are non-functional in nature — they relate solely to cable identification (colour coding and tagging). No item affects electrical performance, signal integrity, SIL rating, or operability. Such items follow manufacturer’s standard and a requirement for a full conformance of vendor packages with the project standard seems unpractical. In fact, Vendor or manufacturer’s guarantees on package performance are still valid despite of such deviations.
