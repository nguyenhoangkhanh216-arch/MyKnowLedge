Title: OT Network system installation
Query:
Based on MCB requirement that an OT network system shall be installed on LDV-A Production
Platform, in-order to support the on-shore work, the following Contractor additional scope is
considered. Company please review and confirm on both MCB and Contractor scope so that
Contractor can start its work immediately to minimize impact to Contractor on-going work in the Yard.
Contractor additional scope is as follows:
-Provision of a removable support for an additional OT cabinet (Company supplied equipment) to be
installed at designated location in the Electrical room (Attachment #1).
-Provision of material, running, coiling up and sea fastening in-situ on-shore for two (02) power cables
of RUOU H-M 0.6/1kV 3C x 6mm² (L+N+E), IEC 60332 (approx. 20m length each) between the UPS
Distribution board (tag no. 13-DBV-11 ) and the new OT cabinet.
-Provision of material, running, coiling up and sea fastening in-situ on-shore for two (02) 02 fiber optic
cables 24 Cores x 40m, single mode between the ICSS Network cabinet (tag no. 13-SC-9703) and
the new OT cabinet.
 
Besides, in order to complete work, MCB shall:
-Deliver the OT cabinet to the Contractor Yard latest by 08-Jun-2026.
-Procure and install two (02) 24-port ODFs in ICSS Network Cabinet 13-SC-9703 offshore to facilitate
cable termination at offshore. 
-Do fiber splicing and termination for the two (02) fiber optic cables and provide/install the required
fiber optic patch cords between ICSS Network Cabinet 13-SC-9703 and Telecom Cabinets 13-TEC-
9003 at offshore.
-Do terminate the two (02) power cables at the UPS Distribution board (tag no. 13-DBV-11) and the
new OT cabinet at offshore.
