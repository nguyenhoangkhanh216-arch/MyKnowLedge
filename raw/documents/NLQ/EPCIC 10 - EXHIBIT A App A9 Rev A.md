Definitions

General Pre-commissioning

CONTRACTOR  shall  carry  out  Pre-commissioning  activities  in  accordance  with  COMPANY  Standards  and,  in

Pre-commissioning  covers  the  testing  activities  of  the  PLANT  to  be  performed  onshore  and  offshore  by
CONTRACTOR  prior  to  starting  the  Commissioning  activities.  Pre-commissioning  is  part  of  Construction
organisation and Scope of WORK.

Pre-commissioning activities are summarised hereafter:

i.

ii.

Systematic conformity checks carried out on each part or item of equipment or component, such
as pressure gauges, motors, cables, to visually verify the condition of the equipment, the quality of
installation, the compliance with project drawings and specifications, manufacturers’ instructions,
safety rules, codes, standards and good practice.
Static,  de-energised  tests  of  specific  equipment  to  ensure  the  quality  of  a  number  of  critical
components.  This  cold  testing  work  will  concern  all  disciplines  and  cover  activities  such  as:
calibration  of  instruments,  machinery  alignment,  calibration  of  pressure  safety  valves,  hydro-
testing, line flushing and gross air leak test of piping systems, testing of cable continuity.

For each SUB-SYSTEM, a READY FOR COMMISSIONING certificate (as defined in AGREEMENT) is issued at the
completion of pre-commissioning activities. Commissioning activities shall begin when systems have reached
the status Ready for Commissioning (RFC). There shall be one RFC dossier per SUB-SYSTEM.

The list of documents to be produced during the Pre-commissioning engineering shall follow but shall not be
limited to the requirement of COMPANY Standard SD-NOC-EC-601.

CONTRACTOR  shall  develop  a  specific  procedure  and  associated  matrix  related  to  witnessing  that  shall  be
approved  by  COMPANY.  In  particular,  activities  like  tests  on  pad  eyes/lifting  equipment,  flushing  of  lines,
removal of sensitive equipment during flushing, vessel inspection, PSVs and safety equipment shall be witnessed
at 100%.
