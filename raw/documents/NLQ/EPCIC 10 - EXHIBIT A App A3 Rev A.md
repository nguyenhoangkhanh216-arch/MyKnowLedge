GENERAL

In addition to technical audits which COMPANY have performed or may perform on all aspects of the WORK, at
any time and as deemed necessary, COMPANY policy is to have independent Project Reviews (PRs) of the WORK
carried out at different stages of the WORK.

Project Reviews (PR3 and PR4) Audits are carried-out during the project EPCIC phase which aims at ensuring
that the intent of the design developed in the previous phases is fulfilled, and that the original “fit-for-purpose”
objectives,  in terms of value  creation and risk management, are maintained. This assessment is focused  on
design, project execution, and operations readiness.

The Project Reviews may be split into several partial audits (per WORKSHOP/WORKSITE, etc.) depending mainly
on planning arrangements. Additionally, COMPANY, at its discretion, retains the option to cover elements of the
project reviews internally without CONTRACTOR involvement.

2
