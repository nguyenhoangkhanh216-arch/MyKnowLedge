INTRODUCTION

This Appendix is intended to define the minimum requirements for CONTRACTOR DOCUMENTS to be
prepared and issued for COMPANY information, review or APPROVAL.

The lists in this  Appendix  are not  intended to be exhaustive and any document  required within the
frame of this CONTRACT, necessary for the execution of the WORK, shall be prepared by, or caused to
be prepared by, CONTRACTOR.

CONTRACTOR shall prepare and submit for APPROVAL the detailed Documents List (Master Document
Register)  on  which  he  shall  have  marked  for  each  document  an  appropriate  class  number  agreed
previously by COMPANY and based upon the classification defined as follows:

Class 1: Document for APPROVAL

Class 2: Document for Review

Class 3: Document for Information

CONTRACTOR shall mark each document issued with a stamp for Documentation Class and enter the
appropriate class number.

CONTRACTOR documents shall be issued to COMPANY and simultaneously to other addressees at all
stages of progress and for each revision.

The number, type of support and procedure for submission of documents will be found  in EXHIBIT G,
Coordination Procedure.

CONTRACTOR  is  required  to  identify  on  each  document  issued  the  Document  Class.  Where  the
definition of the Document Class of a document is in doubt, CONTRACTOR shall refer to COMPANY for
agreement.

Documents shall be developed to Approved for Construction (AFC) and as-built (ASB) status.

Periodic  CONTRACTOR  DOCUMENTS  shall  be  issued  to  COMPANY  in  accordance  with  their  stated
periodicity.

distribution of documents in addition to CONTRACTOR and shall be submitted for APPROVAL.
