MONITORING

If the TERM is longer than one (1) year, CONTRACTOR shall complete an ICV PLAN TRACKER annually.

The ICV PLAN TRACKER will be used by CONTRACTOR to monitor spend and ICV SCORE realization during
the TERM and for comparison against the ICV PLAN at the ICV EVALUATION DATE.

If the TERM is one (1) year or less, the submitted ICV PLAN will be used for comparison between the planned
and achieved ICV SCORE at the ICV EVALUATION DATE.

The ICV PLAN assumptions detail the planned activities and commitments by CONTRACTOR to be able to
achieve the planned ICV SCORE. These assumptions will be used by COMPANY for monitoring and assessing
results during the TERM and at the ICV EVALUATION DATE.
