1 GENERAL
1.1 The descriptions given in this EXHIBIT K are the minimum requirements of CONTRACTOR’s organisation,
facilities and resources for the performance of the WORK. Such descriptions shall not be construed as a
limitation of CONTRACTOR’s obligations under the CONTRACT.
1.2 CONTRACTOR acknowledges and agrees it shall provide the organisation, facilities and resources
described in this EXHIBIT K, as well as any additional organisation, facilities and resources necessary to
complete the WORK in accordance with the WORK TIME SCHEDULE, all within CONTRACT prices stated
in EXHIBIT B.
1.3 CONTRACTOR Project Management Team shall consist of a Project Manager, Deputy Project Manager,
managers, team leaders, lead engineers, interface management leader, supervisors and other key
operational PERSONNEL for each phase and element of the WORK, including the management of HSE,
quality, risk and document control.
2 CONTRACTOR ORGANISATION
2.1 CONTRACTOR shall submit to COMPANY an organisation chart for APPROVAL, which shall include the
names and positions with all job descriptions and detailing responsibilities and mobilisation and
demobilisation dates. Resumes of CONTRACTOR PERSONNEL holding positions of responsibility shall be
also provided.
2.2 With respect to the management and the supervision of the execution of the WORK, Key PERSONNEL
shall be submitted to COMPANY for APPROVAL.
2.3 CONTRACTOR’s organisation chart for the performance of the WORK is as shown in Attachment K1 and
consists of the following:
i. Project Management Team.
ii. Engineering and ICSS Integration Organisation.
iii. Procurement Organisation.
iv. LQ Substructure, Topside & Bridge Construction Organisation.
v. Onshore fabrication organisation.
vi. Offshore Hook-up and Commissioning Works Organisation.
vii. Marine and Offshore Operation Organisation.
viii. Pre-commissioning Organisation.
ix. Onshore Commissioning Organisation.
x. Quality Assurance and Quality Organisation.
xi. Interface Management Team.

5 KEY HSE PERSONNEL
5.1 For HSE activities, the nominated key managers, team leaders, lead engineers, supervisors and other key
HSE PERSONNEL (including SUBCONTRACTOR PERSONNEL) shall be submitted for APPROVAL prior to the
commencement of the WORK. COMPANY shall have the right to interview such Key PERSONNEL.

direction, management, supervision and co-ordination for the HSE activities on site, as required by
EXHIBIT L. CONTRACTOR’s team at each WORKSHOP/WORKSITE shall be led by a person who shall be
responsible for all activities at the location and shall report directly within the Project Management Team
organisation.
