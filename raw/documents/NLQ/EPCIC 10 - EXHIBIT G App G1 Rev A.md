1 WEEKLY REPORT STRUCTURE
1.1 Work Package 1 – Management, Project Control and Administration
1.1.1 General Management
This section shall cover all Work Packages and include:
 Summary of the main achievements including list of documents issued during the week and
coordination with SUBCONTRACTORS and third parties.
 Review of the critical points for the WORK.
 Main areas of concern and plan for resolution.
 Resources, actual and forecast by Work Packages and Work Units.
 Review of action list from previous meeting.
 Activities for next reporting period.
 HSE statistics, including, as a minimum:
- Exposure Man-hours, including direct manhours (weekly/monthly, and cumulative),
- Lagging indicators, including Loss Time Incident Frequency (LTIF), TRIR (weekly/
monthly, and cumulative), and
- Leading indicators.
For further detail, refer to EXHIBIT L.

vi. Alert notices received.
vii. Special training and inductions.

ix. Safety actions planned for next period.

For each relevant Work Unit, CONTRACTOR shall include:

1.5 Work Package 5 – Onshore Construction and Testing
For each relevant Work Unit, CONTRACTOR shall include:
i. A narrative on achievements, activities planned for the coming period, critical activities,
safety matters and areas of concern.
ii. Status of materials, equipment and AFC drawings received, actual versus planned.
iii. Progress achieved on WORKSITES and WORKSHOPS.
iv. Clearance status of FAT punch lists.
Typical following detail information should be present in this Onshore Construction Weekly meeting
Report.
a) Weekly and Summary HSE statistics and KPI’s
b) Weekly and Summary Quality statistics and KPI’s including Structural and Piping
NDT statistics and KPI’s, summary and backlog quantities and reject welder’s
identification and reject type for the current reporting week period and rolling 4 weeks
past performance
c) Construction Schedule Critical Path Analysis and Action Plans
d) Planned and Actual Fabrication/Construction Manpower Histograms/Tables by Facility by
Deck by Design/Work Area by Subcontractor by Discipline
e) Planned and Actual Progress and Dates for production and issue of Shop Drawings by
Discipline by Facility by deck by Design/Work Area by Quantity
f) Planned and Actual Progress and Dates for each fabricated commodity by Discipline by
Facility by Deck by Design/Work Area by Quantity
g) Planned and Actual Dates for Pre Inspection Meeting by Discipline prior to commencing
new work activities and scopes
h) Planned and Actual Progress and Dates for fabrication and assembly by Facility by Deck by
Design/Work including setting and stacking of structures, decks and platforms
i) Planned and Actual Dates for ETA, Inspection, Pre Assembly, Assembly and Installation of
Package and Tagged Equipment, including 8 week Lookahead Plan
j) Planned and Actual Dates for Piping System Pre commissioning by Piping System Test Pack
for each phase of Piping System Pre commissioning activities including flange
management, line walk, flushing, pressure test, reinstatement and preservation
k) Planned and Actual Dates for System and Subsystem Pre commissioning completion and
handover
l) Areas of Concern and Action Plans

2 MONTHLY REPORT STRUCTURE
The structure of the monthly report shall be identical to the structure of the weekly report, but
information given shall be on a monthly basis (e.g. progress figures) and shall include the following
additional topics in the Work Package 6 section:
i. Executive Summary.
ii. Risk Management.
iii. Photographs.
2.1 Executive Summary
This section shall include:
i. Review of action list from previous meeting.
ii. Main decisions taken during the monthly reporting period.
iii. Summary of the main achievements, main meetings held, and main milestones reached.
iv. Review of the critical points for the WORK.
v. Main areas of concern and plan for resolution.
vi. Personnel resources.
vii. Activities for next reporting period.
2.2 Risk Management
This section shall include:
i. Identification and mitigation of critical risks.
ii. PSRA (Project Schedule Risk Analysis)
2.3 Photographs
2.3.1 CONTRACTOR shall provide in this section a set of photographs showing main achievements/progress
on PROJECT activities.
2.3.2 Supporting documents attached to Monthly Report are summarised in Section 3.
