1.2 General Provisions
This EXHIBIT C is the WORK TIME SCHEDULE applicable to the CONTRACT; CONTRACTOR shall develop and
update its Detailed Work Time Schedule to incorporate the CONTRACT Key Dates (KD), Milestone Dates (MD)
and COMPLETION DATES (CD) as stated below.
The WORK shall commence, or be deemed to have commenced, upon the EFFECTIVE DATE, and shall continue,
subject to the provisions of Articles 49, 50, and 51 of the AGREEMENT, until issuance of the last FINAL
ACCEPTANCE CERTIFICATE.
In accordance with the provisions of sub-Article 34.1 of the AGREEMENT in the event of failure to achieve the
COMPLETION DATE set out in Clause 4 of this EXHIBIT, COMPANY shall have the right to apply the liquidated
damages stated in Clause 9 of EXHIBIT B.
CONTRACTOR shall, within forty-five (45) days after EFFECTIVE DATE, submit for APPROVAL the Detailed Work
Time Schedule related to the engineering and procurement (Level 3) activities of the WORK.
Within three months after EFFECTIVE DATE, CONTRACTOR shall submit for APPROVAL the Detailed Work Time
Schedule (Level 3) covering the entire CONTRACT. Hook-up, pre-commissioning, and commissioning scopes
planning shall be submitted at Level 2 breakdown in the Detailed Work Time Schedule.
If engineering deliverables are received by COMPANY after the relevant date specified in the Master Document
Register (MDR) Issued For Approval (IFA), COMPANY may, at its sole option, defer said date in which case,
CONTRACTOR shall bear sole responsibility for the consequences thereof.
Unless otherwise specified, the progress of any activity listed herein shall be measured as per CONTRACTOR
Progress and Monitoring Procedure that shall be developed as required in EXHIBIT G.
