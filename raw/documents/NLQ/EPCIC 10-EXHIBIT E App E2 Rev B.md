2  BROWNFIELD INTEGRATION WORK – BH & BJ LOCATION

Brownfield integration work organisation and identification are within CONTRACTOR’s scope as detailed below
in Clause 3.

Follow-up and performance of identified tasks related to  brownfield integration  work  are an integral part  of
CONTRACTOR’s scope of WORK.

Brownfield integration work management is within CONTRACTOR’s scope of WORK and shall be executed from
EFFECTIVE  DATE  until  issuance  of  the  COMPLETION  CERTIFICATE.  Brownfield  integration  work  management
covers  all  CONTRACTOR  activities
including  engineering,  procurement,  fabrication/construction,  pre-
commissioning, transportation, installation, hook-up and commissioning.

For sake of clarity and at the time of the CFT, the two platforms BJ and BH are under EPCIC Execution phase by
OTHER CONTRACTORs.

After EFFECTIVE DATE, one of the two following scenarios may occur:

•
Either CONTRACTOR will have to carry-out the brownfield integration work with OTHER CONTRACTOR,
•  Or CONTRACTOR will have to carry-out the brownfield integration work directly with COMPANY under

the brownfield scope of WORK.

In any case, the scope of WORK shall be executed in compliance with the CONTRACT and brownfield integration
scope defined in this section 3 / Attachment #1.
