OBJECTIVE

The  objective  of  this  document  is  to  ensure  that  all  loss,  damage,  or  injury  likely  to  give  rise  to  an
insurance claim under the COMPANY’s Construction All Risk Policy described in the AGREEMENT is:





Reported promptly and accurately from WORKSITES and WORKSHOPS.

Processed and settled appropriately by COMPANY and CONTRACTOR.

GENERAL

This insurance claim procedure is not intended to vary or supersede in any way existing internal loss
reporting within COMPANY, CONTRACTORS, SUBCONTRACTORS, and VENDORS.

This Insurance Claim Procedure is a set of general guidelines to be followed. In certain cases, depending
on circumstances, there may be other requirements, which will be advised by COMPANY to be more
expedient.

This Insurance Claim Procedure does not address the issue of coverage provided by the Construction
All  Risks  Policy  and  a  reference  to  the  AGREEMENT  is  always  necessary  to  ascertain  the  extent  of
coverage provided.

All  loss  or  damage  concerning  any  item  within  the  insurance  cover  affected  by  COMPANY  shall  be
submitted to Construction All Risk Underwriters through the COMPANY, on behalf of the PARTIES.

Nevertheless,  it  shall  be  CONTRACTOR’s  responsibility  to  extend  full  cooperation  to  COMPANY  to
enable COMPANY to submit COMPANY’s claim against insurers in accordance with this procedure.

may at its sole discretion let CONTRACTOR submit and substantiate its claim directly against insurers
and be paid by them.
