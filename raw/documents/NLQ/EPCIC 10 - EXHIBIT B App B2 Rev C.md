1.1 Selection of OPTION
COMPANY will confirm its selection of the below listed OPTIONS where possible prior to the EFFECTIVE DATE.
Accordingly, any selected OPTION will be deleted from Appendix B2 with the relevant Work Package, Work
Unit and Sub Work Units and the CONTRACT PRICE amended accordingly.
1.2 Preamble
For OPTION listed below, CONTRACTOR is deemed to have:
I. listed in detail the main activities associated (principle of Work Unit);
II. provided the overall impact to the ICV PLAN in accordance with Attachment 17, item 13 of the ITT;
III. included for CONTRACTOR performing all activities as more fully described in EXHIBIT A Work Unit/s
and as specified in the tables below; and
IV. represented to COMPANY, CONTRACTOR’s compliance with the detailed schedule milestones which
are provided in the WORK TIME SCHEDULE (EXHIBIT C).
1.3 Validity of OPTION
Unless otherwise expressly stated the OPTION may be exercised at COMPANY’s sole discretion in
accordance with the CONTRACT and EXHIBIT C.
The OPTION prices within this Appendix B2 shall be fixed and firm in accordance with the AGREEMENT.
