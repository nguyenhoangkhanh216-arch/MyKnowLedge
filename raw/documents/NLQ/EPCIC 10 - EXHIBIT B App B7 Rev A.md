1. GENERAL PREAMBLE
In the event that, as the result of a CHANGE ORDER, a change shall take place in the quantity of the
WORK, based upon a measurement of the WORK as installed, CONTRACTOR shall be compensated by
the application of the unit rates stated hereinafter.
The following general provisions, together with the specific discipline preambles here below shall apply
in respect of all unit rates:
i. The unit rates are fully inclusive of all costs and allowances necessary to complete the
WORK unless specifically excluded within the respective discipline preambles and shall be
deemed to include all costs and allowances for CONTRACTOR - furnished material and
equipment, procurement, receiving, transportation to WORKSITE, storage, preservation,
handling, manoeuvring into position, alignment, fixing, preparation, fabrication and
installation, testing, pre-commissioning and commissioning, and the use of associated
CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENT and consumables.
ii. The unit rates are fully inclusive of the engineering and procurement work associated with
the work items listed including input to the preparation of CONTRACTOR DOCUMENTS
including FINAL DOCUMENTATION.
iii. The unit rates are fully inclusive of the cost of waste material.
iv. In the following schedule of unit rates, the descriptions associated with the respective
items are intended only as brief descriptions sufficient for identification of, and not
exhaustive detailing of, the operations involved in carrying out the WORK, and rates stated
against the respective items shall be deemed to be the fully inclusive rates for carrying
out and completing the WORK described under such items.
v. The rates shall apply to executing the WORK in large or small quantities and difficult or
easy situations and no claims will be considered which arise from the eventual proportions
of the WORK falling into these categories. The unit rates shall apply regardless of the
complexity of the WORK to be carried out.
vi. The quantity of WORK comprising a Change shall be determined based on the
measurement of the work as installed, and no allowance shall be made in the measured
quantities for waste. All measurements shall be taken from latest revisions of Approved
for Construction documents. The adjustment to the CONTRACT PRICE shall be calculated
on the basis of the net change (increase or decrease) in agreed measured quantity of
WORK resulting from the CHANGE ORDER multiplied by the applicable unit rates.
vii. Within the following Schedules:
a. m means linear metres.
b. m2 means square metres.
c. m3 means cubic metres.
d. n° means each.
e. kg means Kilogrammes.
f. t or T = metric tonne.
g. # in connection with piping and valves means refers to ANSI rating (e.g. 300#).
viii. The unit rates shall include for meeting the requirements of the CONTRACT, including the
DESIGN DOSSIER.
ix. The unit rates shall include for all rigging, habitats and shelters required to carry-out the
work.
x. The unit rates shall include for all operations to perform the work. The unit rates include
for all required pre-commissioning and commissioning activities.
