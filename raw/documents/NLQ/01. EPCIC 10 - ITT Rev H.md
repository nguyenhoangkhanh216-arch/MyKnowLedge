3. OVERVIEW OF THE SERVICES
3.1. The Al Shaheen field is Qatar’s largest oilfield. It is a conventional oilfield (approximately 60m water depth) situated
offshore Qatar, approximately 70 km NNE of Ras Laffan. The field overlays the giant pre-Khuff gas field known as
North Field. The field has been developed through several phases and further development phases are envisaged
by COMPANY.
3.2. Considering the planned phases of development by COMPANY through Gallaf and current development under
Ruya, which consists of 12 additional platforms, COMPANY is planning to have a permanent accommodation
facility at B Location with 200 beds capacity.
3.3. The present CFT is for the EPCIC of “New Living Quarter” (NLQ) platform located on B-Location and another like
for like NLQ platform located on A-Location as an OPTION. TENDERER shall submit its TENDER for the Engineering,
Procurement, Construction, Transportation, Installation and Commissioning of the “New Living Quarter” (NLQ)
platform, in order to provide sufficient accommodation capacity at B-location.
3.4. The WORK and components of the PLANT as further described in the EXHIBITS A and E shall be developed based
on the CFT Technical Specifications
