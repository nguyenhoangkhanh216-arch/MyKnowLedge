GENERAL AND PURPOSE

CONTRACTOR guarantees that the WORK shall be designed, constructed, installed and commissioned
in accordance with the requirements set out in the AGREEMENT and EXHIBITS.

CONTRACTOR shall guarantee the WORK and the PLANT as per Article 20 of the AGREEMENT.

In  addition,  as  per  the  AGREEMENT,  performance  must  be  demonstrated  in  accordance  with
requirements  and  criteria  described  in  this  document  as  well  as  remedial  action  in  case  such
performance guarantees are not met.

CONTRACTOR warrants that the CONTRACTOR ITEMS shall meet the performance criteria set out in the
DESIGN  DOSSIER  which  shall  be  complemented  by  datasheets  and  specifications  developed  by
CONTRACTOR during the detailed engineering APPROVED by COMPANY. The values given in the DESIGN
DOSSIER,  datasheets or specifications are the  minimum performance requirements. Acceptability of
individual  system  capacities/performances  shall  reflect  the  needs  or  requirements  to  achieve  the
overall production performance (as indicated in the DESIGN DOSSIER) and/or the utilities to support
the required production performances.

2

PERFORMANCE OF CONTRACTOR ITEMS

CONTRACTOR  warrants  that  the  CONTRACTOR  supplied  equipment  shall  meet  the  performances  set  in  the
corresponding data sheets and SPECIFICATIONS. These performance guarantees may be higher than those given
for the overall design.

CONTRACTOR guarantees that the minimum performance specifications of specific equipment, meet or exceed
the performance specifications defined in DESIGN DOSSIER.

2.1

2.2

The performances guarantees shall be measured by CONTRACTOR and witnessed by COMPANY on SITE.

PERFORMANCE TEST Procedure shall be prepared by  CONTRACTOR  and  submitted  to COMPANY  for
APPROVAL during the detailed engineering phase and shall be in accordance with SUBCONTRACTORS,
VENDORS and SUPPLIERS appropriate procedures. The PERFORMANCE TEST duration is continuous and
uninterrupted for each functionality of each system to be tested as per DESIGN DOSSIER.

The performance guarantees will be demonstrated and satisfied in accordance with the PERFORMANCE TESTS.
These PERFORMANCE TESTS are additional to the Factory Acceptance Tests (FATs), Site Acceptance Tests (SATs),
Pre-Commissioning and Commissioning tests, Operational Tests (OTPs) required in EXHIBITs A, E, G, and H, which
shall be successfully achieved for the same equipment design.

COMPANY shall have the right to use the equipment with insufficient performance until proper remedial WORK
is performed and completed by CONTRACTOR to COMPANY's satisfaction.

PERFORMANCE  TESTS  of  potential  additional  equipment  subject  to  the  performance  guarantees  will  be
specifically identified at an early stage of the WORK and the list of those PERFORMANCE TESTS will be subject to
APPROVAL.

COMPANY. Should the PARTIES fail to reach an agreement, COMPANY shall have the right to determine
such PERFORMANCE TESTS conditions as appropriate to the circumstances.

COMPANY and CONTRACTOR shall agree during the detailed engineering phase on any special sampling
and instrumentation necessary for these PERFORMANCE TESTS.

CONTRACTOR shall ensure that utilities and equipment performance guarantees as per EXHIBIT E demonstrated
and satisfied as part of Commissioning Offshore Operational Test.

4.2.3

Criteria

The minimum requirement shall be respected and satisfied by CONTRACTOR for the utility operating condition
prior to start the PERFORMANCE TEST as defined in the DESIGN DOSSIER.

Any adjustment, optimisation or update in the utility conditions specified in the DESIGN DOSSIER is subject to
COMPANY APPROVAL prior to the PERFORMANCE TEST execution.

4.2.4

Turndown Test

The turndown test shall demonstrate the ability of the installed facilities to achieve the turndown specification
for  a  continuous  period  of  forty-eight  (48)  hours  of  stable  operation,  whilst  meeting  the  project  product
specifications set out within the CONTRACT.

The turndown condition to be defined and agreed with COMPANY prior to starting the test.

The duration of this test can be reduced if requested by COMPANY.

4.2.5

Conditions prior to PERFORMANCE TESTS

Prior  to  initiating  the  PERFORMANCE  TESTS,  COMPANY  and  CONTRACTOR  (together  with  VENDORS)
representatives shall agree that PLANT have been commissioned and is operating in a satisfactory manner, that
sufficient measuring instruments have been installed, calibrated and are operational, and that the probability of
successful completion of the test is high.

Prior to the start of the PERFORMANCE TESTS, the PLANT shall be set at operating conditions as close as possible
to those given by CONTRACTOR. For this purpose, the test run procedure shall be based on a specific design
run/simulation performed on the basis of actual and prevailing conditions.

4.2.6

Conditions during PERFORMANCE TESTS

COMPANY shall perform all sampling according to a sampling protocol agreed jointly between COMPANY and
CONTRACTOR  (together  with  VENDORS).  Unless  otherwise  agreed,  the  first  analysis  will  be  carried  out  at
COMPANY’S  laboratory  and  cost.  A  second  analysis  may,  at  CONTRACTOR’S  option,  be  carried  out  at
CONTRACTOR’S preferred laboratory and cost. All samples will be retained until the test-run analysis is complete.
In case of discrepancies in the results the sample(s) will be sent for analyses to a third-party laboratory chosen
by the COMPANY and agreed with CONTRACTOR. Both COMPANY and CONTRACTOR shall equally share the cost
of this third set of analyses.

Conditions following PERFORMANCE TESTS

TESTS to determine conformance or otherwise with the performance guarantee criteria.
