1 RISE AND FALL MECHANISM FOR MARINE FUEL
1.1 General
1.1.1 Except as expressly provided in this CONTRACT, and subject to 1.1.2 below the Lump Sum Prices shall be
firm throughout the duration of this CONTRACT and shall not be revised for any reasons whatsoever
including but not limited to cost escalation or currency fluctuations, except for fluctuations in the price of
fuel from the price quoted by CONTRACTOR in its proposal for this CONTRACT in which event the affected
Lump Sum Prices and rates for APPROVED MARINE SPREAD shall be adjusted accordingly.
1.1.2 For the avoidance of doubt, fuel volumes exceeding those defined LSM and LSD (by campaign) hereunder
shall not be entitled for any adjustment to the Lump Sum Prices.
1.2 Marine Gas Oil (MGO) procured by CONTRACTOR for MARINE SPREAD consumption
1.2.1 Subject to Section 1.1 the fuel component stated in the Lump Sum Prices shall be entitled for adjustments
(increase/decrease) based on the actual change in the MGO prices and shall be based on the following
adjustment formulae.
Dadj = (Dd x Dt)-LSM
Dadj: The positive or negative adjustment to the Lump Sum Prices in USD due to the change in the MGO
prices.
Dd: The price of MGO per Metric Ton (mT) for “Global Average Bunker Price” published on the website of
“Ship and Bunker” in its website (www.shipandbunker.com) for the Day “d”, where “d” shall mean the actual
day when the bunker is purchased by CONTRACTOR for the vessel as demonstrated through vessel
fuel/bunkering logs maintained in the vessel by Captain. The prices of this website are updated Monday to
Friday, and in case of no update on the actual bunker day, then the previous day’s update will be taken for
the calculation. If “Ship and Bunker” have ceased to publish the rates, both parties shall mutually agree on
the equivalent index for the price for “Dd”.
In the event of carrying out the bunkering inside Qatar, then the above “Global Average Price” shall not
apply and instead, the price of MGO (Diesel) in QR per liter published by WOQOD on its website
(www.woqod.com) for that Month “m’ shall apply for “Dd” after converting the liters into equivalent Metric
Tons. The conversion factor for QR to USD shall be “1 USD = 3.65 QR”.
Db: The price of MGO per Metric Ton for “Global Average Bunker Price” published on the website of “Ship
and Bunker” in its website (www.shipandbunker.com) for the date 30 days prior to the Tendered
Commercial Bid date.

ranging from ED to the RFSU date specified in the CONTRACT.

evidenced by WOQOD invoices. Where “m” shall mean the month ranging from ED to the RFSU date

2 CHANGES IN FUEL PRICE
2.1.1 Subject to Section 1.1:
(a) In the case where the fuel bunker rate (“Dd/Dm”) may have increased above the base rate (“Db”)
and the total cumulative cost of the fuel bunker consumed is greater than (“LSM/LSD”), COMPANY
will reimburse CONTRACTOR the increase of the fuel bunker cost, calculated on the actual rate for
fuel bunker but only up to a maximum of the fuel volume specified in (“LSM/LSD”) in accordance
with the applicable adjustment formulae; or
(b) In the case where the fuel bunker rate may be greater or lower than the base rate of (“Db”) and the
total cumulative cost of the fuel bunker is less than USD (“LSM/LSD”). COMPANY shall raise a
negative Change Order for the difference of the actual fuel bunker cost, calculated on the actual
rate for fuel bunker and actual fuel consumption for the performance of the WORK.
