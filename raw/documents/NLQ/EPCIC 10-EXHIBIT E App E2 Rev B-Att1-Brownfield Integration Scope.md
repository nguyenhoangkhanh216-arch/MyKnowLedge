6.2  ELECTRICAL INTEGRATION (Power Supply)
➢  NLQ shall be powered from BJ 11kV switchboard.
➢  Space for up to two 11kV feeders is provided in BJ Electrical room ETR1 for CONTRACTOR
to provide switchgear panels.
➢  Space for laying of HV cables feeding power to NLQ as well as for some control cables is
considered in BJ CPP ETR as well as on the platform up to interface connection point of NLQ
bridge with BJ platform. Control cables will be typically for switchboards, ECS, Navaid
synchronization etc. CONTRACTOR to decide on lactating their asset among the space for
future equipment.
➢  CONTRACTOR shall provide and install the power supply & control cables from BJ platform
to NLQ connecting to necessary HV/LV transformers installed at Living Quarters transformer
room. 2 (Two) numbers of Multi Cable Transit (MCT) for HV cables from NLQ to BJ electrical
room HV switchgear are provided on floor of BJ electrical room. MCT for control cables on BJ
platform Electrical room wall will be provided.
➢  CONTRACTOR shall provide necessary FO patch panels at NLQ for ECS interface.
➢  CONTRACTOR shall do the Integration of ECS Cyber security existing system.
➢  CONTRACTOR shall do the Integration of Display and History with existing system.
➢  CONTRACTOR shall do all the necessary integration of ECS with the existing Ruya system.
➢  CONTRACTOR shall provide cable trays/ladders from NLQ up to end of BI-BJ bridge.
CONTRACTOR to include connection to BJ platform as part of NLQ Scope (while scope inside
BJ platform remains under scope of BJ platform CONTRACTOR).
