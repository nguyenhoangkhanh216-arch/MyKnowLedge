2.1  HSE Incentive Scheme 1 (HSE IS1): WORKSHOPS/WORKSITES and CONSTRUCTION

EQUIPMENT Standards Improvement

CONTRACTOR shall develop an Improvement plan focusing on WORKSITE working conditions, practices
and standards, that will be based on COMPANY HSE self-audit assessment matrix. Findings from this
self-audit will serve for the CONTRACTOR Improvement Action Plan.

The proposal from CONTRACTOR for HSE improvement plan shall cover as a minimum the following
areas:







Improve Workers safety behaviour and risks awareness,

Improve the WORKSITE work practices,

Ensure the working environment (tools, equipment, hoses, temporary working platforms and
the like) are regularly controlled and safe to use.

CONTRACTOR  shall  review  its  HSE  improvement  plan  with  COMPANY  periodically  during  the
CONTRACT, as agreed with COMPANY.

2.2  HSE Incentive Scheme 2 (HSE IS2): WORKSITE HSE Performance

The WORKSITE HSE Performance measured through indicators listed in EXHIBIT L Table 15.2, reflects
the  proactive  actions  required  from  CONTRACTOR.  It  shall  be  filled  in  and  transmitted  monthly  by
CONTRACTOR to COMPANY for review. A 100% achievement is expected each month, for the complete
duration of the project until issuance of the final COMPLETION CERTIFICATE.

2.3  HSE  Incentive  Scheme  3  (HSE  IS3):  HSE  Behaviour  and  Safety  Cultural  Mindset  Incentive
Program  and  additional  WORKSHOPS/WORKSITES  and  CONSTRUCTION  EQUIPMENTS
improvements

CONTRACTOR shall propose a program for promoting safety leadership and exemplary behaviour at the
WORKSITE through individual and collective recognition and rewards of superior HSSE performance.

In  addition  to  HSE  IS1  WORKSHOPS/WORKSITES  and  CONSTRUCTION  EQUIPMENT  Standards
Improvements resulting from Improvement plan based on a self-audit and Findings; and as part of HSE
IS3,  CONTRACTOR  may  also  propose  additional  solutions  for  WORKSHOPS/WORKSITES  and
CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENT standards improvements for COMPANY APPROVAL.
