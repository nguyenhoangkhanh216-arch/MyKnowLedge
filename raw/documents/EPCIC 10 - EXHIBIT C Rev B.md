ACCEPTANCE CERTIFICATE.

COMPLETION DATE set out in Clause 4 of this EXHIBIT, COMPANY shall have the right to apply the liquidated
damages stated in Clause 9 of EXHIBIT B.

ScheduleWORK TIME SCHEDULE (Level 3) covering the entire CONTRACT. Hook-up, pre-commissioning, and
commissioning scopes planning shall be submitted at Level 2 breakdown in the Detailed Work Time
ScheduleWORK TIME SCHEDULE.
If engineering deliverables are received by COMPANY after the relevant date specified in the Master Document
Register (MDR) Issued For Approval (IFA), COMPANY may, at its sole option, defer said date in which case,
CONTRACTOR shall bear sole responsibility for the consequences thereof.
