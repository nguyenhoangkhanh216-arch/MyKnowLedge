2.1 LUMP SUM PRICES
2.1.1 Lump Sum Basis
The CONTRACT is priced substantially on a Lump Sum compensation basis.
This concept entails that:
i. CONTRACTOR is fully responsible and has accounted for the complete performance of the WORK,
in accordance with the requirements of the CONTRACT.
ii. CONTRACTOR undertakes to provide all means and resources of whatever nature necessary for the
complete performance of the WORK.
Lump Sum Prices comprise the Work Units as identified as “Lump Sum” in Appendix B1. Except as otherwise
stated in sub-Clause 2.2, 2.3 and 2.4 below, it includes all the CONTRACT requirements.
2.1.2 Work Breakdown Structure
Consistent with the WORK Breakdown Structure of EXHIBIT A, the WORK is divided into Work Packages, Work
Units, Sub Work Units in order to provide a Work Breakdown Structure (WBS).
The breakdown of the WBS provided in Appendix B1 shall serve as a reference for the purpose of cost reporting
and progress measurement and under no circumstances whatsoever shall such price division and subdivision be
construed as a qualification to the concept of a fully inclusive overall Lump Sum.
The details of the contents and extent of obligations which are priced within the Work Packages, Work Units,
Sub Work Units are provided within EXHIBIT A and the CONTRACT DOCUMENTS generally. Such descriptions are
intended to be sufficient for identification purposes but do not exhaustively detail the operations involved in
performing the WORK. Whenever WORK is shared between several Work Units, CONTRACTOR shall identify in
which Work Unit, the corresponding Lump Sum has been allocated.
Any Work Unit, Sub Work Unit left un-priced is deemed included in other Work Packages, Work Units, Sub Work
Units and items.
Notwithstanding any omission from the description of any WORK item contained within the CONTRACT
DOCUMENTS, the costs of all activities and operations associated with such WORK item are deemed to be
included with that item and therefore included within CONTRACTOR’s pricing. No Change Order Request will be
accepted on the grounds of insufficient or defective description, or that further operations were required to
complete any WORK item as described, shown, or inferred in the CONTRACT DOCUMENTS.
2.1.3 Downtime
The Lump Sum Price set forth in this EXHIBIT B is inclusive of the cost of any and all downtime resulting from
SITE conditions, transportation conditions, mechanical breakdown or weather conditions resulting in stand-by(s)
of MARINE SPREADS and any other CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENT and PERSONNEL on WORKSITE(S).
Further, CONTRACTOR shall not be entitled to any adjustment to the WORK TIME SCHEDULE for any delays to
the WORK resulting from any such downtime of CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENT including MARINE SPREADS.
