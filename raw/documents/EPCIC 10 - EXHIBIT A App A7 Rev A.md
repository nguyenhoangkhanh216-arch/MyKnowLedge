1 INTRODUCTION
The purpose of this Appendix is to describe COMPANY minimum quantity and quality requirements for facilities
and services to be provided by CONTRACTOR to COMPANY, at the various locations where the WORK will be
performed.
The following descriptions are to indicate COMPANY's minimum requirements and are only intended to be
sufficient to identify the required services and facilities. CONTRACTOR shall allow for the full extent of each item
to achieve the intended purpose and include provisions in its provisional sums for all the associated costs. No
claim shall be entertained on the grounds of defective descriptions or that further details other than those
described are necessary to complete an item described, shown or implied.
The standard of services and facilities shall be "first class" standard, conform to HSE and security requirements
and be representative of the quality used by international oil and gas operators. All equipment and supplies shall
be new.
Unless ‘shared services’ or ‘access’ to services is specified herein, all services, facilities and equipment shall be
provided for the exclusive use of COMPANY on the selected WORK location in CONTRACTOR / SUBCONTRACTOR
/ VENDORS facilities.
2 SERVICES AND FACILITIES
All costs relating to the requirements of this Appendix shall be included in EXHIBIT B, Appendix B1, Work Unit
1.5.
Except if otherwise stated, CONTRACTOR shall be compensated under provisional sums for the services provided
to COMPANY.
Any additional quantities provided by CONTRACTOR at COMPANY request will be compensated based on the
unit prices contained in EXHIBIT B, Appendix B4.
3 STANDARD REQUIREMENTS
3.1 Offices
For the avoidance of doubt the following shall apply to CONTRACTOR’s Engineering Offices, Fabrication Site and
MARINE SPREAD at SITE:
At each place where the WORK will be performed, CONTRACTOR shall put its offices in one location or utilize
existing office in the vicinity. All personnel shall be located on the same floor.
The office area will be designated as a non-smoking area.
CONTRACTOR shall provide free access on a 24 hours/day and 7 days/week basis, to the building in which the
office suite is located for all COMPANY’s personnel and any party nominated by COMPANY.
All offices and the office suite shall be lockable and provided with adequate safety equipment such as fire
extinguishers, hose-reels, smoke detectors, fire alarms.
Type I Office Large
Large single occupancy, fully furnished and serviced offices for COMPANY’s personnel of not less
than eighteen (18) square metres. This office shall include a meeting table for 4 people and a
teleconference facility.
Type II Office
Single occupancy, fully furnished and serviced offices for COMPANY’s PERSONNEL of not less than
twelve (12) square metres.
Type III Office
Double occupancy, fully furnished and serviced offices for COMPANY’s PERSONNEL of not less than
eighteen (18) square metres.

SPREAD and CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENT mobilised by CONTRACTOR.
