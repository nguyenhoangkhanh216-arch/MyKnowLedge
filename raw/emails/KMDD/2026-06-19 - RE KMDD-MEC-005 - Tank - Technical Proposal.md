Hi Nam,

Nhờ Nam phản hồi Bidder DKE 1 số điểm như sau:

“

**1.        ****Position of Calming Baffle, Plate Pack Coalescer and Weir Plate**

Bidder is requested to verify the position and arrangement of the **Calming Baffle, Plate Pack Coalescer** and **Weir Plate.**

PTSC notes that the position of the internal parts show in the HC 10% calculation documents does not match the arrangement shown in the GAD.

Bidder is requested to clarify the discrepancy and submit the revised document/ GAD accordingly, if applicable.

In addition, Bidder clarify the dimension for Coalescer in GAD.

**2.        ****Design and Position of Nozzle N4 – Overboard Outlet**

Bidder is requested to review and verify the design and position of nozzle N4 – Overboard Outlet.

As understand form DKE’s current design, the outlet nozzle is located at a very low position. This may create a risk of oil, sludge, or solid particles being discharged through the outlet, and may affect the ability to achieve the required outlet oil content of 15 mg/L in water.

PTSC recommends the following for nozzle N4:

-The nozzle shall be provide with a dip pipe.

-The height form the dip pipe end to the tank bottom shall be approximately **150 mm**.

-The outlet point of nozzle N4 shall be lower than or at the same elevation as the **Weir Plate**.

Bidder is requested to review this option, calculate and revise the GAD as necessary to ensure the required outlet quality of 15 mg/L oil in water can be achieved.

**3.        ****Maintenance Access for Plate Pack Coalescer.**

Bidder is requested to provide a hatch at the Plate Pack Coalescer location to ensure easy access for inspection, removal, and maintenance during operation.

Bidder is requested to review the above comments and submit the revised documents for PTSC’s review and approval.”
