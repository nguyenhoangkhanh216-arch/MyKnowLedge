**Subject:** KMDD - Physical Scope Split between Greenfield and Brownfield

 

Dear anh Hải,

 

With reference to several recent discussions regarding certain interface packages requiring alignment between the Greenfield and Brownfield scopes, such as **ICSS** and **Telecom** , PTSC would like to clarify the following for common understanding.

 

During the bidding stage, PMC was initially assigned to prepare the proposal for the full Engineering and Procurement scope covering both Greenfield KM & DD Topsides and the related Brownfield scope, in accordance with **the attached demarcation schematic.**

 

However, under the final execution decision, PTSC’s responsibility was limited to the Greenfield KM & DD WHPs Topsides area only. Accordingly, the associated Engineering and Procurement scope and cost for Brownfield were excluded in line with the agreed physical scope split.

 

During project execution, the teams have identified certain areas where, from a technical and interface standpoint, common procurement and coordinated implementation across both Greenfield and Brownfield may be necessary in order to ensure overall compatibility and system integrity. The Telecom package is one such example.

 

For these interface areas, it is therefore necessary for the relevant parties to align on the execution approach, interface boundaries, and the appropriate mechanism for cost allocation / compensation between the respective responsible parties.

 

Please let us know a suitable time for further discussion so that this matter can be clarified and managed properly.
