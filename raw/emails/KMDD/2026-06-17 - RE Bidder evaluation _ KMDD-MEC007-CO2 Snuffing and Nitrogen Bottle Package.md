Hi Ngân,

Em gửi thông tin đến Bidder TTE sau BCM nhé:

“

**1.        ****Control logic for CO2 Snuffing Skids:**

Revise PID following 2 options:

**Option 1:** Remove Solenoid valve at Header, Keep Solenoid Valve at each of cylinder.

**Option 2:** Remove Solenoid valve at each of cylinder, keep Solenoid Valve at Header. Bidder note that leak and loss pressure in operating process.

Bidder is required to submit Datasheet for Solenoid valve to ensure open and close for CO2 cylinder in operating process.

**2\. Process Sizing Calculation:**

\- For each snuffing operation, the inlet pressure of the PCV will gradually decrease. Therefore, we would like to request the calculation file showing the remaining pressure available for the subsequent snuffing operation after each activation, and confirm whether it is sufficient to satisfy the requirement of 1% CO2 of the maximum venting rate. Please note that a minimum of 3 snuffing operations is required as per the datasheet.

\- RO sizing calculation.

**Deadline to Response: 19/6/2026** ”
