Dear Mr. Hải VSP, and Mr. Hưng PVEP-KM,

 

PTSC M&C would like to highlight the current critical status of the SHWT system load basis for KM & DD WHP as per attachment.

 

Based on the latest load review cut-off 15-Jun-2026, the 24VDC load has increased significantly compared with the FEED assumption. The increase is mainly driven by updated / corrected loads from WHCP, Chemical Injection Package, DEG, Telecom / CCTV.

 

And particularly the ICSS load, PTSC M&C would like to draw special attention that based on unofficial information from the working team, the ICSS load is currently estimated at 3,045 W (at DDWHP), which is almost double the FEED assumption of 1,567 W. With the current platform layout and weight constraints, the existing FEED-based SHWT configuration may not be able to accommodate such increase without major impact to solar panel / wind turbine arrangement, equipment sizing, platform layout, and weight. However, PTSC M&C has not yet received official confirmation from VSP for this Package load. 

 

In view of the urgency of SHWT TBE finalization, PTSC M&C respectfully requests VSP and COMPANY to prioritize this issue and include it for direct discussion in this afternoon’s engineering meeting, with the objective to align on the official load basis and required way forward. In addition, as consistently aligned throughout the project execution, changes affecting the SHWT package should be strictly controlled and minimized to avoid significant impacts on platform layout, weight control, and project schedule. Thank you,
