Dear Mr. Chai,

Following internal discussions with our team, PTSC has decided to proceed with the option of installing pneumatic on/off valves at the header inlet and removing the solenoid valves at the CO₂ cylinder outlets.

Please take note of the following technical requirements:

  1. The solenoid valves used to control the pneumatic on/off valves shall be **SIL 3 capable** compliant, in accordance with the Specification requirements.
  2. The Bidder shall ensure and submit the RO Sizing Calculation to demonstrate that the system can satisfy the requirement for three snuffing operations for each skid. The calculation shall also address PTSC’s concerns regarding no liquefaction/solidification at the vent outlet and confirm that the operating temperature is suitable for the applicable piping specification.
  3. PTSC agrees that UL/FM compliance is not required for the complete skids.



Please refer to the sample PID for our option.

4.      Please find the attached documents of Solenoid valve to control Pneumatic Valve for your reference.

If the Bidder can accept this design concept, please provide your confirmation within today so that PTSC can proceed with review and evaluation. The relevant supporting documents may be submitted subsequently by **24 June.**
