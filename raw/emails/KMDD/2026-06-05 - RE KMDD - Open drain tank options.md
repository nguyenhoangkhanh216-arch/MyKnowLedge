Dear Mạnh and team,

 

Regarding the basis for oil in water content at inlet fluid of Open Drain Tank, process have summarized design data from recent goby Vietnamese projects as attached file.

 

In general, the inlet oil in water content is around 1000-2000 ppmv based on assumption with NO clear basis or technical justification.

 

Regarding API 421, there is no specifications for inlet oil in water content. It only specifies performance guidance for 2 types of API gravity-based oil-water separators (conventional type vs parallel-plate type) and recommend whether the separator is operating within design range or not, if not another type of separator shall be used. However, it does not specify a specific maximum oil content.

 

In process point of view, technical justification for 1000 ppmv oil in water content of Open Drain Tank can be as below:

1.       Go-by project specifications

2.       Propose to minimize oil content at Tank inlet by re-routing large oil sources (maintenance drain of Closed Drain Vessel, overflow of Diesel Storage Tank, etc.) directly to Oil compartment instead of Separation Compartment. Other oil sources from equipment drip pans can be considered negligible.

3.       Vendor data for 2 options of oil content: PROS and CONS.
