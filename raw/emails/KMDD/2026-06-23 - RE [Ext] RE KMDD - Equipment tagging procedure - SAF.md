Thanks for CPY’s advice on previous clarification. 

During implementation, there is an additional concern from the team regarding the Safety Equipment tag name format. CTR would like to seek CPY’s advice on the following matter for further action:

1/In the current Equipment Numbering Procedure (Doc. No. KDP-OOO-00-PS-G-0003 – B2), section 2.3 (snapshot above). The last digits is train identifier, as description which represents for Represents the secondary identifier, which is a single A/B/C etc., used to differentiate between identical items in the same service.

For the safety equipment, the following cases have been identified:

  * Lifebuoy with lifeline and light is identical to Lifebuoy with lifeline, light, and smoke signal (RLB).
  * Plumbed type safety shower/eyewash is identical to Portable eyewash station (RSS).
  * 15 lifejackets cabinet is identical to 1 lifejacket on crane cabin (RLJ)



Therefore, CTR would like to propose using the train identifier as follows to distinguish these identical items:

EQUIPMENT |  Identifier |  Example  
---|---|---  
Lifebuoy with lifeline, light, and smoke signal |  A |  RLB-81-0201-A  
Lifebuoy with lifeline and light |  B |  RLB-81-0202-B  
Plumbed type safety shower/eyewash |  A |  RSS-81-0202-A  
Portable eyewash station |  B |  RSS-81-0201-B  
1 lifejacket on crane cabin |  A |  RLJ-81-0201-A  
15 lifejackets cabinet |  B |  RLJ-81-0202-B
