## Summary

NDUM (Nam Du – U Minh) is the WHP and Pipeline EPCI project for the Nam Du-U Minh Field Development, Blocks 46/07 and 51 offshore Vietnam. Jadestone Energy (JSE) is the COMPANY; PTSC M&C is the EPCI Contractor. As of June 2026 the project is in Early Detail Engineering (EDE), focused on the flexible riser package and T&I TR/RFQ.

## Key Points

- **Blocks:** 46/07 and 51, offshore Vietnam
- **COMPANY:** Jadestone Energy (JSE)
- **CONTRACTOR:** PTSC M&C
- **Current phase:** Early Detail Engineering (EDE) — commenced June 2026
- **EDE formal scope (CPY service order):** Detailed engineering for flexible riser package; assess delivery lead times and support schedule concept selection
- **EDE internal scope:** T&I TR/RFQ survey in conjunction with T&I concept assessment
- **Key package:** ND-P1-PKG-PSF-05 Flexible Risers (early procurement)
- **Document prefix:** JSEV00 / JSEV02
