## Summary

LDV-A (Lac Da Vang-A) is a Production Platform development project on Block 15-1/05 in the Cuu Long Basin, operated by Murphy Cuu Long Bac Co. Ltd. (Murphy Oil / MCB). PTSC M&C is the Detailed Engineering Services contractor. As of mid-2026 the project is in the Construction & Commissioning (C&C) phase at PTSC M&C's yard with the offshore T&I campaign less than 1 month away (as of 10-Jun-2026).
