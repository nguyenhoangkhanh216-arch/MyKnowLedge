## Key Technical Facts

- **CA Category:** CAT B
- **Pumps in scope:** EODD Transfer Pumps (P-5110/5120), Diesel Transfer Pumps (P-5710/5720), Service Water Pumps (P-6510/6520)
- **Semi-open impeller:** Acceptable per ASME B73.1 and ISO 5199 (confirmed MEC MOM-005, 04-Jun)

- **Motor spec:** Zone 2, IP56, Ex ec IIC T3
- **Baseplate:** Standard accepted for Non-API pumps; project spec required for closed drain pump
