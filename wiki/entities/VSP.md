## Summary

VSP (Vietsovpetro) is the main EPCI Contractor (CONTRACTOR) for the KMDD project under the contract with PVEP-KM. VSP holds the prime contract and subcontracts topsides engineering and procurement to PTSC M&C, and jacket/pipeline work to NIPI/OCD. VSP also acts as the Engineering Manager at the project level, reviewing and approving all PTSC M&C deliverables. VSP is a Vietnam–Russia joint venture operating primarily out of Vung Tau.
