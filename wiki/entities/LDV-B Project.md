## Summary

LDV-B is the Wellhead Platform component of the Block 15-1/05 Lac Da Vang oil development, operated by Murphy Cuu Long Bac Oil Co. Ltd. (Murphy Oil). It is an unmanned WHP with 8 well slots, no topsides process equipment, and a minimalistic electrical/control philosophy (solar/wind/fusible plugs). LDV-B is planned online 20–24 months after LDV-A First Oil (targeted Q4 2026). PTSC M&C is bidding for the Early Engineering contract (SOW Rev B2, Oct-2025).

**Key design philosophy (unmanned, minimalistic):**
- No topsides process equipment — fully rated process piping only; cold vent
- Hydraulic actuated valves (no pneumatic instrument air system as primary)
- Electrical: solar arrays + wind turbines (principal power); diesel generator (manned periods and restart only)
- ICSS: DC electric hydraulic pumps + instrument air bottle driven pneumatic controls
- Fire detection: Fusible plugs as primary fire detection (instead of conventional point detectors)
- Temporary Refuge: Emergency shelter for max 6 personnel, no beds
- WHCP contains fusible plug circuit controls (derived from LDV-A deluge panel)

**Engineering approach:**
- All LDV-B documents to be generated/updated to the same standard as LDV-A
- Analogue design: LDV-A Detailed Engineering (PTSC M&C) used as basis for all disciplines
- 3D modelling: PDMS; structural: Bentley SACS; process: HYSYS; pipe stress: Caesar II
- Pipeline option: intra-field pipelines included in scope but may be bid separately

**Early Engineering phase objectives:**
1. Engineering sufficient to compile Lumpsum EPCIC ITB (based on LDV-A ITB modified for LDV-B)
2. Definitive ±10% cost estimate with risk/benchmarking
3. Draft EPCIC ITB document
4. Contractor strategy support for COMPANY

**HAZOP/safety approach:**
- LDV-B HAZOP: CONTRACTOR compares LDV-B P&IDs vs FEED P&IDs and HAZOPS significant changes
- Full suite safety analysis deferred to LDV-B EPCIC contractor scope
- HAZID: conducted during Early Engineering
