## Summary

The New Living Quarter (NLQ) Project is an EPCIC 10 contract issued by North Oil Company (NOC) for the Al Shaheen Oil Field, offshore Qatar (CFT ref. 10210491). The project scope is the Engineering, Procurement, Construction, Installation, and Commissioning of a new 200-bed permanent accommodation platform at B-Location, with a like-for-like platform at A-Location as a priced option. PTSC M&C is in the bidding phase for this contract.

## Key Points

- **Client (COMPANY)**: North Oil Company (NOC), designated operator under the Development and Fiscal Agreement for Al Shaheen Oil Field, Qatar — ITT, §1
- **Field location**: Al Shaheen Oil Field, ~60m water depth, ~70 km NNE of Ras Laffan, Qatar — ITT, §3.1
- **Scope**: EPCIC of NLQ platform at B-Location (base scope); optional identical NLQ at A-Location (Option No. 1) — ITT, §3.3
- **Accommodation**: 200 beds capacity; driven by Gallaf and Ruya development programs adding 12 additional platforms — ITT, §3.2
- **Platform options**: (1) Conventional (new-built jacket + piles + topside) or (2) Unconventional (new built or conversion of MOU/Jack-up Barge/SEP) — ITT, §4
- **Contract Effective Date target**: 30 September 2026 / 29 October 2026 — ITT, §4
- **Governing law**: State of Qatar — ITT, §7.1
- **ICV requirement**: In-Country Value (Tawteen initiative, QatarEnergy) is mandatory and part of tender evaluation — ITT, §2.3, §9.8
