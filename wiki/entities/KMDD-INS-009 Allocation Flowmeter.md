## Summary
KMDD-INS-009 is the Allocation and Well Test Flowmeter package for KM-WHP and DD-WHP. Two bidders are in active TBC#4 stage as of 16-Jun-2026: Bao Nghi (McMenon / TÜV SÜD NEL technology) and Nhan Viet (McCrometer V-Cone technology). The key technical unresolved items are V-Cone turndown ratio confirmation and 3rd party wet gas laboratory calibration requirements.

## Key Technical Facts

- **Application:** Allocation measurement and well test for wet gas (offshore, multi-phase)
- **Technology under evaluation:** V-Cone (McCrometer, Bao Nghi's McMenon)
- **Wet gas calibration:** McCrometer advises factory water cal + wet gas correction factor is sufficient; 3rd party wet lab may be required by other suppliers

- **PVT requirement:** SGS confirmed PVT needed at 1st commissioning; DBBV configuration confirmed for PVT tracer injection
