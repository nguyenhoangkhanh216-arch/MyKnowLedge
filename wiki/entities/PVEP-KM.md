## Summary

PVEP-KM (PetroVietnam Exploration Production Corporation — Khanh My) is the Client (COMPANY) for the KMDD project. They hold the development rights to the Khanh My and Dam Doi blocks offshore Vietnam and have issued the EPCI contract to VSP for the KM-WHP and DD-WHP wellhead platforms. Known as "CPY" (COMPANY abbreviation) in project documentation.

## Key Points

- **Full designation:** PVEP-KM — the Khanh My project unit of PetroVietnam Exploration Production Corporation
- **Role in KMDD:** Client (COMPANY) — contract party that issued the EPCI contract; has final approval authority on technical and commercial decisions
- **Project abbreviation:** CPY (used interchangeably with PVEP-KM in emails and MOMs)
- **Contract structure:** PVEP-KM → VSP (main EPCI contractor) → PTSC M&C (Topsides subcontractor)
- **Document review:** 171 documents overdue under PVEP-KM review chain as of 02-Jun-2026; documents submitted via DocPro external portal
