## Summary

KMDD is the KM-WHP (Khanh My Wellhead Platform) and DD-WHP (Dam Doi Wellhead Platform) Detailed Engineering & Procurement project, executed by PTSC M&C as EPCI contractor. The project covers two offshore wellhead platforms connected by a pipeline, with PVEP-KM as the client (COMPANY) and VSP (OCD, NIPI) as subcontractors. As of mid-2026 the project is in the Detail Engineering phase.

## Key Points

- **Platforms:** KM-WHP and DD-WHP — two offshore wellhead platforms
- **Pipeline:** FWS (Fluids from Wells at KM-WHP) flows subsea to DD-WHP, cooling to near seabed temperature en route
- **Contract parties:** PTSC M&C (PMC, EPCI contractor), PVEP-KM (COMPANY/client), VSP OCD (owner's engineer), NIPI (subcontractor)
- **Document numbering:** KDP-KMA-xx (KM-WHP documents), KDP-DDA-xx (DD-WHP documents)
- **FEED Verification obligation:** 30 days after Effective Date to submit IFR; 60 days for final. Missing items = no Change Order rights
- **Phase:** Detail Engineering (as of 2026)
- **Key packages:** MEC-001 (Pressure Vessels), MEC-002 (Pig Launcher), MEC-003 (Closed Drain Pump), MEC-006 (Chemical Injection), MEC-007 (CO2 & N2), MEC-009 (HVAC), INS-002 (PRVs), INS-009 (Allocation & Well Test Flowmeters), PIP-001 (Manual Valves), ELE-007 (E&I Junction Boxes)

## Resolved Engineering Decisions (as of 03-Jun-2026)

- Seawater/Firewater piping: **GRE → CuNi 90/10** (confirmed May-2026; TQR with VSP for approval)
- Fresh water piping: **CS Galvanized → SS 316** (confirmed May-2026)
- Duplex class bolts: **SS B8M → CS A320 + ZnNi + PTFE** (confirmed April-2026)
- SS piping P-T rating: line 250-PG-50014-63410X max design temp reduced 80°C → 65°C (Class 600)
- **HIPPS d/s design pressure: confirmed 133 barg** (both KM-WHP and DD-WHP; SS316 at 900# limit)
- **Methanol pumps upgraded:** KM 507.8 L/h (triplex, 7.5 kW, 30% start-up), DD 560.8 L/h (triplex, 7.5 kW, 50% start-up) — TQ issued KDP-OOO-03-TQ-B-0004
- **Chemical dosing rates confirmed (May-2026):** PPD 500 ppmv (↑ from FEED 300), SI 40 ppmv u/s choke (injection point moved), CI 50 L/d base + 130 L/d optional at DD-WHP — TQs issued
- **PSV-1931A/B removal proposed:** HIPPS dynamic study confirms max d/s pressure 88.2 barg (< 92 barg design); awaiting COMPANY confirmation
- **HIPPS bleed-off line retained:** vs PSV option — PMC recommendation; COMPANY confirmation pending
