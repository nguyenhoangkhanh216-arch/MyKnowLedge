## Summary

KMDD-MEC-006 is the Chemical Injection Package (CI skid + methanol pump skid) for KM-WHP and DD-WHP. CA Category B. Critical package: covers corrosion inhibitor, PPD, scale inhibitor, and methanol injection systems. Five bidders at varying TBC stages as of Jun-2026. BCM#2 sessions confirmed in-line integrated CI+MeOH configuration feasible (POS, DKE). IRCD type changed from remote to manual (04-Jun). New open issue: IRCD fail-safe philosophy conflict — DKE (fail-closed) vs PVDT (fail-last-position), under internal review as of 15-Jun.

## Key Technical Facts

- **CA Category:** CAT B
- **Package scope:** CI skid + methanol pump skid, all associated pumps/tanks/instruments (separate skids)
- **Methanol pump type required:** Triplex 7.5 kW (only PVD Tech compliant at TBC#2 stage); POS simplex 18.5kW exceeds EDG limit
- **Methanol pump capacity:** KM-WHP 507.8 L/h, DD-WHP 560.8 L/h (VSP accepted 08-Jun)
- **IRCD:** Changed from remote (24VDC) to manual type (resolved power constraint 04-Jun)
- **CI+MeOH skid integration:** CPY preferred; BCM#2 confirmed feasible (POS, DKE); currently separate skids
- **Methanol skid width constraint:** 1200mm maximum
- **Chemical dosing rates (VSP-confirmed 08-Jun):** PPD 500 ppmv, SI 40 ppmv u/s choke, CI 80 L/d KM / 130 L/d DD, MeOH 507.8/560 L/h
