## Summary
The North Oil Company (NOC/NOC Qatar) maintains a library of ~610 Company Standards (SD-NOC-*, GM-NOC-*, PL-NOC-*, RL-NOC-*) that apply to all NLQ EPCIC 10 project activities. These standards are mandatory (Level 3 Standard or Level 4 Guideline) and are listed in Exhibit E Appendix E3. They are NTBE (Not To Be Endorsed) but legally binding via the CONTRACT.

## Key Points
- 610 NOC documents in App E3 Rev B: all issued at CFT stage; all marked "CFT" (one exception: GM-NOC-PJC-512 added via Tender Bulletin 5). — Exhibit E App E3 Rev B
- **Key discipline groups:** COR (corrosion protection), ELE (electrical), HVA (HVAC), INS (instrumentation/ICSS), MEC (rotating machinery), PLR (pipelines), PVV (piping/pressure vessels), SAF (safety), STR (structural), TEL (telecom), GEO (geotechnical/geophysical), HIH (industrial hygiene/health), MAR (marine), MNT (maintenance), ENV (environment), FO (field operations). — Exhibit E App E3 Rev B
- NOC Company Standards take precedence over international codes only when explicitly stated in Project Documentation. Most stringent requirement always applies. — Exhibit E, §2
- **Frequently cross-referenced NOC standards in NLQ tender:** SD-NOC-EC-600/601/602/603 (pre-comm/comm), SD-NOC-INS-103 (AVEVA INS E&I Database), SD-NOC-HVA-802 (HVAC deliverables), SD-NOC-PJC-501 (CONTRACTOR Quality Management), SD-NOC-EC-106 (Equipment Tagging), SD-NOC-SAF-009 (HIPPS Design), SD-NOC-PJC-703 (Final Documentation). — Multiple Exhibit A appendices
- Document levels: Level 1 (Policy), Level 2 (Company Process/Rule), Level 3 (Standard — mandatory), Level 4 (Guideline). Most documents in App E3 are Level 3. — Exhibit E App E3 Rev B
