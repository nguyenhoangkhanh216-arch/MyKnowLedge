## Summary
MEC-005 Open Drain Tank is one of the most delayed KMDD procurement packages — TBE 35 days overdue as of 26-Jun-2026. The package involves complex design trade-offs between inlet oil content (10% HC vs 1000 ppmv basis) affecting tank dimensions significantly. Bidders DKE and PPS have passed technical evaluation; advance cost impact summaries sent to Commercial on 26-Jun.

## Key Points
- Status as of 19-Jun-2026: 35d overdue on TBE submission; worst performer in procurement
- 4 bidders: PPS, DKE, AECC, VHI
- TBC#02 Rev.A issued to VHI, deadline 23-Jun-2026
- Design basis options being evaluated (agreed with CPY 09-Jun):
  - Base case: 8 m3/h, 1000 ppmv oil → PPS proposes 1800×1312×1400 mm
  - Option 1: 5 m3/h, 10% HC → PPS: 1930×711×1732 mm
  - Option 2: 3 m3/h, 10% HC → PPS: 1930×711×1237 mm
  - Option 3: max flowrate at 10% HC that keeps base case dimensions → PPS: current CPI plate pack NOT suitable at 10% HC; new dim 3842×1348×1824 mm
- DKE key technical items in TBC:
  1. Calming Baffle/Coalescer/Weir Plate position: inconsistency between HC 10% calculation and GAD
  2. Nozzle N4 (oil outlet): dip pipe required ~150mm from bottom; outlet ≤ Weir Plate elevation
  3. Maintenance hatch at Coalescer position required
- Manufacturer restriction: China/India origin not acceptable for tank fabrication
- Previous basis: FEED used 10% HC; 1000 ppmv proposed by PTSC process team to optimize size; CPY meeting 09-Jun confirmed new 4-case study basis
