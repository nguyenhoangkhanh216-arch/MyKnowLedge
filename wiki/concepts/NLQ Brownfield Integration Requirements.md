## Summary
The NLQ EPCIC 10 project requires CONTRACTOR to manage brownfield integration with existing Al-Shaheen B-Location and A-Location facilities, and specifically with platforms BH and BJ which are under EPCIC execution by OTHER CONTRACTORs at CFT stage. This scope is active from Effective Date through Completion Certificate.

## Key Points
- B-Location and A-Location as-built documentation is available on iView at Effective Date; CONTRACTOR requests native files via the RFI process during execution. — Exhibit E App E2, §1
- Brownfield integration at BH and BJ locations is FULLY within CONTRACTOR's scope: engineering, procurement, fabrication/construction, pre-commissioning, T&I, hook-up, and commissioning of the integration works. — Exhibit E App E2, §2
- At Effective Date, two possible scenarios: (a) CONTRACTOR interfaces with the OTHER CONTRACTOR executing BJ/BH, OR (b) CONTRACTOR works directly with COMPANY under brownfield scope. Scope of work remains identical in either case. — Exhibit E App E2, §2
- Brownfield integration scope details are in Attachment 1 of App E2: "NLQ – Brownfield Integration Scope" (doc CPPR1-MDM5-ASBJA-05-273123-0002_rev.04, updated and integrated within §6.5.5 of the FSS). — Exhibit E App E2, §3
- All brownfield integration work management is CONTRACTOR's responsibility and must comply with the CONTRACT from Effective Date to issuance of the COMPLETION CERTIFICATE. — Exhibit E App E2, §2
