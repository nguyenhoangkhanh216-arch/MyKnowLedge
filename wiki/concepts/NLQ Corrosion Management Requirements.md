## Summary

This page captures all Corrosion Management requirements for the NLQ EPCIC 10 project as defined in Appendix A1 §2.10. CONTRACTOR is responsible for ensuring the 25-year design life is met without integrity risk through material selection, coatings, cathodic protection, insulation, PFP, and chemical injection system design. Painting systems must be high durability CX category for superstructure and IM4 for immersed zones. CONTRACTOR responsibility is not absolved by any COMPANY approval.

## Key Points

### Design Life and Responsibility
- CONTRACTOR shall perform analyses, design, and calculations per international standards to ensure design life of 25 years is met without any integrity risk — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- CONTRACTOR shall ensure material is not corroded or damaged at any stage: production, hydrotesting, preservation, packaging, transportation, storage, end of construction, pre-commissioning, commissioning, until handover — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- Any COMPANY comment and/or approval shall not absolve CONTRACTOR from their responsibility of meeting the 25-year design life — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10

### Material Selection and Corrosion Philosophy
- CONTRACTOR shall develop and define Material Selection and Corrosion Management Philosophy and Report — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- CONTRACTOR shall develop Material Selection Diagrams (MSDs) for all process and utility flow diagrams — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- CONTRACTOR shall establish requirements for corrosion protection and develop specifications for: painting, passive fire proofing coating, insulation, cladding, and cathodic protection — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10

### Painting and Coating
- Painting systems shall be of high durability category and shall comply with ISO/NORSOK/IOGP standards — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- Painting systems shall comply with CX category for Superstructure and IM4 for immersed and splash zone — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- Rigid steel caissons shall be coated with IM4 coating system both internally and externally — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- CONTRACTOR shall update painting specification (external and internal) — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- CONTRACTOR shall develop Specification for Cladding and Weld Overlay if required — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10

### Cathodic Protection
- CONTRACTOR shall develop Cathodic Protection Philosophy and Design Report including: design hypotheses (lifetime, resistivity), list and surface areas of all structures to be protected, calculations — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- Sacrificial anodes on rigid steel caissons shall be suitable for design life of substructure + 1 year — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- CONTRACTOR shall develop Specification for Substructure monitoring and inspection if required — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10

### Insulation and Fire Proofing
- CONTRACTOR shall perform design and calculations associated with insulation and fire proofing per Project Requirements — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- Fire proofing requirements shall be assessed by fire risk and analysis study conducted by third party and approved by CONTRACTOR and COMPANY safety engineers — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- Acoustic insulation requirement shall be based on VENDOR input and CONTRACTOR calculation and assessment — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- CONTRACTOR shall develop Specification for Passive Fire Proofing and its Thickness Calculation per fire and blast analysis — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- CONTRACTOR shall develop Specification for Thermal and Acoustic Insulation — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10

### Chemical Injection and Other Deliverables
- CONTRACTOR shall develop chemical injection systems with injection rate calculations — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- CONTRACTOR shall develop technical specifications for corrosion coupons, holders, and other devices if required — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- CONTRACTOR shall maintain Dead Leg Register — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- Calculations shall be performed using COMPANY Approved Software (ECE.5 for corrosion rate prediction; BEASY for CP modelling) or validated by COMPANY Approved Third Party certification — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
