## Summary

This page captures the COMPANY-approved software suite mandated for the NLQ EPCIC 10 project as defined in Appendix A1 §3 (Table 3-1). CONTRACTOR must use these specific software tools for all engineering disciplines. Use of non-listed software requires COMPANY approval. AVEVA platforms (AVEVA E3D, AVEVA Instrumentation, AVEVA Electrical, AVEVA Diagrams) are the primary digital backbone across disciplines, with discipline-specific analysis tools also mandated.

## Key Points

### Design and Modelling Platform
- P&IDs and D&IDs: AVEVA Diagrams + AutoCAD + I-TOOLS (all disciplines) — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- 3D CAD Design: AVEVA Everything3D (E3D) — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- 3D CAD Review: Review Reality / Navisworks (AVEVA / AUTODESK) — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- 2D CAD: AutoCAD + I-TOOLS (AUTODESK + COMPANY) — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Project Data Base Multi-Disciplines: AVEVA Licenses with Multi-Discipline modules for automatic generation of required engineering documents — Exhibit A App A1, §3

### Instrumentation and Telecommunication
- Instrumentation: Instrucalc (instrument sizing); AVEVA Instrumentation; CadnaA (acoustic simulation / PAGA coverage study) — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Telecommunication: AVEVA Instrumentation — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Metering: AGA3/AGA8 — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Commissioning preparation: ICAPS (COMPANY software) — Exhibit A App A1, §3

### Electrical
- Electrical system studies: ETAP (latest version) for load flow, short-circuit, relay setting, motor starting, transient stability, arc-flash, harmonic analysis — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Electrical design: AVEVA Electrical — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Switching overvoltage studies: PALADIN or EDSA Paladin or EMTP-RV or EMTP-ATP or PSCAD — Exhibit A App A1, §3

### Process and Piping
- Flow assurance and water hammer: PIPENET (also used for firewater hydraulic calculations) — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Heat exchangers: IST, VIB and ACE as relevant (HTRI) — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Vessel design (PD5500/ASME VIII Div 1 & Div 2): PVElite; COMPRESS — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Piping stress analysis: CAESAR 2 (COADE); AutoPIPE — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Offshore pipe laying analysis: OFFPIPE (RCM); ORCAFLEX (Orcina) for dynamic analysis — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Pipeline wall thickness: F101 (DNVGL) — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Free span analysis: Fat-free (DNVGL) — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- On-bottom stability: AGA level 2 (PRCI) — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- On-bottom roughness/crossing analysis: SAGE Profile (Fugro) — Exhibit A App A1, §3

### Structural
- Structural analysis: SACS; STAAD PRO (onshore calculations); USFOS — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Launch, floatation, upending, mating, dynamic analysis: MOSES — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Finite element analysis: Femap, ANSYS, or ABAQUS — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Global buckling and walking: SIMULIA/ABAQUS — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Pile drivability: GRLWEAP — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Soil/foundation calculation: PLAXIS 3D (Bentley) — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Multi-purpose FEA: ANSYS — Exhibit A App A1, §3

### Safety Studies
- Consequence modelling: PHAST — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Fire/explosion simulation: FLACS — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- F&G mapping/coverage: Detect3D — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- HAZID and HAZOP: PHA Pro — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- SIL Assignment: Risk Graph or ExSILentia — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- SIL Verification: GRIF (SI TOTAL) — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- RAM study: MAROS or GRIF — Exhibit A App A1, §3

### Corrosion Management
- Corrosion rate prediction: ECE.5 Electronic Corrosion Engineer — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Cathodic protection modelling: BEASY Corrosion and CP — Exhibit A App A1, §3

### HVAC
- Heat load calculations: HAP Carrier — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Psychrometric calculations: AHH — Exhibit A App A1, §3

### Architectural
- 3D design: AVEVA E3D — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- 2D drawings: AutoCAD 2D (AUTODESK) — Exhibit A App A1, §3

### General and Spare Parts
- Office productivity: Microsoft Office and Adobe Acrobat V7 — Exhibit A App A1, §3
- Spare parts management: E-SPIR 2000 (Shell Global Solutions) — Exhibit A App A1, §3
