## Summary

Exhibit D defines the contractual performance guarantee framework for the NLQ EPCIC 10 project. CONTRACTOR guarantees that the PLANT meets all DESIGN DOSSIER minimum performance criteria, demonstrated through formal Performance Tests witnessed by COMPANY. These tests are in addition to FATs, SATs, pre-commissioning and commissioning tests. All Performance Tests must be completed before the Provisional Acceptance Certificate (PAC) is issued.

## Key Points

### General Framework
- CONTRACTOR guarantees the PLANT design, construction, installation, and commissioning complies with all AGREEMENT and EXHIBIT requirements — Exhibit D, §1
- Minimum performance values are set in the DESIGN DOSSIER, CONTRACTOR-developed datasheets, and COMPANY-approved specifications; system capacities must reflect overall production performance needs — Exhibit D, §1.4
- Performance Tests are additional to FATs, SATs, Pre-commissioning and Commissioning tests, and OTPs required in Exhibits A, E, G, and H — Exhibit D, §2
- PERFORMANCE TEST procedures prepared by CONTRACTOR; APPROVED by COMPANY at least 1 month prior to commissioning start — Exhibit D, §4.1.i
- Standard conditions: 1.01325 bara and 15.56 °C — Exhibit D, §4.2.1
- COMPANY to ensure throughput for tests within 24 weeks after TOM approval — Exhibit D, §4.1.iv
- All Performance Tests must be completed before the PROVISIONAL ACCEPTANCE CERTIFICATE (PAC) — Exhibit D, §4.1.x
- If a system fails a test, the test restarts from the beginning unless COMPANY approves otherwise — Exhibit D, §4.1.vi
- CONTRACTOR issues test results report within 30 days of receiving required analytical data — Exhibit D, §4.2.7

### Plant General Guarantees (§3)
- 25-year design life — Exhibit D, §3.i
- PLANT to be safe, reliable, maintainable, accessible, operable and efficient throughout design life — Exhibit D, §3.ii
- Full environmental respect — Exhibit D, §3.iii
- PLANT to withstand all site environmental conditions for the full 25-year life — Exhibit D, §3.iv
- Corrosion coating protection warranty as per SPECIFICATIONS — Exhibit D, §3.v

### HVAC Performance Guarantee
- HVAC system must prove cooling capacity, humidity, airflow rate, room pressurisation, and temperature per CONTRACTOR DOCUMENTS — Exhibit D, §4.2.2, Table 4-1 Item 4
- Test duration: 1 month total (20 days summer + 10 days winter) — Exhibit D, §4.2.2, Table 4-1 Item 4

### Electrical and UPS Performance Guarantee
- Lighting: lux measurement per specification in normal offshore conditions; 1-month test — Exhibit D, §4.2.2, Table 4-1 Item 2
- UPS and Battery: 100% load test onshore (load bank active and reactive); operation test offshore; 24 h onshore / 1 month offshore — Exhibit D, §4.2.2, Table 4-1 Item 3
- Emergency Diesel Generator (EDG): 12-hour site run test per CONTRACTOR DOCUMENTS — Exhibit D, §4.2.2, Table 4-1 Item 10

### ICSS and Telecom Performance Guarantee
- ICSS: 99.9% availability with no major fault for 1 month; major fault defined as failure of controller CPU/PSU/I/O cards, HIS station/network card, or Ethernet switch — Exhibit D, §4.2.2, Table 4-1 Item 6
- Telecom (PA/GA, WIFI, other devices): 99.99% availability with no major fault for 1 month — Exhibit D, §4.2.2, Table 4-1 Item 7
- Fire & Gas Detectors: no faulty detection by any detector installed; 1-month test — Exhibit D, §4.2.2, Table 4-1 Item 5

### Mechanical and Utility Performance Guarantees
- Air Compressor & Dryer: pressure and dew point per datasheet; noise, vibration, bearing temperatures, oil consumption per spec; 1-week test — Exhibit D, §4.2.2, Table 4-1 Item 1
- Crane: 133% load tested onshore; re-tested at 100% SWL after transit; certificate required — Exhibit D, §4.2.2, Table 4-1 Item 8
- NAVAIDS: per CONTRACTOR DOCUMENTS; 1-week test — Exhibit D, §4.2.2, Table 4-1 Item 9
- Potable Water System: per CONTRACTOR DOCUMENTS; 1-week test — Exhibit D, §4.2.2, Table 4-1 Item 11
- Sewage Treatment: per CONTRACTOR DOCUMENTS; 1-week test — Exhibit D, §4.2.2, Table 4-1 Item 12
- Fire Water Pump: per CONTRACTOR DOCUMENTS; 12-hour site run test — Exhibit D, §4.2.2, Table 4-1 Item 13

### Turndown Test
- Installed facilities must sustain turndown specification for 48 continuous hours of stable operation while meeting product specifications — Exhibit D, §4.2.4
- Turndown condition agreed with COMPANY prior to test start; duration may be reduced at COMPANY's request — Exhibit D, §4.2.4
