## Summary

This page captures the procurement and supply requirements for the NLQ EPCIC 10 contract as defined in Work Package 4 (WP4) of Exhibit A. CONTRACTOR holds full responsibility for procurement engineering, vendor management, TBE, inspection, expediting, and supply of all CONTRACTOR ITEMS. All procurement activities are governed by Exhibit G (Coordination Procedure) and Exhibit J (Approved Vendor List).

## Key Points

- CONTRACTOR shall prepare all MTO, requisitions, and technical documents for procurement of all CONTRACTOR ITEMS and bulk materials — Exhibit A, §10.1
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare Material Requisitions for each equipment item with all required documents: datasheets, project specifications, applicable codes — Exhibit A, §9.5 (WU 3.4)
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare complete RFQ packages with requisitions, specifications, drawings, VDRL, certification and inspection requirements, spare parts, and VENDOR assistance requirements — Exhibit A, §9.5
- CONTRACTOR shall evaluate VENDOR/SUBCONTRACTOR offers, prepare Technical Bid Evaluation (TBE) including all deviations, and submit to COMPANY for review and approval — Exhibit A, §9.5
- TBE must include all VENDOR deviations; recommended VENDORS/SUBCONTRACTORS must be COMPANY-approved — Exhibit A, §9.5
- CONTRACTOR shall obtain APPROVAL for any amendments to the Approved Vendor List in Exhibit J — Exhibit A, §9.5
- CONTRACTOR shall perform individual KOM with each VENDOR with COMPANY participation — Exhibit A, §9.5
- CONTRACTOR shall review VENDOR recommended spares and prepare final recommended list for pre-commissioning, commissioning, start-up, operational, and capital spare parts — Exhibit A, §9.5
- CONTRACTOR shall supply all CONTRACTOR ITEMS including equipment, bulk materials, consumables, and special tools for pre-commissioning and commissioning — Exhibit A, §10.2–§10.5
- CONTRACTOR shall supply all capital spare parts per COMPANY requirements — Exhibit A, §10.6
- CONTRACTOR shall maintain a Material Tracking System (MTS) capable of locating any piece of equipment from requisitioning through to incorporation into the PLANT — Exhibit A, §7.2.2.3
- CONTRACTOR shall carry out expediting and inspection of VENDOR and SUBCONTRACTOR equipment and materials — Exhibit A, §7.2.1
- CONTRACTOR shall develop performance test procedures for all major equipment and packages; witness and report on tests — Exhibit A, §9.5
