## Summary

This page captures all Piping, Layout, and 3D Modelling requirements for the NLQ EPCIC 10 project as defined in Appendix A1 §2.6. CONTRACTOR must perform all piping design activities from design basis through to MTO; execute piping stress analysis using COMPANY-approved software; manage GRE piping coordination with COMPANY-approved vendor; and develop the complete brownfield integration scope in all engineering documents including the 3D model (AVEVA E3D).

## Key Points

### Design Basis and Piping Classes
- CONTRACTOR shall perform all piping design activities per the DESIGN DOSSIER including review and development of all work elements — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- CONTRACTOR shall develop project-specific piping classes in addition to piping classes in DESIGN DOSSIER; inform COMPANY of any additional piping classes in advance with design calculations — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- CONTRACTOR shall maximise use of standardised pipe supports; special pipe supports shall be designed for loading/dimension criteria exceeding standard catalogue — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6

### Plot Plans and Layouts
- CONTRACTOR shall update Complex layouts for B Location to include new BI Platform and Bridge BI-BJ connected to host BJ platform — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- CONTRACTOR shall develop equipment layouts and plot plans (including elevation drawings) from the DESIGN DOSSIER, taking into account: safety/equipment zoning, actual footprint, piping routing, material handling access, future facilities provisions — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6

### Piping Drawings and Lists
- Piping General Arrangement Drawings and isometrics shall be extracted from approved 3D model — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- Isometric drawings incorporating all details for fabrication, inspection, and testing shall be prepared by CONTRACTOR — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- All piping/manual valves and specialty items shall be identified with unique tag numbers — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare and maintain: Hook-up/Tie-in list/schedule, Stress Critical Line List, Valve List with COMPANY validated TAG Numbers, List for piping specialty items with COMPANY TAG Numbers, Fluids List, Dead Leg Register — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6

### Material Take-Off (MTO)
- CONTRACTOR shall develop MTO/Bill of Material (BOM) for all bulk piping items (pipe, fittings, flanges, gasket, bolting, insulation, etc.) — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- MTOs shall include appropriate design and construction allowances; sortable by size, piping class, system, and product codes — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- MTOs shall include weight data for all piping, valves, fittings, and pipe supports; and all tubing (including chemical injection tubing) — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6

### Piping Stress Analysis
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare a detailed piping stress design basis for all stress-critical lines — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- Computer-assisted piping flexibility analysis shall be performed using COMPANY-approved software only (CAESAR 2 or AutoPIPE) — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- CONTRACTOR shall perform assessment for Flow Induced Vibration (FIV) and any advance screening/FEA/CFD analysis required — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- Piping stress analysis shall consider all loads: deck support flexure, thermal, pressure, slugging, liquid hammering, piping induced vibrations, ocean transport conditions — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- GRE piping stress and surge analysis shall be performed only by COMPANY's APPROVED GRE VENDOR; CONTRACTOR shall coordinate with GRE vendor for design, supply and stress analysis — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6

### Pulsation Study
- CONTRACTOR shall perform a pulsation study on suction, recycle and discharge lines of positive displacement pulsating machines and exhaust system of EDGs — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- Pulsation study shall include: sizing of anti-pulsation/silencers, fatigue life assessment, identification of mechanical and acoustic natural frequencies, margin between natural frequencies and excitation frequency — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6

### Brownfield Integration
- CONTRACTOR shall identify and integrate the new BI Platform, B-Location host platform, and all associated integration requirements into all design/engineering documents including 3D model — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- All brownfield integration scope shall be performed per requirements specified in Exhibit E, Appendix E2 — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
