## Summary

This page captures the NLQ EPCIC 10 training obligations (Appendix A8). CONTRACTOR must prepare, obtain APPROVAL for, and deliver a comprehensive Training Program for COMPANY operations personnel. All 12 mandatory training sessions are held OFFSHORE at B-Location with VENDOR specialist attendance. Training must be completed before Transfer of Operation and Maintenance (TOM).

## Key Points

### Scope and Key Duties
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare a Training Program for COMPANY APPROVAL and train all COMPANY personnel based on the approved program — Exhibit A App A8, §2
- Training covers all disciplines: instruments/controls, electrical, telecom, HVAC, galley appliances, mechanical — Exhibit A App A8, §2
- Emphasis on START-UP, shutdown, and troubleshooting procedures (least understandable from manuals alone) — Exhibit A App A8, §2
- Training begins during detailed engineering phase and must be completed before Commissioning and before TOM — Exhibit A App A8, §2

### Training Plan Requirements
- Detailed technical training plan submitted for COMPANY review and APPROVAL no later than 6 months post contract award — Exhibit A App A8, §2(i)
- Training schedule fully integrated with CONTRACTOR's overall delivery schedule — Exhibit A App A8, §2(iii)
- All training modules (syllabus, schedules, materials, competency assessment) agreed in advance with COMPANY; materials available no less than 3 months prior to each course — Exhibit A App A8, §2(ii)
- Classroom components: equipment description; normal startup/shutdown/operation; routine and specialist maintenance; troubleshooting per discipline — Exhibit A App A8, §2(iii)
- Demonstration/simulation components: VENDOR workshop familiarisation (assembly/disassembly, operation); Yard/Site familiarisation on facility — Exhibit A App A8, §2(iii)
- Training plan must include: duration; locations; course content; expected learning objectives; formal competencies and evidence of learning — Exhibit A App A8, §2(iii)
- Practical phase required for operator familiarity with FACILITIES before start-up — Exhibit A App A8, §2(iv)
- All training delivered in English by competent personnel; training materials in English; format must be APPROVED and editable — Exhibit A App A8, §2(v)
- CONTRACTOR must appoint a Training Manager/Lead; provide documentation (attendees, objectives, content, outcomes) within one calendar week of each session — Exhibit A App A8, §2(vi)
- CONTRACTOR shall involve qualified VENDOR Specialists for completion of the training plan — Exhibit A App A8, §2(vii)
- Training Program shall integrate trained COMPANY personnel into CONTRACTOR Commissioning team for FACILITIES familiarisation — Exhibit A App A8, §2(viii)
- Training matrices for each personnel category (management, section heads, supervisors, operators, maintenance) — Exhibit A App A8, §2(x)
- COMPANY offshore personnel work on 4-week rotation; CONTRACTOR shall arrange sufficient sessions for all personnel — Exhibit A App A8, §2(xii)
- Training plan schedule submitted for APPROVAL 30 days before commencement — Exhibit A App A8, §2(xi)
- Upon completion: CONTRACTOR shall prepare a Training Close-out Report covering training activities, attendance records, and competency evaluations — Exhibit A App A8, §2

### Minimum Training Schedule (Table 3-1)
- ICSS (Safety Logic, C&E, Interfaces & Cybersecurity, F&G): 2 sessions × ½ day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance required — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Emergency Diesel Generator (EDG): 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance required — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Potable Water Maker: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance required — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Sea Water System (lift pump): 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance required — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Fire Water Pump Package: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance required — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Instrument Air Compressor Package: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance required — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Crane: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance required — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- HVAC system: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance required — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Galley Appliances: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance required — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- ECS Training, Protection Relays in Switchgear & Power Management System: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance required — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- AC UPS: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance required — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Telecoms (PAGA, Wifi): 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance required — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Maximum 10 trainees per session; VENDORs not based in Middle East give training during commissioning period — Exhibit A App A8, §3, Notes 1 & 2
