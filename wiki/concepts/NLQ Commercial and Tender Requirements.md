## Summary

This page captures the commercial and tender requirements for the NLQ EPCIC 10 Call for Tender, issued by North Oil Company (CFT ref. 10210491). It covers the full tender timeline, submission format and packaging rules, bid bond requirements, qualification and exception templates, and evaluation criteria as specified in the ITT (ST-ITTS-2023 Ver.01 Rev.GH).

## Key Points

### Tender Timeline
- CFT launch: 26 June 2025; TENDERER must confirm or decline participation within 7 working days via e-SOURCING TOOL (SMART GEP) and submit signed Confidentiality Undertaking (Attachment 1) — ITT, §4, §5
- TENDERER confirms selected technical proposal (Conventional or Unconventional): 15 August 2025 — ITT, §4
- Bid Bond submission (original): 31 August 2025 — ITT, §4
- MDR (Master Document Register) submission deadline: on or before 15 August 2025 — ITT, §8.2.3.1
- Contractual exceptions submission (Attachment 6 via SmartGEP): 27 February 2026 — ITT, §4
- Technical Closing Date (TECHNICAL/CONTRACTUAL/HSE): 30 April 2026 (wave 1) / 20 May 2026 (wave 2) — ITT, §4
- Post-TBC meetings: 17 May 2026 and 21 June 2026 — ITT, §4
- TENDERER's updated Technical Bid: 31 May 2026 / 15 July 2026 — ITT, §4
- Commercial Closing Date: 30 June 2026 (wave 1) / 12 August 2026 (wave 2) — ITT, §4
- Contract Effective Date target: 30 September 2026 / 29 October 2026 — ITT, §4
- Clarification requests must reach COMPANY at least 14 calendar days before CLOSING DATE using form in Attachment 2 — ITT, §6.4.3

### Submission Format and Structure
- Tender submitted via e-SOURCING TOOL in two separate sections: (1) TECHNICAL/CONTRACTUAL/HSE and (2) COMMERCIAL; mixing prices into the technical section causes disqualification — ITT, §8.1.5, §8.2
- All tender documents, attachments, and notes shall be in English language — ITT, §8.1.3
- TECHNICAL/CONTRACTUAL/HSE submission: three packages — Package A (Contractual), Package B (Technical), Package C (HSE) — ITT, §8.2.1
- Package A (Contractual Tender) must include: cover letter on letterhead, Form of Tender Letter (Att. 3A), TENDERER details (Att. 4 + Att. 14 Due Diligence), Association of Companies form (Att. 5 if Consortium), insurances confirmation (Att. 21), bank guarantee certificate (Att. 20), Parent Company Guarantee letter (if >50% subsidiary), contractual exceptions (Att. 6), Bid Bond (Att. 15), and audited financial statements (last 3 full years, IFRS) — ITT, §8.2.2
- Package B (Technical Tender) must include 15 sections: Key Engineering Deliverables, PEP, Management/Project Control, Engineering, Procurement, Subcontracting, Onshore Construction, T&I, SITE Works, Pre-commissioning/Commissioning (×2 sections), Schedule, Technical Exceptions, Alternative Proposals, Unpriced Exhibit B + ICV Plan — ITT, §8.2.3
- Package C (HSE Tender) must include: cover letter, acknowledgement of Exhibit L, HSE questionnaire (Att. 12), exceptions (Att. 8), pandemic management plan, GHG emission optimization — ITT, §8.2.4
- Commercial Tender must include: Form of Tender Letter (Att. 3B), Exhibit B (Schedule of Prices, all in USD, inclusive of all taxes and overheads), Appendices B1–B11 completed, ICV Plan — ITT, §8.3
- Tender validity period: 180 calendar days from COMMERCIAL CLOSING DATE; rates/prices remain unchanged and binding throughout — ITT, Att. 3A

### Bid Bond
- Original USD 500,000 Bid Bond required; no tender accepted without it — ITT, §8.2.2, §9
- Bid Bond form must strictly follow Attachment 15 — ITT, §9
- Bank must be: international bank with S&P rating AA- or better (or Moody's Aa3 or better), OR local Qatari bank under URDG 758 — ITT, §8.2.2, §6 (Bank Guarantees)
- Bid Bond valid from submission date through TENDER VALIDITY PERIOD; auto-extended upon award until bank/parent guarantee received — ITT, §9
- Original Bid Bond transmitted by registered post (signed for) or hand delivery with transmittal sheet; tracking number sent via e-SOURCING TOOL — ITT, §9

### Key Engineering Deliverables (KED) Compensation
- COMPANY compensates unsuccessful tenderers who properly complete KEDs with a fixed lump sum of USD 800,000 — ITT, §7.3.1
- To qualify for compensation: deliverables must reach AFD (Approved for Design) status, all COMPANY comments addressed via CRS, and documentation must allow COMPANY to understand the technical proposal clearly — ITT, §7.3.1(b)
- Three revisions required: IFR (rev0) → COMPANY review cycle 1 → IFA (rev1) → COMPANY review cycle 2 → AFD (rev2, final) — ITT, §8.2.3.1
- Compensation forfeited if TENDERER: declines, withdraws, fails to deliver Bid Bond on time, fails to submit complete TENDER by closing dates, becomes insolvent, or breaches laws/ethics — ITT, §7.3.1(a–g)
- Payment within 30 days from end of month of receipt of undisputed invoice — ITT, §7.3.3

### Qualifications and Exceptions
- Contractual exceptions: use Attachment 6 (Excel format); must include alternative wording, reasons, and added value; no price in the technical package — ITT, §8.2.2(8), §8.2.1
- Technical exceptions: use Attachment 7 (Excel format); must include alternative wording and reasons; commercial impact in Commercial Tender only; no exceptions authorized after TECHNICAL CLOSING DATE — ITT, §8.2.3.13
- HSE exceptions: use Attachment 8 (Excel format); same format rules — ITT, §8.2.4
- General remarks or imprecise wording in exceptions will not be considered — ITT, §8.2.2(8)
- COMPANY reserves right to reject tenders with significant or fundamental qualifications — ITT, §8.2.3.13
- If no exceptions listed, TENDER DOCUMENTS deemed accepted in full — ITT, §8.2.2(8)

### Evaluation Criteria
- Tenders evaluated in order: (1) TECHNICAL/CONTRACTUAL/HSE qualification, then (2) COMMERCIAL evaluation — ITT, §9.4
- Fundamentally non-compliant, incomplete or inappropriate tenders shall be rejected — ITT, §9.2
- ICV (In-Country Value) is part of the evaluation to determine the awarded tenderer — ITT, §9.8
- COMPANY may request clarifications, unit rate breakdown, ICV Plan breakdown, and quantification of financial impact of exceptions — ITT, §9.5, §9.6
- Preference given to unconditional offers and tenders with minimal exceptions — ITT, §7.2.6

### Consortium and Subcontracting
- If submitting as Consortium: complete Attachment 5; all partners sign tender jointly and severally; nominate a sole agent — ITT, §8.2.2(4)
- Subcontractors named in Exhibit J are pre-approved; new subcontractors require COMPANY prequalification with full supporting documentation — ITT, Att. 11

### Qatar Registration
- If no Qatari Commercial Registration: proof of application within 10 calendar days of award notification; certificate within 30 calendar days of award notification — ITT, §6.4.2
- TENDERER must comply with Qatar Law No. 19/2005 for Regulation of the Practice of Engineering Professions — ITT, §6.4.2
