## Summary

This page captures all Process Engineering requirements for the NLQ EPCIC 10 project as defined in Appendix A1 §2.3. CONTRACTOR must validate, model, verify, and modify the DESIGN DOSSIER process design basis; develop all PFDs, P&IDs, equipment sizing calculations, and line sizing calculations; produce all process and utility philosophies; and maintain all interface details per the contracting strategy.

## Key Points

### Design Basis and PFDs/P&IDs
- CONTRACTOR shall validate, model, verify and modify as required the DESIGN DOSSIER of the PLANT — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- CONTRACTOR shall maintain and update Project Design Basis to include: Fluid Codification List, design capacity bases and assumptions, brownfield integration scope between BI and BJ/BH platforms — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- CONTRACTOR shall develop PFDs/UFDs and P&IDs for BI Platform and BI-BJ Bridge including all VENDOR package interfaces and future unit operations — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- CONTRACTOR shall develop Vendor package P&IDs and perform HAZOP on those packages — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- CONTRACTOR shall detail all VENDOR package, equipment and control interfaces on P&IDs; reproduce/integrate VENDOR package P&IDs on CONTRACTOR P&IDs — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- CONTRACTOR shall detail accurate low-point drain/high-point vent details required for all piping and layout design — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- All instrument/equipment/line numbering shall be per NOC Standard and generated by NNG tool; CONTRACTOR is responsible for tag checking at first EPC phase revision — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3

### Equipment and Line Sizing
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Equipment Sizing Calculations; detailed calculation notes shall be provided — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare Equipment Process Data Sheets (PDS) for all process and utility equipment on PFDs/UFDs including equipment on packages — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Line Sizing Calculations with detailed Line Sizing Calculation Report; line list shall identify all lines subject to support condition and critical lines needing stress analysis — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- FIV (Flow Induced Vibration) considerations shall be part of line sizing criteria — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- Support stiffness shall be derived and flagged as a requirement within Line Sizing Calculation Report, Line List, and P&IDs — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare Instrument Process Data Sheets (IPDS) with all VENDOR data and instrument sizing calculation notes — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3

### Safety and Relief
- CONTRACTOR shall develop Overpressure Protection Philosophy capturing all SIL Verification outcomes and HP/LP Interface Register — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Relief System Calculations defining all relief loads; Acoustic Induced Vibration (AIV) study shall be done per DESIGN DOSSIER — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3

### Philosophies and Procedures
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare: Utility Balances (including all chemicals), Process and Utility Design Philosophy, Process Control and Operating Philosophy, Isolation and Tie-in Philosophy, Shutdown Philosophy, Relief and Blowdown Philosophy, Start-up Philosophy — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare ESD Logic Diagrams for PLANT and existing platforms including brownfield integration; ESD narrative shall complement ESD logic diagrams — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- CONTRACTOR shall develop Cause and Effect Diagrams (C&E), Process and Utility Systems Description by system number, and Locked Open/Locked Closed Valve Register — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- CONTRACTOR shall develop Start-up Procedure and provide inputs to the Operating Manual — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare Line List with COMPANY validated line numbers and Tie-in and Interface Schedules — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
