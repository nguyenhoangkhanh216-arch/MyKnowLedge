## Summary
The NLQ EPCIC 10 Design Dossier (Exhibit E) defines the complete set of technical governing documents and their hierarchy. Understanding this hierarchy is critical for identifying which document takes precedence when conflicts arise during detailed engineering and bid preparation.

## Key Points
- **Document precedence order (highest to lowest):** (1) Qatar state law → (2) Project Documentation & Particular Specifications → (3) International standards/codes → (4) NOC Company Standards (SD-NOC) → (5) Other recognised codes → (6) Industry practice. When conflict exists, CONTRACTOR always applies the most stringent requirement at no extra cost. — Exhibit E, §2
- **Design Dossier components:** (a) CFT Technical Specifications (FSS + Safety Concepts — TBE by CONTRACTOR), (b) Al-Shaheen existing facilities as-built documentation (NTBE, available on iView), (c) Additional technical documents to rely upon (geotechnical, HAZAN, ENVID — NTBE), (d) 610 NOC Company Standards (NTBE). — Exhibit E, §§3–6
- **TBE (To Be Endorsed) documents** — CONTRACTOR endorses these as free from errors/omissions/ambiguities per Article 13.4 of the AGREEMENT. 4 documents in App E1: NLQ FSS (NLQ01-MPEN-ASBII-00-390001_rev00), NLQ Safety Concept (NLQ01-MPEN-ASBII-00-390002_rev01), NLQ A-Location FSS (NLQ01-MPEN-ASAGI-00-390001_rev00), A-Location Safety Concept (ALS-AY-CPDPA-SAF-0001_rev02) — plus key AFD deliverables from EARLY WORK and CFT Clarifications/Exceptions. — Exhibit E App E1, §1
- **NTBE (Not To Be Endorsed)** — App E2 (brownfield as-builts), App E3 (NOC Standards), App E4 (additional rely-upon docs). CONTRACTOR is still bound by these; they simply cannot be endorsed. — Exhibit E, §3
- Prior to EPC award, CFT Technical Specifications are updated to include key engineering deliverables developed by CONTRACTOR in AFD status during the EARLY WORK phase. — Exhibit E, §4.1
- Any ambiguity not resolved by the precedence order must be formally raised with COMPANY per sub-Article 6.4 of the AGREEMENT (clarification request). — Exhibit E, §2.3
- **App E1 — Technical Documents:** Lists the 4 TBE FSS/Safety Concept documents plus CFT Clarification and Exception registers to be attached prior to EPC award. — Exhibit E App E1, §§1–3
- **App E2 — Brownfield Integration:** B-Location and A-Location as-built documentation available on iView at Effective Date; CONTRACTOR requests native files via RFI. Brownfield at BH/BJ locations fully within CONTRACTOR's scope. — Exhibit E App E2, §§1–3
- **App E4 — Additional Technical Documents to Rely Upon:** Covers B-Location and A-Location field layout charts, geodetic/bathymetry data, geophysical/geotechnical reports, ENVID, HAZAN, and Safety Concept documents — all NTBE. — Exhibit E App E4, §§1–2
- **Exhibit A Rev C §4.11** — CONTRACTOR designs per FSS and Safety Concept (international standards); all other contract activities comply with EXHIBIT E Appendix E3 (NOC Company Standards). — Exhibit A Rev C, §4.11
