## Summary

This page captures all Mechanical Engineering requirements for the NLQ EPCIC 10 project as defined in Appendix A1 §2.4. CONTRACTOR must perform all mechanical analysis, design, and calculations; maintain the Master Equipment List in AVEVA format; develop all equipment specifications and datasheets; and manage material handling philosophy. Noise and vibration studies, criticality assessment, and capital spare parts list are also mandatory deliverables.

## Key Points

### Mechanical Design Basis and Equipment List
- CONTRACTOR shall perform mechanical engineering (analysis, design, and calculations) including delivery of datasheets and detailed specifications of equipment, mechanical handling and maintenance philosophies — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- CONTRACTOR shall develop a Mechanical Basis of Design forming the design basis for all mechanical equipment and packages on BI Platform and BI-BJ Bridge — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- CONTRACTOR shall maintain and update the Master Equipment List from the DESIGN DOSSIER covering all tagged equipment/packages (Mechanical, E&I, HVAC, Safety, etc.) — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- Master Equipment List shall be maintained as an Excel spreadsheet sortable by equipment type, tag number, and location; updated bi-weekly or monthly — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- CONTRACTOR shall provide Master Equipment List in COMPANY-approved AVEVA format — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4

### Equipment Specifications and Datasheets
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare PROJECT-specific mechanical equipment specifications and all package specifications in line with engineering progression based on International codes and COMPANY general specifications — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare all necessary calculation notes and datasheets (including drivers) for all tagged equipment; separate datasheets for each item and each platform — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- Mechanical datasheets shall include all design, material, inspection, testing requirements, and necessary process data for the design of equipment — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- CONTRACTOR shall calculate sizing of driver rating (electrical motors for pumps, air compressors, etc.) and confirm power requirement adequacy based on finalised vendor data — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- CONTRACTOR shall estimate weight and footprint for each package and equipment for structural design; confirm based on finalised vendor data — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4

### Material Handling Philosophy
- CONTRACTOR shall perform engineering and design to determine material handling requirements for all items requiring access/removal/handling/replacement including maintenance and laydown areas — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- Material handling simulations shall be developed in E3D and presented to COMPANY for review and approval — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- Feasibility study for air-lifted equipment shall be submitted to COMPANY for review and approval if found applicable — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- CONTRACTOR shall develop: Platform Crane Selection Study/Crane Load Plan, Material Handling Philosophy, Material Handling Study/Report, Mechanical Handling Plan, List of Mechanical Handling Equipment, Datasheets/Specifications/MR/TBE for cranes and handling equipment — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4

### Other Mechanical Requirements
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Noise and Vibration Study for rotating equipment — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Criticality Assessment Workshop and prepare Criticality Study Report — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare Capital Spare Parts List — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- CONTRACTOR shall study transportation, load-out, and fastening from VENDOR to WORKSITE and from WORKSITE to SITE — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- CONTRACTOR shall design equipment supporting systems, erection/mounting methods — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- CONTRACTOR shall update and maintain general specification for site environment conditions, utility and units of measurement — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- CONTRACTOR shall perform material selection for all mechanical equipment — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
