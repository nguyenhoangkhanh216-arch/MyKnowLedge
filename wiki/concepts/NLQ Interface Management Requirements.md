## Summary

This page captures the NLQ EPCIC 10 interface management obligations (Appendix A10). CONTRACTOR's scope includes interface identification, organisation, and management from Effective Date through Completion Certificate. While COMPANY retains overall responsibility, CONTRACTOR must designate an Interface Leader, conduct weekly coordination meetings, and use a COMPANY-provided IT tool for all interface tracking and communications.

## Key Points

### Scope and Principles
- Interface management scope covers all CONTRACTOR activities: engineering, procurement, fabrication, pre-commissioning, transportation, installation, hook-up, and commissioning — Exhibit A App A10, §1
- Interface organisation, identification, and follow-up of tasks related to interfaces are integral to CONTRACTOR's scope of WORK — Exhibit A App A10, §1
- COMPANY shall always remain responsible for overall management of all interfaces — Exhibit A App A10, §2

### CONTRACTOR Obligations
- CONTRACTOR shall ensure early identification of interfaces in accordance with scope and battery limits — Exhibit A App A10, §2
- CONTRACTOR shall channel all communications with other contractors and third parties THROUGH COMPANY only — Exhibit A App A10, §2
- CONTRACTOR shall collaborate with all parties to ensure prompt, reliable, and traceable information exchange — Exhibit A App A10, §2
- CONTRACTOR shall formally advise COMPANY of any interface problems that arise during the project — Exhibit A App A10, §3

### Interface Management Process
- CONTRACTOR shall implement procedures and submit for COMPANY APPROVAL: Interface Plan, Interface Schedule, Interface Coordination Procedures, physical interface drawings and tables — Exhibit A App A10, §3
- Interface kick-off meeting shall be organised within the first month after contract award — Exhibit A App A10, §3
- Weekly Technical Interface Coordination meetings shall be held throughout the project — Exhibit A App A10, §3
- CONTRACTOR shall designate a focal point (Interface Leader) for all interface matters with clearly defined roles and responsibilities — Exhibit A App A10, §3
- CONTRACTOR shall provide a follow-up and reporting tool; critical interface items shall be reported in the project monthly report — Exhibit A App A10, §3
- COMPANY will provide an interface management IT tool which CONTRACTOR must use — Exhibit A App A10, §3
