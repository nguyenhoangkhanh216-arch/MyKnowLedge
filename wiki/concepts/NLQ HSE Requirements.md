## Summary

This page captures the Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) submission requirements for the NLQ EPCIC 10 tender, as specified in ITT Package C, Attachment 8, and Attachment 12. TENDERER must demonstrate a mature HSE management system through a structured questionnaire covering leadership, risk management, operational controls, emergency preparedness, and subcontractor management. HSE performance directly affects the evaluation score.

## Key Points

### Package C — HSE Tender Structure
- HSE Tender submitted as a separate Package C within the TECHNICAL/CONTRACTUAL/HSE section; no prices allowed — ITT, §8.2.4
- Package C must contain 6 sections: (1) Cover Letter on TENDERER letterhead signed by authorised representative, (2) Acknowledgement of understanding of Exhibit L of Draft Contract, (3) HSE questionnaire (Attachment 12) with supporting evidence, (4) HSE exceptions on Attachment 8 form, (5) HSE Pandemic Management Plan (if applicable), (6) GHG emission optimization initiatives — ITT, §8.2.4
- HSE exceptions must be submitted using Attachment 8 (Excel format) only; no exceptions authorized after TENDER CLOSING DATE — ITT, §8.2.4

### HSE Qualifications Template (Attachment 8)
- HSE exceptions submitted only in Excel format per Attachment 8 template (ITT-Att8_HSE_exceptions.xlsx) — ITT, Att. 8
- Each exception must include: alternative wording, reasons for the exception, any added value — ITT, §8.2.4
- If no exceptions listed, HSE requirements of TENDER DOCUMENTS deemed accepted in full — ITT, §8.2.4

### HSE Questionnaire Requirements (Attachment 12)
TENDERER must demonstrate and evidence compliance with the following HSE categories:

**1. Leadership and Management Commitment**
- HSE Policy, Alcohol and Drugs Policy, Safe Driving Policy, Stop Work Authority Policy, Code of Conduct all required — ITT, Att. 12, §1.1
- HSE performance data for last 5 years required: fatalities vs manhours, LTIF, MTC, RWDC, FAC, TRIR ratios, near miss reports, anomaly/stop cards, and incident gravity records — ITT, Att. 12, §1.2
- ISO 45001 certification (or equivalent formal documented HSE management system) required — ITT, Att. 12, §1.5
- ISO 14001 (or equivalent) environmental certification required — ITT, Att. 12, §1.6
- Formal process for HSE organizational roles, responsibilities, authorities from senior managers down to worksite supervision required — ITT, Att. 12, §1.8

**2. Compliance with Laws and Regulations**
- Demonstrated process for compliance with applicable national and international laws, industry standards, and maintenance of legal registers — ITT, Att. 12, §2.1
- Valid permits, certifications and authorizations for performing the WORK (including equipment, vehicles, vessels) — ITT, Att. 12, §2.2

**3. Risk Management**
- Formal process to systematically identify, assess, evaluate, and mitigate all hazards (health, safety, security, environment, community) — ITT, Att. 12, §3.1
- Risk management process must include reviewing technical data and local conditions from HSE Exhibit — ITT, Att. 12, §3.2
- HAZID workshops required per HSE Exhibit requirements — ITT, Att. 12, §3.3
- HSE Risk Register maintained throughout contract lifecycle; assignment of risk management accountabilities — ITT, Att. 12, §3.4
- Job Safety Analysis (JSA) process per HSE exhibit requirements — ITT, Att. 12, §3.5
- Management of Change (MoC) process covering: working procedures, organization, personnel (especially safety-critical positions), engineering, equipment/safety systems, materials — ITT, Att. 12, §3.7

**4. Operational Controls**
- Security management process with access/egress controls and PERSONNEL on Site (POS) register — ITT, Att. 12, §4.1
- Permit to Work procedure covering all aspects of WORK including PTW Audit Program — ITT, Att. 12, §4.2
- Medical coverage commitment for all PERSONNEL and Health Management Plan — ITT, Att. 12, §4.4, §4.5
- MEDEVAC Plan and Contagious Disease Response Plan — ITT, Att. 12, §4.8
- PPE management process — ITT, Att. 12, §4.9
- Heat Stress Management Plan (particularly relevant for summer offshore Qatar operations) — ITT, Att. 12, §4.13
- Transportation Management process — ITT, Att. 12, §4.14
- Lifting activities process, Work at Height process — ITT, Att. 12, §4.15, §4.16
- Chemicals Management Plan including management of radiation sources — ITT, Att. 12, §4.20
- Waste Management Plan — ITT, Att. 12, §4.21
- Oil Spill Contingency Plan — ITT, Att. 12, §4.22
- GHG/environmental performance monitoring (energy consumption, natural resources, GHG emissions) — ITT, Att. 12, §4.23

**5. Subcontractor HSE Management**
- Process for HSE prequalification, evaluation, and selection of subcontractors — ITT, Att. 12, §5.1
- Subcontracting limited to one level — ITT, Att. 12, §5.3
- HSE audit and inspection schedule for subcontractors, including example HSE bridging document — ITT, Att. 12, §5.4, §5.5

**6. Incident Free Program**
- Stop Work Authority Program empowering all PERSONNEL to stop unsafe work — ITT, Att. 12, §6.1
- HSE Recognition and Reward program for duration of WORK — ITT, Att. 12, §6.2

**7. Competencies and Training**
- Formal HSE Training Matrix for each job/function aligned with COMPANY requirements; includes competency levels, validity, referential standards, prerequisites, refresher requirements — ITT, Att. 12, §7.1
- HSE Training Compliance register with up-to-date certifications and records — ITT, Att. 12, §7.2
- Newcomer onboarding process including short-service employees — ITT, Att. 12, §7.3
- All PERSONNEL must comply with COMPANY English language requirements (written and spoken) — ITT, Att. 12, §7.4

**8. Emergency Preparedness**
- Emergency Response Plan(s) for CONTRACTOR worksites covering all applicable scenarios — ITT, Att. 12, §8.1
- COMPANY Emergency Response Plans communicated to all TENDERER PERSONNEL — ITT, Att. 12, §8.2
- Medical provisions at CONTRACTOR locations for medical emergency — ITT, Att. 12, §8.3

**9. Learning from Events**
- Observation Reporting Program for hazardous/positive situations — ITT, Att. 12, §9.1
- Incident reporting and investigation process — ITT, Att. 12, §9.2

**10. Monitoring, Reporting, and Audits**
- HSE performance monitoring covering leading and lagging indicators — ITT, Att. 12, §10.1
- Internal HSE Audit process and HSE Audit Program covering all activities — ITT, Att. 12, §11.1, §11.2
- HSE Inspection Program including Equipment Inspection and Maintenance register — ITT, Att. 12, §11.3

**12. Security**
- Nominated Security Officer, Security Risk Assessment for TENDERER locations, Security Management System, Security Plan — ITT, Att. 12, §12.1–§12.4

### HSE Pandemic Management Plan
- If applicable (as determined by COMPANY), TENDERER shall provide a Pandemic Management Plan addressing safety measures and quarantine considerations under State of Qatar requirements — ITT, §8.2.4, Section 5

### GHG Emissions
- TENDERER to complete GHG Emission Questionnaire (Attachment 18) and describe any initiatives to optimize GHG emissions during SERVICES, with estimated savings — ITT, §8.2.4, Section 6
