## Summary

This page captures the NLQ EPCIC 10 Regulatory Compliance obligations (Appendix A4). CONTRACTOR bears full responsibility for identifying, obtaining, and complying with all applicable Qatari laws, permits, and environmental regulations for the Al Shaheen field project. The cornerstone deliverable is the Regulatory Compliance Plan (RCP), a Class 1 document submitted for COMPANY APPROVAL.

## Key Points

### Objectives (§2)
- No non-compliance notices or fines from Qatari authorities or COMPANY during the entire project lifecycle — Exhibit A App A4, §2
- No environmental incidents attributable to CONTRACTOR activities — Exhibit A App A4, §2
- CONTRACTOR must not wait for COMPANY instruction; proactive identification of all regulatory requirements is CONTRACTOR's obligation — Exhibit A App A4, §2

### CONTRACTOR Obligations (§3)
- Identify all applicable Qatari laws, decrees, regulations, and permits relevant to the project scope — Exhibit A App A4, §3
- Identify all environmental monitoring requirements imposed by Qatari legislation and COMPANY environmental standards — Exhibit A App A4, §3
- Identify all required training obligations for regulatory compliance — Exhibit A App A4, §3
- Obtain all permits, licences, and authorisations required from Qatari authorities before commencing work — Exhibit A App A4, §3
- Submit and obtain COMPANY APPROVAL for the Regulatory Compliance Plan (RCP) — Exhibit A App A4, §3
- Monitor ongoing compliance throughout the project and report monthly to COMPANY — Exhibit A App A4, §3
- Prepare and submit monthly regulatory compliance reports to COMPANY — Exhibit A App A4, §3
- Register all non-compliances (actual or potential) in the compliance register; report immediately to COMPANY — Exhibit A App A4, §3
- Inform COMPANY immediately of any new regulatory requirement identified during project execution — Exhibit A App A4, §3
- Ensure SUBCONTRACTORS comply with all regulatory requirements; CONTRACTOR retains full responsibility — Exhibit A App A4, §3
- Store all evidence of compliance (certificates, permits, licences) in a retrievable manner for COMPANY audit — Exhibit A App A4, §3

### Regulatory Compliance Plan (RCP) Content (§4)
- Section 1: List and reference of all applicable permits and authorisations with obtaining status — Exhibit A App A4, §4
- Section 2: List of all applicable Qatari laws and regulations, identifying applicable clauses — Exhibit A App A4, §4
- Section 3: Summary of COMPANY HSE, Technical and Quality Standards applicable to regulatory compliance — Exhibit A App A4, §4
- Section 4: Compliance monitoring programme (schedule, frequency, responsible party, reporting format) — Exhibit A App A4, §4
- Section 5: Compliance assurance and performance reporting to COMPANY — Exhibit A App A4, §4
- Section 6: Environmental monitoring requirements (parameters, frequency, measurement locations) — Exhibit A App A4, §4
- Section 7: Training requirements for regulatory compliance (who, what, when, how) — Exhibit A App A4, §4
- Section 8: Compliance assessment programme (how CONTRACTOR assesses its own compliance) — Exhibit A App A4, §4
- Section 9: Roles and responsibilities of CONTRACTOR personnel for regulatory compliance — Exhibit A App A4, §4
