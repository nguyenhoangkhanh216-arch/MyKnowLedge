## Summary

This page captures all Safety Engineering requirements for the NLQ EPCIC 10 project as specified in Appendix A1 §2.2. CONTRACTOR must review the DESIGN DOSSIER Safety Concept, perform all safety studies (HAZID, HAZOP, SIL, QRA, EERA, FRA, etc.), integrate findings into detailed design, maintain an HSE Actions Tracking Register, and deliver all safety documentation to COMPANY-approved standards. All safety consultancies must be COMPANY-approved.

## Key Points

### Safety Concept and Documentation
- CONTRACTOR shall review, further develop, and update the Safety Concept provided in the DESIGN DOSSIER and ensure all requirements are incorporated in Detailed Engineering — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall develop an Engineering Safety Plan to ensure key safety reviews, safety studies, calculations, deliverables, and audits are reflected in the overall project schedule — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall update and consolidate DESIGN DOSSIER Safety Documents and provide additional documents as necessary — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall provide tracking methods (HSE Actions Tracking Register) auditable to ensure all actions are correctly implemented and closed-out — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall incorporate CTC permit requirements and safety distances defined in Hazan Analysis into the detailed design — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2

### Safety Studies (Minimum Required List)
- CONTRACTOR shall conduct HAZID Review at early stage of Detailed Engineering (must precede HAZOP and SIL); HAZID shall cover processing facilities, pipeline/gathering systems, construction, commissioning, SIMOPS, occupational risk — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall conduct HAZOP Review Report with HAZOP Terms of Reference — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall conduct SIL Assignment Review Report with SIL Assignment Terms of Reference (if applicable); any SIF not achieving SIL target shall be re-evaluated and re-verified — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Fire Risk Analysis (FRA) using PHAST software for consequence modelling per DESIGN DOSSIER and SD-NOC-SAF-001; FRA shall include smoke compartmentation study — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Dropped Object Study Report based on lift register — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Ship Collision Study Report — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform EERA for normal operation AND HUC/SIMOPS; EERA shall reflect connection of NLQ to existing BJ platform — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Detailed Risk Assessment (DRA) per SD-NOC-SAF-001 including sensitivity analysis, MAE/MAH register, Bow-ties, SECE identification, RRW, ALARP demonstration and CBA — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform QRA (Quantitative Risk Assessment) — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform CFD Smoke Dispersion Study — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Natural Ventilation Study — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Indoor and Outdoor Noise Study Report — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Vibration Study Report — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Emission and Discharges Estimates (Vent Dispersion Study) including local vents, EDG and Fire Water Pump exhaust dispersion — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Flame and Gas Detector Coverage Study (using Detect3D) — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall identify Safety and Environmental Critical Elements (SECE) and develop associated Performance Standards — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Transportation Risk Assessment — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Aviation Studies required to obtain Helicopter certification agency approval — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2

### Fire and Gas / Firewater Engineering
- CONTRACTOR shall develop Fire and Gas Detection Philosophy including Fire Areas, F&G Detection Logic Diagram, and F&G Detection Cause and Effect Matrix — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall assess need for passive fire protection per COMPANY Project specifications; FEA structural response analysis shall be carried out on critical targets of the living quarter — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Firewater Hydraulic Analysis (steady and transient state) using PIPENET model; PIPENET analysis from CFT phase shall be updated in Detailed Engineering — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall complete detailed engineering of brownfield work associated with firewater integration of BI platform on B Location — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform automatic flooding extinguishing system calculation note and detailed specifications for technical rooms, enclosures, and battery room — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall coordinate with 3D model engineering team to ensure fire and gas detection coverage, safety and firefighting equipment, and firewater network coverage are properly considered in the 3D model — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2

### Hazardous Area and Environmental
- CONTRACTOR shall perform full hazardous area classification considering possible gas release sources and potential ignition locations on new and existing host platforms — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall develop Security Concept including findings from Security Risk Assessment, passive security, IT security, and business continuity — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform all environmental studies: Chemical Management Plan, Environmental Risk Register, Waste Management Plan, Lighting Study, Underwater Noise Monitoring Plan, GHG Emission Report, Construction Environmental Management Plan — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
