## Summary

This page captures the pre-commissioning and commissioning requirements for the NLQ EPCIC 10 contract, drawn from Work Package 8 (Commissioning), Work Units 3.7 and 3.8 (Engineering), and Work Package 10 (Assistance to Start-up). CONTRACTOR is responsible for full OPERCOM methodology compliance, mandatory ICAPS software use, and all commissioning engineering from onshore through offshore phases.

## Key Points

- CONTRACTOR shall issue the pre-commissioning dossier with all procedures at least 3 months before pre-commissioning operations start — Exhibit A, §9.8
- CONTRACTOR shall organise OPERCOM training sessions for pre-commissioning teams using an APPROVED SUBCONTRACTOR — Exhibit A, §9.8
- Pre-commissioning engineering shall comply with SD-NOC-EC-600, SD-NOC-EC-601, and SD-NOC-EC-602 (OPERCOM methodology) — Exhibit A, §9.8
- CONTRACTOR shall define pre-commissioning database in ICAPS software for each discipline and populate it — Exhibit A, §9.8
- Piping Pressure Test Packs (flushing, pressure test, drying, reinstatement, preservation) shall be submitted to COMPANY for review and APPROVAL — Exhibit A, §9.8
- CONTRACTOR shall complete piping system pre-commissioning to maximum extent possible prior to module lifting and integration — Exhibit A, §9.8
- All commissioning procedures shall be ready 3 months prior to start of Commissioning activities — Exhibit A, §9.9
- Commissioning dossier and operational procedures for each sub-system shall be ready 3 months before commissioning starts on that sub-system — Exhibit A, §9.9
- Commissioning engineering shall comply with SD-NOC-EC-600, SD-NOC-EC-601, and SD-NOC-EC-603 (OPERCOM methodology) — Exhibit A, §9.9
- CONTRACTOR shall define the commissioning database in ICAPS software; ICAPS is mandatory for progress measurement — Exhibit A, §9.9
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare start-up sequence, commissioning planning, manpower plan, mobilisation plan, management and operational procedures, and operational test procedures — Exhibit A, §9.9
- CONTRACTOR shall perform all commissioning engineering and prepare all documents for brownfield integration scope per Appendix E2 — Exhibit A, §9.9
- Work Package 8 covers: onshore commissioning (WU8.1), offshore commissioning (WU8.2), transfer of O&M dossier (WU8.3), Pre-start-up Safety Review / PSSR (WU8.4) — Exhibit A, §14
- CONTRACTOR shall deliver Transfer of Operation and Maintenance (TOM) dossier to COMPANY — Exhibit A, §14.5
- CONTRACTOR shall perform Pre-Start-up Safety Review (PSSR) — Exhibit A, §14.6
- Commissioning organisation structured per OPERCOM methodology with segregation between Construction/Pre-commissioning teams and Commissioning teams — Exhibit A App A1, §2.1 / ITT, §8.2.3.10
