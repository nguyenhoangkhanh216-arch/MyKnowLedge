## Summary

This page captures the NLQ EPCIC 10 Project Review requirements (Appendix A3). Two mandatory independent COMPANY project reviews (PR3 and PR4) are conducted during the project lifecycle: PR3 at 60% engineering completion and PR4 at 80% construction completion. Both are COMPANY-led audit events; CONTRACTOR must support fully and bear the cost in the Lump Sum.

## Key Points

### PR3 — Engineering Review at 60% Engineering (§2)
- PR3 is conducted when engineering is 60% complete — Exhibit A App A3, §2
- PR3 covers the following topics: Design Basis, Project Execution, Materials and Equipment Procurement, Interface Management, Documents and Information Management, Regulatory, Safety (HAZID/HAZOP/SIL Status), Environmental, and Lessons Learned — Exhibit A App A3, §2
- PR3 specifically examines procurement status: ITB launching, bid evaluation, vendor qualification, critical vendors — Exhibit A App A3, §2
- PR3 reviews project execution status: schedule performance, cost performance, risk register, change management — Exhibit A App A3, §2

### PR4 — Construction Review at 80% Construction (§3)
- PR4 is conducted when construction is 80% complete — Exhibit A App A3, §3
- PR4 covers: Project Execution status, Procurement and Expediting, Fabrication and Construction, Mobilisation Planning, Installation and Hook-up Planning, Pre-commissioning Planning, Commissioning Planning, and Operational Readiness Planning — Exhibit A App A3, §3
- PR4 scope includes review of CONTRACTOR's plans for all phases from construction completion through to TOM — Exhibit A App A3, §3
- PR4 examines commissioning planning readiness: OPERCOM/ICAPS status, organisation, staffing, equipment readiness — Exhibit A App A3, §3

### CONTRACTOR Support Obligations (§4)
- CONTRACTOR shall make available 15 persons for 10 working days for each project review; cost included in the LUMP SUM — Exhibit A App A3, §4
- CONTRACTOR shall submit all required documents listed in the appropriate document register prior to the Project Review — Exhibit A App A3, §4
- CONTRACTOR shall host COMPANY and COMPANY-appointed reviewers; provide office space, IT connectivity, and administrative support — Exhibit A App A3, §4
- CONTRACTOR must resolve all action items arising from each project review within the agreed timescales — Exhibit A App A3, §4
