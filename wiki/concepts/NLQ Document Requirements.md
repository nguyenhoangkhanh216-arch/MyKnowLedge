## Summary

This page captures the NLQ EPCIC 10 Minimum Contractor Document Requirement Schedule (Appendix A2). All CONTRACTOR documents are classified Class 1 (Approval), Class 2 (Review), or Class 3 (Information). The document list spans general project management, all engineering disciplines, procurement, construction, QA/QC, marine, transportation, installation, pre-commissioning, commissioning, and offshore hook-up.

## Key Points

### Document Classification System
- Class 1 = Document for APPROVAL; Class 2 = Document for Review; Class 3 = Document for Information — Exhibit A App A2, §1
- CONTRACTOR shall stamp each document with its Documentation Class and enter the class number — Exhibit A App A2, §1
- Master Document Register (MDR) shall be established, prepared, and submitted for APPROVAL by CONTRACTOR; documents shall progress to AFC and As-Built status — Exhibit A App A2, §1
- SUBCONTRACTOR documents shall be submitted by CONTRACTOR to COMPANY with CONTRACTOR comments incorporated — Exhibit A App A2, §1

### Key Class 1 Documents by Discipline
- General: PMP, PEP, Regulatory Compliance Plan, Interface Management Plan, EEP, Risk Management Plan, Pre-Commissioning/Commissioning Plan, MDR, Levels 0-3 Schedules — Exhibit A App A2, §3.1
- Safety: Safety Concept, HSE Dossier, SIMOPS Dossier, Safety Reviews (HAZID, HAZOP, SIL, LOPA), Fire and Gas Philosophy, HAZID/HAZOP Reports and Close-Outs, PFP specifications — Exhibit A App A2, §3.3
- Process: PFDs, P&IDs, Process Basis of Design, Relief Calculation Report, Operating/Shutdown Philosophy — Exhibit A App A2, §3.3
- Structural: Structural/Geotechnical Basis of Design, Third-Party Certification, all installation analyses, Weighing final dimensional report — Exhibit A App A2, §3.3
- Piping: 3D CAD Model Execution Plan, Piping Material Classes, Piping Design Basis, Piping Stress Design Basis; first 10 isometrics shall be Class 1 — Exhibit A App A2, §3.3
- Electrical: Black start philosophy, Arc Flash studies, UPS/Battery sizing, EDG sizing, Electrical room/Battery room layout, Lighting calculations — Exhibit A App A2, §3.3
- Instrumentation: Control and Safety System Philosophies, ESD Cause and Effect Diagrams, F&G Cause and Effect Matrix — Exhibit A App A2, §3.3
- Telecom: Telecom Philosophy, Architecture, all system specifications, telecom studies (PAGA, CCTV, Radio, Fibre Optic Link Budget) — Exhibit A App A2, §3.3
- HVAC: HVAC Basis of Design, HVAC Philosophy, HVAC Basic Heat Load Calculation, D&ID and P&IDs — Exhibit A App A2, §3.3
- Corrosion: All coating/CP/PFP specifications, Material Selection Report, PFP Thickness Calculations — Exhibit A App A2, §3.3
- Procurement: Manufacturing QA/QC Plans, ITPs, TBEs, Capital Spare Parts list — Exhibit A App A2, §3.5
- Construction: Detailed Construction Plan, Construction Method Statement, Welding procedures, NDT procedures, Work permit system, Hook-up procedures — Exhibit A App A2, §3.6
- Pre-commissioning/Commissioning: Engineering Schedules and Milestones, Inhibit/Override Procedure, Punch List Management Procedure, ICAPS Procedures, OPERCOM Forms, Hydro Testing Procedure, Operational Test Procedures — Exhibit A App A2, §3.11
- Hook-up: SIMOPS Coordination Procedure, SIMOPS Work Sequence, Tie-in procedures including Job Cards — Exhibit A App A2, §3.12

### HSSE Deliverables
- CONTRACTOR shall refer to HSSE Deliverables as listed in Exhibit L — Exhibit A App A2, §3.2

### Environmental Documents (under Engineering — General)
- Environmental Management Plan (Class 1), Waste Management Plans (Class 2), Oil Spill Response Plan (Class 2), GHG Emission Report (Class 2), CTC Compliance Register (Class 2), ENVID Reports (Class 3) — Exhibit A App A2, §3.3

### Quality Documents
- Quality Assurance Documents per Exhibit H (Class 1), Quality Control Plan/ITP (Class 1), QAP (Class 1), QC procedures (Class 1) — Exhibit A App A2, §3.7

### Commissioning and Pre-Commissioning Documents
- ICAPS Management Procedure (Class 1), ICAPS Data Requirement Procedure (Class 1), Pre-commissioning OPERCOM Forms (Class 1), Commissioning OPERCOM Forms (Class 1) — Exhibit A App A2, §3.11
- Hydro Testing Test Pack Dossier (Class 2, one per test); Instrument loop testing procedure (Class 2); Leak testing procedure (Class 1) — Exhibit A App A2, §3.11
