## Summary

This page captures the NLQ EPCIC 10 services and facilities obligations (Appendix A7). CONTRACTOR must provide comprehensive facilities to COMPANY across all work locations — Engineering Offices, Fabrication Site, Offshore SITE, Marine Spread, and other Worksites. All services must meet a "first class" standard with new equipment. All costs are included in Work Unit 1.5 of Exhibit B App B1.

## Key Points

### Duration Commitments
- Engineering Offices services: from Effective Date (KD1) through 6 months after 90% 3D Model review completion — Exhibit A App A7, §4(i)
- Fabrication Site services: from 3 months before first steel cut (KD17) through 2 months after last READY FOR TRANSPORTATION CERTIFICATE — Exhibit A App A7, §4(ii)
- Installation supervision offshore: from arrival of Marine Spread/Construction Equipment through demobilisation — Exhibit A App A7, §4(iii)
- Hook-up and commissioning supervision offshore: from arrival of integration Marine Spread through demobilisation — Exhibit A App A7, §4(iv)

### Office Standards
- 24/7 building access for all COMPANY personnel and nominated parties — Exhibit A App A7, §3.1
- Type I office: ≥18 m², single occupancy, 4-person meeting table, teleconference equipment — Exhibit A App A7, §3.1
- Type II office: ≥12 m², single occupancy — Exhibit A App A7, §3.1
- Type III office: ≥18 m², double occupancy — Exhibit A App A7, §3.1
- Type IV office: ≥25 m², 5-person capacity — Exhibit A App A7, §3.1
- Large Conference Room: ≥25 m², ≥15 persons, video conferencing with ClickShare/HDMI — Exhibit A App A7, §3.1
- Small Conference Room: ≥8 person capacity, video conferencing — Exhibit A App A7, §3.1
- Exclusive sheltered parking for COMPANY; if unavailable, CONTRACTOR shall provide transport — Exhibit A App A7, §3.1

### IT Infrastructure
- Network hardware: Cisco FPR-1120 firewall (outside Doha); Cisco C9300-48-E switches; Cisco Catalyst 9100 access points (within Doha) — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Primary internet: minimum 100 Mbps (scaling to 150 Mbps for 100–150 users, 200 Mbps for 100–200 users) — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Backup internet: minimum 50 Mbps; primary and backup ISPs must be different providers — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Wi-Fi: minimum 100 Mbps dedicated exclusively for COMPANY PERSONNEL — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Workstations: Dell P2422H 24" FHD monitors (×2); Dell Dock WD19S; keyboard, mouse, headset with MS Teams capability, security lock — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Printers: Canon iR-ADV C5850 equivalent MFP; individual HP P2055D equivalent A4 laser; HP LJC 5525 equivalent A3 colour laser — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Technical room: ≥15 m², 2.5 m ceiling, 42U 19" racks, 19°C air conditioning, reinforced concrete walls, no windows, badge access managed solely by COMPANY — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Video conferencing: MS Teams compatible, ClickShare, HD auto-framing camera, noise-cancelling microphone, touch panel — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Hardware SLA: 4-hour response for critical issues; next business day for non-critical; faulty hardware replaced within 24 hours — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- UPS: minimum 30 minutes backup during power failure — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12

### Facilities and Support Services
- Kitchen: fully stocked with mineral water (still and sparkling), Nespresso-type coffee, tea, soft drinks, biscuits, snacks, fruit — Exhibit A App A7, §3.2
- Messing: daily lunch equivalent to CONTRACTOR management/engineering standard; multi-cultural cuisine; outside caterer if insufficient variety — Exhibit A App A7, §3.3
- Administration: assistance for permits, governmental authorisations, visas, car rental, domestic and international travel for COMPANY personnel, families, and nominated parties — Exhibit A App A7, §3.4
- First Aid: kit including defibrillator; replenished by CONTRACTOR; emergency notification procedure in place — Exhibit A App A7, §3.5
- Security: badge access control; video control system if deemed necessary; badge list managed by COMPANY — Exhibit A App A7, §3.6
- Telephone: local SIM cards with unlimited 4G/5G; USD 100 credit per month; 20 hours/month (10 international + 10 national) — Exhibit A App A7, §3.8
- PPE: all work equipment and PPE for COMPANY PERSONNEL including winter wear, hard hats, overalls, safety boots, marine jackets, waterproofs — Exhibit A App A7, §3.11
- VHF/UHF radios: intrinsically safe; ATEX if required; DTMF keypad; spare batteries and charging units — Exhibit A App A7, §3.13
- Prayer room: shared multifaith room; routine cleaning by CONTRACTOR — Exhibit A App A7, §3.17

### Vehicles
- Type A vehicle: Toyota Land Cruiser 4WD, brand new; with fuel and insurance — Exhibit A App A7, §3.7
- Type B vehicle: saloon 1.8L, brand new; with fuel and insurance — Exhibit A App A7, §3.7
- Type C vehicle: 7-seat minivan 1.8L, with driver available 24/7; full insurance, fuel, licences included — Exhibit A App A7, §3.7

### Offshore Cabins
- Type 4 (single occupancy), Type 5 (double), Type 6 (four occupancy); equipped with electricity, heating/AC, water, telephone, internet, satellite TV — Exhibit A App A7, §3.18

### Reporting and Variations
- Monthly report must include a Services and Facilities to COMPANY section covering quantity and cost — Exhibit A App A7, §5
- COMPANY may request additional services; CONTRACTOR must respond within 7 days with cost and schedule impact — Exhibit A App A7, §4.2
