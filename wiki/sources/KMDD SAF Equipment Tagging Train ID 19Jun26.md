## Summary
Safety discipline (Cao Thành Nhật) raised a new equipment tagging issue to PVEP-KM on 19-Jun: train identifier (A/B suffix) needed for identical safety equipment on the same platform that has different configurations. CPY feedback requested by COB 22-Jun-2026.

## Key Points
- Existing convention confirmed 15-Jun: "01"=KM-WHP, "02"=DD-WHP per KDP-OOO-00-PS-G-0003 Rev B2
- New issue: some safety equipment comes in identical base tags but with different physical configurations (A/B trains); requires train suffix
- Examples proposed: RLB-with-smoke-signal vs RLB-without; RSS-plumbed vs RSS-portable; RLJ-crane vs RLJ-cabinet
- Cao Thành Nhật proposed A/B assignment table by equipment type sent to CPY for feedback
- CPY feedback deadline: COB 22-Jun-2026
