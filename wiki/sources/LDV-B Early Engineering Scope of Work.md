## Summary

MCB-0001-10AA-G-A17-0011 Rev B2 (Issued for Bidder, 17-Oct-2025) defines the Early Engineering scope for LDV-B WHP on Block 15-1/05 Lac Da Vang. COMPANY is Murphy Cuu Long Bac Oil Co. Ltd. (Murphy Oil). The work is a pre-EPCIC study: engineer enough to compile an ITB and a ±10% cost estimate for the full LDV-B Lumpsum EPCIC contract. LDV-A Detailed Engineering (done by PTSC M&C) is the primary analogue.

## Key Points

**Project identity:**
- Block: 15-1/05, East Sea, ~120 km east of Vung Tau; water depth 45–50 m
- Facility: LDV-B WHP — unmanned wellhead platform (8 well slots, no process equipment, cold vent, hydraulic actuated valves)
- COMPANY: Murphy Cuu Long Bac Oil Co. Ltd. (Murphy Oil)
- FEED contractor: Ranhill Worley KL (LDV-B FEED deliverables provided as Company Supplied Data)
- DE analogue: LDV-A Detailed Engineering by PTSC M&C
- Design life: 18 years
- Document number: MCB-0001-10AA-G-A17-0011

**Facility design data:**
| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Oil production | 15,000 BOPD (max); 6,000 BOPD per well |
| Water production | 2,500 BWPD |
| Gas production | 15 MMscfd |
| Gas lift | 16 MMscfd |
| Well slots | 8 |
| Jacket target lift weight | ≤ 1,000 MT |
| Topside target lift weight | ≤ 1,000 MT |
| Platform concept | Unmanned; minimal utilities; fully rated process piping |

**Early Engineering objectives (4 key outputs):**
1. Identify remaining engineering work to compile Tender Documentation for LDV-B Lumpsum EPCIC
2. Provide updated ±10% cost estimate with risk profiles and benchmarking
3. Prepare draft ITB for EPCIC (based on LDV-A ITB, modified for LDV-B and Lumpsum nature)
4. Support COMPANY in contracting strategy approvals

**Scope by discipline:**

| Discipline | Key deliverables |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Process (4.6) | BOD update; HYSYS simulation (max oil + turndown); H&MB; P&ID update to HAZOP-ready; line sizing check; relief valve sizing; blowdown calc; all PDS |
| Jacket STR (4.7) | Structural update; SACS analysis (in-place, fatigue, transport, lifting, pile driveability); T&I study; weight report monthly |
| Topside STR (4.8) | Structural update; SACS analysis (in-place, transport, lifting); weight report; temporary refuge (6-person, no beds) |
| Mechanical (4.9) | Equipment list; layout drawings; datasheets; weight estimates; mechanical handling study |
| Piping (4.10) | All 2"+ lines; pipe stress (Caesar II); PDMS 3D model; MTOs; 60% model review |
| INS + Telecoms (4.11) | Update from LDV-A specs; ICSS architecture; fusible plug layouts (primary fire detection); all instrument datasheets; cable/I/O schedules |
| Electrical (4.12) | Power system: solar arrays + wind turbines (principal) + diesel generator (manned/restart only); load studies; heat tracing |
| Fire & Safety (4.13) | HSE philosophy; HAZID; HAZOP (P&ID comparison vs FEED + close-out); firewater UFD/P&ID |
| Studies (4.15) | Relief and blowdown study; Helideck CAP 437 review |
| Cost estimate (5.0) | Definitive ±10% estimate; CTRs per deliverable; vendor quotes for major equipment |

**Notable LDV-B design features (vs LDV-A):**
- Electrical: solar + wind as principal power; diesel as backup only (manned operations / restart)
- ICSS: DC electric hydraulic pumps + instrument air bottle driven pneumatic controls (vs conventional on LDV-A)
- Fire detection: Fusible plugs as primary (no conventional F&G detectors — minimalistic unmanned concept)
- WHCP: Contains fusible plug circuit controls extracted from LDV-A deluge control panel design
- Temporary Refuge: Emergency shelter only, 6 persons, no beds

**LDV-B pipeline scope:**
- Intra-field pipelines included in Early Engineering scope
- Option to bid pipelines separately from topsides/jacket has been retained
- CONTRACTOR reviews geophysical/geotechnical data for pipeline routes (no update scope)

**Schedule and commercial:**
- CONTRACTOR must provide Level 3 schedule for jacket and deck separately
- Lump Sum proposal format with CTRs for each deliverable
- Unit rates required for all job functions (for variation orders)
- Software costs (HYSYS, OLGA, Pipesim, SACS) to be itemized separately

**Reference documents (COMPANY-supplied):**
- LDV-B FEED deliverables (Ranhill Worley KL)
- LDV-A Detailed Engineering MDR (PTSC M&C)
- Admin procedures: numbering (MCB-0001-10AA-G-A10-0001), formatting, document control
