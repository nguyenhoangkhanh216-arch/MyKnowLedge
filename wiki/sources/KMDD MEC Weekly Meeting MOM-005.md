## Summary

Minutes of the 5th KMDD Mechanical Weekly Meeting, held 04-Jun-2026 at PTSC M&C office. Attendees: CPY (Nguyen Thanh Hieu, Nguyen Duy Phan) and CTR mechanical team. Key decisions: IRCD to be changed from remote to manual type (24VDC power constraint); Closed Drain Vessel wall upgraded to 16mm; 3 successful PV bidders; Teflon gasket required for Pig Trap QOC due to 48–50% CO2. CPY deeply concerned about Open Drain Tank 15 ppm outlet warranty.

## Key Points

- U-stamp required for: CD vessel, Diesel Filter, Pulsation Dampener
- Small pulsation dampener (ID<6"): designed per ASME VIII Div.1 without U-stamp

  - IFR submitted, under CPY review: Pressure Vessels, Pig L&R, Reciprocating Pump, HVAC
  - Under TBC: EODD/Non-API Pump, CO2+N2, Handling Equipment
  - **HOLD:** Chemical Injection
  - IDC (internal PMC review): Storage Tank

### Item 2 — Pressure Vessels (MEC-001)
- **Closed Drain Vessel wall thickness: upgraded 12mm → 16mm** — CPY rejected 12mm; all bidders complied; TBE to be updated in next revision
- Diesel Filter: SS316L (ID<250mm — internal coating not possible); no coating
- **3 successful bidders** — TBE (IFR) completed

### Item 3 — Pig Launcher & Receiver (MEC-002)
- **Pig type confirmed: Spray-PIG, 919±15mm length** (proposed by HML/CPY)
- Bidder barrel dimensions (compatible with Intelligent PIG and Spray PIG):
  - Pig Receiver: Major barrel 2250mm, Minor barrel 2250mm
  - Pig Launcher: Major barrel 2250mm, Minor barrel 1400mm
- **QOC material change:** Hub and Door → ASME II SA350 LF2 Class 1 (from original A694 F65/SA516 Gr.70)
- **Teflon gasket required** due to high CO2 composition (48–50%) — NBR damaged by CO2 in operation. Requires Clamp Ring Type QOC.
- U-stamp: Door only (A694 not registered in ASME code, cannot stamp Full QOC)

### Item 4 — Closed Drain Transfer Pump (MEC-003)
- **2 successful bidders with different leak detection:**
  - TTE/McFarland: single packing + drain collection reservoir
  - BN/Sabi: double packings (prevent leakage)
- CPY: Closed drain pump must follow project specification (standard baseplate not accepted); Non-API pumps: standard baseplate accepted
- Soft starters: handled by Electrical team (not in MEC scope)

### Item 5 — EODD & Non-API Centrifugal Pump (MEC-004)
- **Semi-open impeller confirmed acceptable** per ASME B73.1 and ISO 5199
- Diesel/Service water pumps: small size, cannot meet 70–100% BEP operating point — PMC expediting bidder datasheets and performance curves for CPY review
- Auto Pressure Controller for Service Water Pump: PS provided by PMC per latest P&ID

### Item 6 — Open Drain Tank & Tote Tank (MEC-005)
- **Outlet 15 ppm regulatory basis confirmed:** Marpol Annex 1 AND Vietnamese Circular 02/2022/TT-BTNMT
- **Cannot be tested at FAT** — must be warranted by vendor; tested during offshore operations
- **CPY deeply concerned** about how to guarantee 15 ppm outlet with 10% HC inlet — open item
- GRP water tank sunlight protection: canvas cover proposed; CPY queries whether 34°C ambient affects eyewash water temperature

### Item 7 — CO2 & N2 Bottles (MEC-007)
- Corrosion Allowance per datasheet: under TBC with bidders
- Height monitoring vs Pedestal Crane: under TBC
- 10-year design lifetime applicability to bottles: under TBC
- Sample certificates sent to CPY (to be attached in TBE)

### Item 8 — HVAC (MEC-009)
- Drain piping: CS+3mm per piping specification (updated from copper in Rev.E0)
- AC compressor motors: non-hazardous classified (safe area installation — manufacturer standard)
- **Refrigerant: A1 classified (not A2L)** — A2L not feasible for standard 24VDC AC units. A1 has lower energy efficiency → slightly higher power consumption for same cooling capacity. Compliant with CPY request; noted and closed.

### Item 9 — Chemical & Methanol Injection (MEC-006)
- **IRCD manual type proposed** — 80W × 2 base + 320W × 4 future = 480W total 24VDC demand exceeds platform capacity. PMC proposing manual IRCD instead of remote. More practical operationally. PMC to email CPY for advice by **05-Jun-2026**. (see [[KMDD MEC-006 IRCD Power Supply Issue]])
- **CPY prefers integrated CI + methanol skid** — requests PMC to review/optimize overall dimensions; integration preferred. Due 05-Jun-2026.
- **Sight glass on chemical tanks retained** — CPY rejected PMC's proposal to remove; used frequently during normal operation
- Chemical/methanol injection: process updates treated as optional items; CI skid and methanol skid kept separate in current scope; updated datasheets distributed to bidders (bidders need 1–2 weeks to study)
- Chemical injection re-IFR datasheets: HOLD pending final corrosion study; methanol HOLD pending cold well startup study
- PCV on CI PSV recycle line: under review

### Item 10 — Material Handling (MEC-008)
- DEG vendor will NOT provide special tools (hoist, beam) — PMC to study handling method after receiving DEG drawings
- SDV handling method: PMC to send to CPY (due 04-Jun-26)
