## Summary

HAZID study report for Dam Doi Wellhead Platform (DD-WHP), conducted per ISO 17776 in parallel with the KM-WHP HAZID. Identifies 43 hazardous scenarios, confirms 7 MAHs, and raises the same 3 recommendations as KM-WHP. Document KDP-DDA-03-RP-X-0001-D0, issued for Review (IFR) 03-Jun-2026.

## Key Points

### Study Summary
- **Total hazardous scenarios identified:** 43
- **Major Accident Hazards (MAHs):** 7 confirmed
- **Risk distribution:** Low = 22, Medium = 13, High = 8, Very High = 0
- Platform design is near-identical to KM-WHP → same hazard profile

### Primary Hazard Categories
Identical to KM-WHP: hydrocarbons in formation (H-01.02), gas from production/FWS systems (H-01.06), overhead objects (H-06.02), objects under tension (H-07.01), boat collision (H-08.04), fire and explosion (H-31.01).

DD-WHP additionally includes hazards from the **FWS import from KM-WHP** (high-pressure pipeline gas received on topsides).

### Recommendations (3 total — same as KM-WHP)
1. **EPCI — Diesel bunkering hose:** Remove connected-hose diesel bunkering (B2 risk; COMPANY responsibility)
2. **FEED pending — WAT analysis:** Perform live WAT analysis post-production (COMPANY responsibility)
3. **FEED pending — PPD test:** Test PPD chemical on condensate after initial production (COMPANY responsibility)
