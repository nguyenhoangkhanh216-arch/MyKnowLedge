## Summary

Follow-up on the 22-Jun meeting between PVEP-KM, VSP OCD+MED, and PMC to resolve the SHWT power load overrun. Current total platform load is 112,499.01 Wh/day — 33% above FEED and excluding the standard 15% margin. Nine action items were issued to PMC, VSP, and CPY with due dates between 23–25-Jun. Key finding: solar panels confirmed in Non-Hazardous Area (no Ex-certification required); 420–480W panels are feasible. Load list update to bidders targeted 25-Jun.

## Key Points

- **Current load:** 112,499.01 Wh/day (+33% vs FEED); the 15% design margin is NOT yet applied on top of this
- **Meeting:** 22-Jun — PVEP-KM, VSP OCD, VSP MED, PMC
- **Action items and deadlines:**
  - PMC due 23-Jun: (1) check FGS marshalling cabinet power; (2) separate fan ICSS load — Marshalling 4h vs System 16h; (3) review solar panel alternatives (420–480W range); (4) confirm wind parameters with SHWT vendor
  - VSP due 23-Jun: update HVAC heat dissipation load
  - CPY due 24-Jun: (1) advise on spare I/O cards — ship-loose or installed; (2) confirm BRA remote cabinet network backup approach; (3) advise acceptable operating temperature range (28–35°C query); (4) accept or reject removal of the 15% margin
  - PMC due 25-Jun: update Load List and transmit to SHWT bidders
- **Solar panels — HAC clarification (VSP, Nguyễn Hải Sơn, 08:34 23-Jun):**
  - Solar panels are located in Non-Hazardous Area per FEED Hazardous Area Classification (HAC) drawings
  - Therefore Ex-certification for solar panels is NOT mandatory
  - 420–480W panels (higher power density) are technically feasible within this zone
- **ELE-001 SHWT commercial bids due 23-Jun** (same day) — load list update on 25-Jun will be after bid opening; supplementary information or rebid may be needed
