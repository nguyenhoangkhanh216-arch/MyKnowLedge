## Summary

Exhibit G Rev B is the Coordination Procedure for the NLQ EPCIC 10 contract (NOC/Al Shaheen). It is a 41-page contract exhibit that defines how CONTRACTOR and COMPANY interact throughout all project phases. It governs: organisation and key personnel, the Contract Coordination Manual (CCM), meeting cadence (kick-off through daily), reporting obligations (weekly/monthly/daily/cost/QA-QC/KPI), communication protocols, project control, interface and risk management, document control (MDR, transmittal, EDMS, FINAL DOCUMENTATION), supply chain management (procurement, packing, shipping, spare parts), HSE, worksite/workshop coordination, and the 3D model implementation plan (AVEVA Global).

## Key Points

### Organisation (§2)
- COMPANY REPRESENTATIVE: Jerome Morin, NLQ Package Lead, North Oil Company, Doha — Exhibit G, §2.1.1
- CONTRACTOR REPRESENTATIVE and Deputy must be formally designated with full contact details at contract award — Exhibit G, §2.2
- CONTRACTOR organisation is detailed in Exhibit K; any key personnel change requires written COMPANY APPROVAL — Exhibit G, §2.2.4

### Contract Coordination Manual (§3)
- CONTRACTOR shall develop, implement, and maintain a CCM covering all phases of WORK — Exhibit G, §3.1.3
- CCM delivered in three parts: Part 1 (org chart, mob plan, calendar, sub/vendor list, procedures), Part 2 (PEP, Level 0-3 schedules, QP, interface plan, risk plan, procurement plan, logistics plan, AVEVA 3D plan), Part 3 (worksite and site coordination procedures) — Exhibit G, §3.2.3
- CCM comes into force only after COMPANY APPROVAL — Exhibit G, §3.1.4

### Reporting Cut-Off Dates (§4.1)
- Weekly cut-off: every Thursday at close of business — Exhibit G, §4.1.1
- Monthly cut-off dates defined in Appendix G6 — Exhibit G, §4.1.1
- Weekly meeting on agreed day (not the same week as monthly meeting); monthly meeting in second week of each month — Exhibit G, §4.1.3

### Meeting Cadence (§4.2)
- **Kick-off Meeting**: within 2 weeks from Effective Date; discipline KOMs within 15 days; subcontract KOMs within 1 month — Exhibit G, §4.2.2
- **Weekly Meetings**: appraise technical conformity and progress; CONTRACTOR REPRESENTATIVE or deputy + relevant managers — Exhibit G, §4.2.4
- **Monthly Meetings**: all weekly topics + contractual/commercial issues including Change Orders; CONTRACTOR REPRESENTATIVE + key personnel — Exhibit G, §4.2.5
- **Opening Worksite Meetings**: held at start of WORK on each worksite — Exhibit G, §4.2.6
- **Worksite Weekly Meetings**: cover progress, schedules, resources, HSE, logistics, TQs, non-conformances, customs, PTW — Exhibit G, §4.2.7
- **Daily Meetings**: may be requested during pre-commissioning/commissioning — Exhibit G, §4.2.9
- Meeting minutes: real-time displayed on screen, printed and signed by both parties, distributed within 2 days — Exhibit G, §4.2.1.7
- Slide pack transmitted to COMPANY at least 2 days in advance — Exhibit G, §4.2.1.4

### Reporting Requirements (§4.3)
- **Weekly Report**: draft 1 working day before weekly meeting; final within 2 days after meeting; format per App G1 — Exhibit G, §4.3.2
- **Monthly Report**: draft 2 days before monthly meeting; final within 2 days after; includes executive summary, risk, photos — Exhibit G, §4.3.3
- **Cost Report**: monthly; includes initial contract price, expenditure, EFC, cash flow, COI/COR/CO status, advance/customs payment — Exhibit G, §4.3.4
- **Invoice Report**: monthly; 3 sections: lump sum, provisional sum, Change Order, customs, ICV — Exhibit G, §4.3.5
- **Document Control Report**: weekly; documents issued by WU, late documents by acceptance class — Exhibit G, §4.3.6
- **QA/QC Report**: monthly; NCR/CAR registers, KPI status, audit schedule, TQ status, deviation status, FINAL DOCUMENTATION progress — Exhibit G, §4.3.7
- **KPI Report**: monthly; examples: supports fabricated, structural tonnage, weld reject rate, painted area, cable installed — Exhibit G, §4.3.8
- **Daily Reports**: submitted by 08:00 next morning for all SITE activities; include PTW records, POB, manhours, safety records — Exhibit G, §4.3.9
- **Asset Register**: tagged items with tag number, location, description, class, and value; submitted as part of FINAL DOCUMENTATION — Exhibit G, §4.3.11.1
- **ICV Plan Tracker**: completed annually per Exhibit N — Exhibit G, §4.3.11.3

### Change Orders (§4.4 + App G2)
- Change Order Instruction (COI) issued by COMPANY; CONTRACTOR must respond within 7 days with cost and schedule impact — Exhibit G App G2, §2
- Change Order Request (COR) submitted by CONTRACTOR within 7 days of triggering event; must include full cost breakdown, schedule impact, ICV impact — Exhibit G App G2, §3
- COMPANY reviews COR within 14 days — Exhibit G App G2, §3.4
- Price evaluation methods in priority order: lump-sum comparison, unit rates, time rates, reference to nearest rates in Exhibit B — Exhibit G App G2, §4.1.1
- Schedule extensions only granted if affected WORK is on critical path with zero float — Exhibit G App G2, §4.2.2
- WORK must not be delayed pending change order resolution — Exhibit G App G2, §3.6

### Insurance Claim Procedure (§4.5 + App G4)
- CONTRACTOR must report loss/damage immediately via Insurance Incident Report including photos, sequence number, repair estimate — Exhibit G App G4, §3.1.1
- Damaged property must be preserved; no repair without COMPANY and insurer approval — Exhibit G App G4, §3.1.2
- COMPANY is the sole negotiating party with insurers — Exhibit G App G4, §6.2

### Communication (§5)
- All formal correspondence addressed CONTRACTOR → COMPANY REPRESENTATIVE and vice versa; no cross-communication — Exhibit G, §5.3.1
- Correspondence numbered consecutively per SD-NOC-MP-701; all documents under transmittal sheet — Exhibit G, §5.3.1
- Telephone calls and emails are NOT contractual correspondence; must be confirmed in writing — Exhibit G, §5.5
- Documents sent Thursday after 12:00 p.m. treated as received following Sunday (i.e., next working day) — Exhibit G, §9.1.4
- Site communications categories: field memorandum, MOM, COMPANY instructions — Exhibit G, §5.6.1.2

### Document Control (§9)
- Lead Document Controller (LDC) required with experience on COMPANY's EDMS — Exhibit G, §9.1.1
- All documents: max 25 documents per transmittal (TSI); exchange via ShareFile Transfer Folder (FTP) — Exhibit G, §9.1.4
- EDMS: ICAD for drawings, IOffice for all other documents; smart TAGs via iTools mandatory — Exhibit G, §9.1.4
- PDF requirements: intelligent .Ipdf from native file, in colour, with OCR, bookmarks, no PDF/A — Exhibit G, §9.1.4
- MDR maintained weekly; initial MDR is baseline and cannot be modified without COMPANY APPROVAL — Exhibit G, §9.4.8
- Native files (Class 1 and 2) required from IFR through as-built status; COMPANY may request Class 3 native files — Exhibit G, §9.4.13
- Comment Response Sheet (CRS) required: all COMPANY comments consolidated and displayed as first page from Rev 01 until Code 1 — Exhibit G, §9.5.3
- FINAL DOCUMENTATION: 3 sets on DataLocker encrypted drives; includes as-built 3D CAD model, GIS, video subsea surveys, RT films — Exhibit G, §9.8.5
- Vendor final dossiers submitted to COMPANY 1 month after each FAT — Exhibit G, §9.8.6

### Supply Chain Management (§10)
- Procurement register, requisition register, traffic register, spare parts register, consumables register: all issued weekly — Exhibit G, §10.3.3, §10.5.2, §10.6
- Bid evaluation: minimum COMPANY-approved bidder list; 2-stage evaluation (technical → commercial); bid analysis report is Class 2 — Exhibit G, §10.3.1
- Evaluation criteria include: HSE policy, delivery, technical/QC conformity, MTBF/MTTR, spare parts availability, cyber security — Exhibit G, §10.3.1.3
- COMPANY invited to all clarification meetings; if not attending, summary meeting required — Exhibit G, §10.3.1.5
- Packing: heat-treated wood crates per ISPM 15; MSDS for dangerous goods; IATA for airfreight — Exhibit G, §10.4.3
- Shipping documentation: BOL, commercial invoice with HS codes, packing list, CCVO, CRR, SGD — Exhibit G, §10.5.1.3
- Spare parts: catalogues with photo, quantities, sectional drawings, prices, lead time, serial/part numbers, interchangeability — Exhibit G, §10.6.2.2

### 3D Model Implementation Plan (§14)
- CONTRACTOR shall purchase and set up AVEVA Global as satellite location; COMPANY is Hub — Exhibit G, §14
- 3D model plan must define: model review timing (30%/60%/90%), clash checking, isometric extraction timing, MTO update, CAD-material management system link — Exhibit G, §14
- COMPANY 3D model coordinator must have permanent access — Exhibit G, §14
- 3D model is a contractual engineering deliverable subject to COMPANY APPROVAL — Exhibit G, §14
