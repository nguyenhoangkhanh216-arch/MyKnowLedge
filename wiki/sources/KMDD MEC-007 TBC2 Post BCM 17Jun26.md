## Summary
MEC-007 CO2 Snuffing and Nitrogen Bottle Package: Following BCM (Bid Clarification Meeting), only TTE (1 out of 5 bidders) is evaluated as Acceptable. PTSC sent TBC#2 Rev.A to TTE post-BCM on 17-Jun with deadline 19-Jun-2026, raising two technical issues: CO2 snuffing control logic (solenoid valve arrangement) and process sizing calculations.
