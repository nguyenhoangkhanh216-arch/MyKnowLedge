## Summary

Piping team identified that line 250-PG-50014-63410X (stainless steel, Class 600) has a design pressure of 92 barg @ 80°C, which exceeds the ASME Class 600 max allowable working pressure for SS at 80°C (89.12 barg). COMPANY process agreed in principle to reduce max design temperature from 80°C to 65°C, keeping Class 600 rating. This needs to be formalized via a Technical Query (TQ) after LOA.

## Key Points

- **Line affected:** 250-PG-50014-63410X (stainless steel, Class 600)
- **Problem:** Design conditions 92 barg @ 80°C exceed ASME Class 600 SS limit of 89.12 barg at 80°C
- **Resolution agreed:** Reduce max design temperature from 80°C → 65°C to stay within Class 600
- **Justification:** FWS fluid from KM-WHP cools to near seabed temperature during subsea transit to DD-WHP; max operating temp on DD-WHP = 55°C
- **Alternatives considered:** Upgrade rating (Class 900+) or upgrade material (duplex SS)
- **Status:** Verbal agreement with COMPANY process; requires formalization via TQ after LOA. Other SS lines to be reviewed case-by-case.
- **Who raised it:** Piping Team (Nguyen Xuan Tan Binh) flagged to Process Team (Truong Minh Hoang) for resolution
