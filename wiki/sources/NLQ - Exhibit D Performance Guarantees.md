## Summary

Exhibit D Rev A defines the contractual performance guarantees framework for the NLQ EPCIC 10 project (Al Shaheen Oil Field, Qatar). CONTRACTOR guarantees that the PLANT meets all design, construction, and commissioning performance criteria set out in the DESIGN DOSSIER, with formal Performance Tests to be executed and witnessed by COMPANY before the Provisional Acceptance Certificate (PAC) is issued. A structured test programme of 13 equipment/system categories defines acceptance criteria and test durations.

## Key Points

- CONTRACTOR guarantees the WORK shall be designed, constructed, installed and commissioned in accordance with the AGREEMENT and EXHIBITS; performance must comply with DESIGN DOSSIER minimum requirements — Exhibit D, §1
- CONTRACTOR ITEMS shall meet performance criteria set in the DESIGN DOSSIER complemented by CONTRACTOR datasheets and COMPANY-approved specifications — Exhibit D, §1.4
- Performance tests are additional to FATs, SATs, pre-commissioning, commissioning tests, and OTPs required in Exhibits A, E, G, and H — Exhibit D, §2
- PERFORMANCE TEST procedures shall be prepared by CONTRACTOR and APPROVED by COMPANY at least one (1) month prior to the start of commissioning activities — Exhibit D, §4.1.i
- Standard conditions for tests are 1.01325 bara and 15.56 °C — Exhibit D, §4.2.1
- COMPANY shall ensure necessary throughput for Performance Tests within 24 weeks after the final TOM is approved — Exhibit D, §4.1.iv
- All PERFORMANCE TESTS shall be completed prior to issuance of the PROVISIONAL ACCEPTANCE CERTIFICATE (PAC) — Exhibit D, §4.1.x
- If a system fails a PERFORMANCE TEST, the test shall restart from the beginning unless otherwise APPROVED by COMPANY — Exhibit D, §4.1.vi
- CONTRACTOR shall issue a test results report to COMPANY within thirty (30) days after receipt of required analytical data — Exhibit D, §4.2.7
- PLANT general guarantees (§3): 25-year design life; safe/reliable/maintainable/operable throughout life; environmental respect; designed for site environmental conditions including installation and operating conditions; corrosion coating warranty as per SPECIFICATIONS — Exhibit D, §3

### NLQ Test Programme (Table 4-1)

| No | Equipment | Acceptance Criteria | Test Duration |
|----|-----------|--------------------|-|
| 1 | Air Compressor & Dryer | Deliver pressure and dew point per datasheet; offshore conditions; noise, vibration, bearing temp, oil consumption per spec | 1 week |
| 2 | Lighting | Lux measurement per specifications in normal offshore conditions | 1 month |
| 3 | UPS and Battery | Onshore: 100% load test (load bank active and reactive power); Offshore: operation test in operating conditions | Onshore 24 h / Offshore 1 month |
| 4 | HVAC | Cooling capacity, humidity, airflow rate, room pressurisation, temperature per CONTRACTOR DOCUMENTS | 1 month (20 days summer + 10 days winter) |
| 5 | Fire & Gas Detectors | No faulty F&G detection by any gas or fire detector installed | 1 month |
| 6 | ICSS | 99.9% availability with no major fault (controller CPU/PSU/I/O cards; HIS station/network card; Ethernet switch) | 1 month |
| 7 | Telecom | PA/GA, WIFI network and other telecom devices: 99.99% availability with no major fault | 1 month |
| 8 | Crane | 133% load tested onshore; re-tested after transit at 100% SWL; certificate required | — |
| 9 | NAVAIDS | Per latest CONTRACTOR DOCUMENTS | 1 week |
| 10 | EDG | Per latest CONTRACTOR DOCUMENTS | 12 h site run test |
| 11 | Potable Water System | Per latest CONTRACTOR DOCUMENTS | 1 week |
| 12 | Sewage Treatment | Per latest CONTRACTOR DOCUMENTS | 1 week |
| 13 | Fire Water Pump | Per latest CONTRACTOR DOCUMENTS | 12 h site run test |

### Turndown Test (§4.2.4)

- Demonstrates ability of installed facilities to achieve turndown specification for 48 continuous hours of stable operation while meeting product specifications — Exhibit D, §4.2.4
- Turndown condition to be defined and agreed with COMPANY prior to starting; duration may be reduced if requested by COMPANY — Exhibit D, §4.2.4
