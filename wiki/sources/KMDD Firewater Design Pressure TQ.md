## Summary

Technical Query KDP-OOO-03-TQ-X-0001-(P) raised by PTSC M&C (Sub-CTR) to VSP/PVEP-KM (CTR/CPY) on 04-Jun-2026 requesting confirmation of the final design pressure and tie-in flange specification for the KM-WHP and DD-WHP Firewater Systems. The current FEED basis (16 barg, 6" 150#) requires verification against the PV Drilling III Rig firewater system, which was agreed as the basis rig for EPCI detailed engineering per MOA DEV/2026/06 (13-Mar-2026).

## Key Points

- **TQ No.:** KDP-OOO-03-TQ-X-0001-(P) — first Safety TQ on the KMDD project
- **Date issued:** 04-Jun-2026 | **Response required:** 15-Jun-2026 (URGENT)
- **Discipline:** Safety / Process Safety
- **Originator:** Nguyen Thanh Chung / Cao Thanh Nhat (Safety team); Approved by Nguyen Dinh Manh (Engineering Manager)
- **Issue:** FEED firewater design pressure 16 barg with 6" 150# tie-in flanges — not yet verified against Drilling Rig / Supply Vessel actual firewater system parameters
- **Scope:** KM-WHP and DD-WHP firewater systems (P&IDs: KDP-DDA-03-DW-X-63001-D0 and KDP-KMA-03-DW-X-63001-D0, Rev. D0, issued 24-Mar-2026)
- **Proposed solution:** Verify current design against PVD III Rig Firewater System design pressure; if a different rig is later selected, additional measures to be identified during Drilling phase
- **Basis for PVD III:** MOA DEV/2026/06 (13-Mar-2026) between SSF and DEV departments — PV Drilling III Jack-Up rig agreed as basis for all EPCI detailed engineering
- **P&ID data (from attached drawings):**
  - Fire monitors FMO-82-01/02/03 (both KM-WHP and DD-WHP): capacity 120 m³/hr, operating pressure 3.5 barg, **design pressure 16 barg**
  - Firewater ring main: **DRY during routine/normal operation**
  - Firewater supply during drilling: from Drilling Rig (Main Deck tie-in, DN150)
  - Backup supply: from Marine Supply Vessel (Boat Landing East, Cellar Deck tie-in, DN150)
  - Control valves XV-6320 (DD-WHP) / XV-6310 (KM-WHP) at ring main entry — normally closed
  - Monitors locked pointing toward wellbay/escape routes during SIPROD
- **Cost/Schedule impact:** No (per TQ form)
