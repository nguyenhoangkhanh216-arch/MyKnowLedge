## Summary

PTSC M&C (Nguyen Dinh Manh) submitted the Vendor Data Required List (VDRL) for three COMPANY free-issue packages — Crane, HIPPS, and DEG — to VSP (Nguyen Khac Hung) on 15-Jun-2026. The VDRL includes required submission dates for each vendor document/data item to support engineering review, interface alignment, and design incorporation.

## Key Points

- **Sender:** Nguyen Dinh Manh (PTSC M&C Engineering Department)
- **Recipient:** Nguyen Khac Hung (VSP — representing COMPANY/PVEP-KM side)
- **Free-issue packages covered:**
  - **Crane** — COMPANY-supplied crane; PTSC needs vendor data to design structural supports and interfaces
  - **HIPPS** — High Integrity Pressure Protection System; vendor data needed for P&ID integration, instrument loop design
  - **DEG** — Diesel Electric Generator; vendor data needed for electrical load list, mechanical arrangement, exhaust routing
- **Attachment:** KMDD - Free Issued Package - VDRL Required List.xlsx — lists each vendor document, description, and required submission date
- **Purpose:** Allow all parties common visibility and coordinate timely receipt of vendor data; required before engineering can complete interface design

### Free-Issue Package Context
- **Free-issue (FI):** COMPANY (PVEP-KM) procures and supplies equipment directly to the contractor (PTSC M&C) — contractor installs and integrates
- PTSC cannot proceed with interface engineering (support design, cable routing, control system integration) until COMPANY provides the vendor's GA drawings, data sheets, load data, and P&ID mark-ups
- VDRL identifies what documents are needed, from whom, and by when — a formal coordination tool
