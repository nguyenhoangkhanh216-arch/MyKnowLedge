## Summary
PTSC Lead Technical Safety (Cao Thành Nhật) formally requested pipeline length data from VSP (Thiện/Huy) to support the Fire Risk Analysis (FERA/FRA) for KM-WHP and DD-WHP. Based on the Alternative Development Scheme routing sketch, the preliminary lengths are KM-WHP: 6.7 km/20" and DD-WHP: 25.4 km/20" + 5.8 km/22". VSP confirmation required by COB 26-Jun-2026.

## Key Points
- FERA (Fire Risk Analysis) is a 3rd party study being conducted for KM-WHP and DD-WHP
- Pipeline inventory data is needed for section inventory calculations in the FERA model
- Preliminary pipeline lengths from Alternative Development Scheme:
  - KM-WHP: 6.7 km, 20" pipeline
  - DD-WHP: 25.4 km, 20" pipeline + 5.8 km, 22" pipeline
- Requestor: Cao Thành Nhật (Lead Technical Safety, PTSC M&C)
- Addressees: VSP Thiện and Huy (Engineering)
- Response deadline: COB 26-Jun-2026
- Associated engineering documents required: subsea pipeline routing, subsea spool routing, etc.
