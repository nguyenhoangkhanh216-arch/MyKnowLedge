## Summary

On 04-Jun-2026 PVEP-KM (Nguyen Minh Tuan) returned CPY's feedback on the 8 HAZOP close-out actions proposed by PTSC (Nguyen Dinh Manh, 02-Jun). This email thread also contains the full HAZOP/SIL KOM history: KOM was held online 23-Apr-2026 with RISKTEC TUV in UAE; CPY approved single safety study scope covering topsides + jackets + subsea pipeline.

## Key Points

- **Item 1 — PSV-1931A/B removal:** CPY "Agree in principle. PMC to provide full calculation in close-out sheet." (max 88.2 barg < 92 barg design; no credible reverse flow overpressure scenario)
- **Item 2 — PSV downstream HIPPS (replace bleed-off XV-1120):** CPY "Agree in principle. PMC to provide mark-up for PSVs (setpoint 92 barg DD-WHP, 105 barg KM-WHP) for checking and finalization."
- **Item 3 — Positive isolation upstream MOV-1921:** CPY "Agree." (spade & spacer provided upstream of export launcher valve)
- **Item 4 — Recycle line on CD transfer pump P-5020A/B startup:** CPY "PMC to provide mark-up for configuration of newly added recycle line for checking and finalization."
- **Item 5 — Vent thermal sensor TS-5640 backup:** CPY "Noted." (thermal switch/transmitter for vent temp monitoring; access stair/ladder for maintenance)
- **Item 6 — N2 supply to methanol tote tank T-5381:** CPY requires "method that prevents nitrogen loss when there's no operator on the platform." PMC proposed 2×100% PCVs if fully remote startup; single PCV check if local operator present.
- **Item 7 — FW ring main flowrate/pressure on P&ID:** CPY "Noted." (TQR KDP-OOO-03-TQ-X-0001-(P) already submitted; P&IDs to update after CPY response)
- **Item 8 — Water tote tank tarpaulin wrapping:** CPY "**Not accepted**. Tarpaulin unlikely to withstand wind conditions on Main Deck." Only insulation of service water pipework exposed to sun is acceptable.
- **HAZOP/SIL KOM history:** Online KOM 23-Apr-2026 (RISKTEC UTC+8, CPY Vietnam +7); CPY requirement: single set of safety studies for entire greenfield facilities; VSP must participate (jackets/subsea pipeline scope)
