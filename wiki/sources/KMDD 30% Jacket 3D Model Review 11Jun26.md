## Summary

NIPI (Vietsovpetro Research and Design Institute) completed and submitted the 30% 3D model for the KMDD jacket structures. A joint review meeting was organized for 11 June 2026 at the NIPI office in Vung Tau, with attendance from STR, Tin, EMT teams in person and other disciplines online. PVEP KM confirmed attendance.

- **Submitter:** NIPI (Nguyễn Bảo Hoa, Senior Project Engineer, vietsov.com.vn)
- **Model link:** 3D model shared via Vietsovpetro cloud storage (internal link)
- **Review meeting:** Thursday **11 June 2026**, from 08:00 until completion
- **Venue:** Room 203, Building A11 — Viện NCKH&TK (NIPI), 105 Lê Lợi, Phường Vũng Tàu, TPHCM (Vung Tau)
- **Attendance:** STR, Trần Trọng Tín, and EMT teams in person; remaining teams online via MS Teams
- **Participants confirmed:** PVEP-KM (Nguyen Khac Hung confirmed delegation); VSP; PMC (PTSC M&C)
- **Post-review social event:** NIPI invited PVEP-KM, VSP, and PMC to a social gathering at Beer Độ Restaurant (3 Lương Thế Vinh) — relationship building between jacket and topside teams
- **Context:** This is the 30% design milestone review for the jacket sub-structures of both KM-WHP and DD-WHP. It feeds into the overall KMDD design progression toward 60% model review.
