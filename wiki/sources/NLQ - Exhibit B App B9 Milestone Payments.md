## Summary

Appendix B9 Rev D (4 pages) lists 23 milestone payment events that comprise the 35% milestone portion of the Lump Sum Payment. Each milestone has a percentage of the Lump Sum; percentages total 35% of the Lump Sum Prices. Milestones may be added, deleted, or combined depending on TENDERER's execution strategy.
