## Key Points

- **Request:** Bidders (DYNAC and MOTEC) to advise **power consumption of the HCP** (HVAC Control Panel)
- **Purpose:** Electrical Team review and consideration in the **platform power load assessment**
- **Supporting documents required:** Datasheets, catalogs, or any document indicating bidder's proposed power consumption
- **Initiated by:** Nguyễn Tất Đông (Mechanical Team) to Tran Thi Khanh Ngan (buyer)

### Cross-Discipline Note

This is a cross-discipline issue: MEC-009 scope includes the HVAC system, but the power consumption of the HCP is an Electrical discipline concern (electrical load list). HVAC vendors must supply this data to allow Electrical to update the platform load schedule. This type of inter-discipline TBC input is common for packages with significant electrical demand.
