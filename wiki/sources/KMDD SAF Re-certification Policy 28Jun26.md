## Summary
Cao Thành Nhật (Safety Engineer) proposed a 4-point re-inspection/re-certification policy for SAF-001 (Fire Fighting, PE: Hoang An) and SAF-002 (Life Saving, PE: Nguyễn Thành Chung) on 28-Jun-2026. Policy addresses how to handle re-certification of safety equipment during the wait period between delivery and handover to CPY. Feedback requested from PRECOM team (Phan Thanh Bình and Võ Trường Thịnh) by COB 30-Jun-26.

## Key Points
- **Issue:** Safety equipment packages are currently in procurement; after delivery and during waiting for platform handover to CPY, re-certification may be needed
- **Addressees:** Phan Thanh Bình and Võ Trường Thịnh BD003488 (PRECOM team)
- **From:** Cao Thành Nhật (Process-Safety Engineer)

### Proposed 4-point policy:
1. **Minimize re-certification window:** PE requests bidder to perform testing and first inspection/certification as close to delivery date as possible
2. **Re-certification capability confirmed in bidding:** PE clarifies in TBC/bidding phase that bidder must have capability to re-certify OR hire relevant authorities to handle re-certification when requested
3. **Preliminary identification list:** PE reviews and identifies which items require re-certification (preliminary list attached as Excel); clarify with bidder during bidding
4. **PRECOM/COM execution:** PRECOM/COM team requests re-certification as needed based on actual field conditions

- **Packages affected:**
  - SAF-001 Fire Fighting Equipment (Hoang An, PE)
  - SAF-002 Life Saving & Safety Equipment (Nguyễn Thành Chung, PE)
- **Feedback deadline:** COB 30-Jun-2026
