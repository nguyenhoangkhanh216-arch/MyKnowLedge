## Summary
Appendix B1 Rev A is the Contract Price Breakdown WBS table for NLQ EPCIC 10. It maps all Work Packages (WP1–WP11) and Work Units (WUs) to Lump Sum or Provisional Sum columns, with separate In-Country (Qatar) and Out-of-Country (USD) columns. All dollar cells are blank for TENDERER to fill.

## Key Points
- WBS structure: WP (Work Package) → WU (Work Unit) → SWU (Sub Work Unit) → SSWU — Exhibit B App B1
- Work Packages identified: WP1 Management/Project Control/Admin; WP2 HSE; WP3 Engineering; further WPs cover fabrication, procurement, construction, hook-up, commissioning, T&I — Exhibit B App B1
- WU 1.5 Services to COMPANY: classified as Provisional Sum (PS) — Exhibit B App B1
- WU 2.3 HSE Incentive Scheme: classified as Provisional Sum (PS) — Exhibit B App B1
- WU 1.7 EPCIC 10 Digitalisation: classified as Lump Sum — Exhibit B App B1
- TENDERER must submit a second copy with ICV Provisional Sum at maximum 5% per Exhibit N — Exhibit B App B1
- In-Country vs Out-of-Country split required for every WU — Exhibit B App B1
