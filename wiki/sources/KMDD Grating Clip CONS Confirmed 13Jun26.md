## Summary

The KMDD Construction team (Dương Hồng Thái) confirmed on 13-Jun-2026 that the grating clip design from FEED is executable by the Construction team, though noted it is "not the best solution." The email thread also contains a large drawing schedule table listing approximately 100+ AFC (Approved for Construction) drawings for DD-WHP and KM-WHP with their DE planned release dates versus Construction's requested earlier dates.

## Key Points

- **Grating clip from FEED:** CONS confirmed the design is workable ("can be done") — minor concern about construction preference ("not the best solution") suggests CONS prefers a different clip style, but accepted the FEED design
- **Confirmation by:** Dương Hồng Thái (Construction Engineer), 13-Jun-2026
- **AFC Drawing Schedule Conflict:** The email contains a detailed table (~100+ drawings) with:
  - DE Planned release dates for AFC drawings
  - Construction's requested earlier dates (CONS needs drawings earlier for fabrication planning)
  - Discrepancy exists between DE planned dates and CONS required dates — suggests schedule pressure
- **Implications for DE team:** Drawing release dates must be managed carefully to not delay Construction; any slippage in DE schedule directly impacts fabrication planning
