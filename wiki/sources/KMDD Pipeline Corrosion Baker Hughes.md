## Summary

Corrosion workshop (20-Apr-2026) covering TOL (Top of Line) and BOL (Bottom of Line) corrosion in the KM-DD and DD-BK-C subsea pipelines. Baker Hughes engaged as chemical treatment vendor. Uninhibited corrosion rates are very high (11–19 mm/y). Two treatment options under study: V-Jet Pig injection or CI injection. Chemical dosing rates for scale inhibitor and PPD/Wax inhibitor under Baker Hughes review.

## Key Points

### Pipeline Fluid Properties (Corrosion Design Basis)

| Parameter | KM → DD (20", ~7 km) | DD → BK-C (20", 26 km) |
|---|---|---|
| Operating Temp (°C) | 25–70 | 25–54 (can reach -1.3 to 3.0°C) |
| Mixed velocity (m/s) | 1.8–2.4 | 1.2–3.5 |
| Liquid hold-up | <3%, Stratified Wavy | 5–10%, Stratified Wavy |
| Uninhibited corrosion (mm/y) | **11–19** | **11–17** |
| CO₂ (mol%) | 30–57 | 30–61 |
| pH | 4.5–7.5 | 4.5–7.5 |
| TDS (mg/L) | 21,000–26,000 | ~32,000 |
| Acetic acid (ppm) | 0–1,000 | 0–1,000 |
| GLR (MMscf/bbl) | 0.10–0.12 | 0.04–0.05 |
| Water Cut | 56–83% | 20–65% |
| Corrosion type | TOL and BOL | TOL and BOL |

### Fluid Properties (Topsides)
- Pour point temperature: 29°C (dead oil)
- Wax Appearance Temperature (WAT): 39°C (dead oil)
- Scale inhibitor dosing (current design): 40 ppmv based on total liquid rate (injection: downstream of choke valve)
- PPD/Wax Inhibitor dosing (current design): 300 ppmv based on oil rate (injection: downstream of choke valve)

### Action Items from Meeting
- **TOL impact on Pipeline:** NIPI/Thế Anh to perform corrosion risk assessment based on FA steady state results (due 05-May-2026)
- **TOL impact on Topsides — two options under study:**
  - Option 1: V-Jet Pig to carry CI to top of pipe wall
  - Option 2: CI injection rate increase (PTSC to study consumption, layout, and associated topside impact)
- **Baker Hughes actions (due 08-May-2026):** MSDS for CI chemical, lab/field test report (TOL + BOL), recommended dosing rate and efficiency, slug pig vs V-Jet pig recommendation, effective film duration and max effective pipeline length
- **Key technical question:** Can one CI chemical handle both TOL and BOL? Or two separate products needed?
- **Timeline constraint:** ~1–1.5 months to finalize corrosion treatment concept (tight)
