## Summary

Confirmed scope change for both platforms: DD-WHP production well relocated from slot D3 to slot D1 (CPY/PVEP-KM initiated 20-May-2026). KM-WHP conductor tags swapped between slots D2 and D3, with associated well names and W-numbers changed. All engineering disciplines must update design documents, 3D models, and MTOs. Change Order basis established.

## Key Points

### DD-WHP Well Relocation (D3 → D1)
- **Change:** Production well at slot D3 relocated to slot D1; cushion well placed at D3
- **Initiated by:** CPY (PVEP-KM) on 20-May-2026
- **Impact:** All DD-WHP engineering deliverables referencing slot D3 production well must be updated — P&IDs, 3D model, Structural drawings (conductor framing), Instrument documents, piping MTO

### KM-WHP Conductor Tag Swap (D2 ↔ D3)
- **Change:** Conductors at slots D2 and D3 have their well designations swapped:
  - Slot D2: W-1010 / W-1015 (KM-1P / KM-2P) **swapped to** W-1020 / W-1025 (KM-3P / KM-4P)
  - Slot D3: W-1020 / W-1025 (KM-3P / KM-4P) **swapped to** W-1010 / W-1015 (KM-1P / KM-2P)
- **SIPROD P&ID note:** KM-3P/4P wells not installed simultaneously with KM-1P/2P; P&ID split required (already flagged in Process MOM-009)
- **Impact:** All KM-WHP documents that tag individual conductors/wells by slot designation or well number must be updated

### Action Required (All Disciplines)
- Update design documents (P&IDs, datasheets, drawings)
- Update 3D models and piping MTO
- Update Structural conductor framing drawings
- Update Instrument tag lists and loop drawings
- CPY to issue formal Memorandum; PTSC to propose implementation via TQR
- **Change Order:** Additional conductor installation scope creates Change Order basis for additional EPCI scope
