## Summary

For the ELE-001 Solar Hybrid Wind Turbine Package, PTSC M&C (Nguyễn Văn Tây) confirmed on 13-Jun-2026 that the solar panel array support structure will be handled under Option 2: the bidder designs the support structure and provides MTO and drawings; PTSC M&C fabricates the support frame and procures materials based on the bidder's GA/MTO. This is the base scope.

## Key Points

- **Package:** ELE-001 — Solar Hybrid Wind Turbine Power System (for both KM-WHP and DD-WHP)
- **Solar panel support scope decision — Option 2 is base scope:**
  - Bidder designs the support structure, provides MTO and fabrication drawings to PTSC
  - PTSC M&C fabricates the support frame and procures materials
  - Solar panel skid itself is vendor-supplied
- **Options that were considered:**
  - Option 1: Full skid (solar panels + structure all from vendor) — risk of lengthy delivery timeline
  - Option 2 (selected): Vendor provides design only; PTSC fabricates — allows parallel procurement
  - Option 3: PMC/DE designs the support, vendor provides panels — no vendor design input
- **Rationale for Option 2:** Balances vendor knowledge for support design with PTSC's fabrication capability; reduces dependency on vendor supply chain schedule
- **Key contact:** Nguyễn Văn Tây (Technical Manager), confirming as of 13-Jun-2026
