## Summary

KMDD-MEC-002 Pig Launcher & Receiver package is in the final cost impact finalization phase as of 04-Jun-2026. The sole bidder progressing to award is PVD Trading and Technical Services JSC (PVDT/PVD Tech). The TBC thread spans Mar–Jun 2026 with 17+ rounds of clarification. The outstanding issue at EOD Jun 4 is the Teflon gasket location on the JEONJIN QOC Clamp Ring not shown on manufacturer drawing.
