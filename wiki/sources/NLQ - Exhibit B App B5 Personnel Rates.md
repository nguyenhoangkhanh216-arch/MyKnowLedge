## Summary
Appendix B5 (with sub-tables B5.1, B5.2, B5.3) defines personnel rates applicable to Change Orders under NLQ EPCIC 10. Three tables cover project office personnel (hourly), worksite personnel (daily non-SITE), and offshore SITE personnel (daily). All rates are in USD/hour or USD/day, blank for TENDERER to fill.

## Key Points
- B5.1 Table: Project Office Personnel — hourly rates for ~41 categories; separates Expatriate vs Local; Within Qatar vs Contractor's Country — App B5.1
- Categories include: Project Manager, Engineering Manager, Lead/Senior Discipline Engineers for all disciplines (Arch, HVAC, Structural, PVV, Mechanical, Electrical, Instrumentation, ICSS, Process, Safety, Corrosion), CAD/PDMS operators, Cost Controller, Scheduler, Document Control — App B5.1
- B5.2 Table: Daily rates for additional WORK performed on WORKSITES (other than SITE) — covers yard construction foremen, craft trades — App B5.2
- B5.3 Table: Daily rates for additional WORK performed on SITE (offshore) — includes Offshore Manager, Field Engineer, Piping Lead, Structural Lead, HVAC Lead, E&I/Telecom Lead, HSE Supervisor — App B5.3
- "Senior" = >10 years experience; "Lead" = managerial or discipline focal point — App B5, note
- Mob/demob lump sums are also listed per personnel category per App B5 — Exhibit B, §3.2.1.1
