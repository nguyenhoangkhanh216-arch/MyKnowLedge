## Summary

Exhibit A Rev A is the primary Scope of Work for the NLQ EPCIC 10 contract (Al Shaheen Oil Field, Qatar). It defines 12 Work Packages covering Management (WP1), HSE (WP2), Engineering (WP3), Procurement (WP4), Onshore Construction (WP5), T&I (WP6), Site Construction (WP7), Commissioning (WP8), Brownfield Integration (WP9), Startup Assistance (WP10), Final Documentation (WP11), and Options (WP12). The facility is one NLQ topside (BI), one substructure, one bridge (BI-BJ) with 25-year design life. CONTRACTOR holds full EPCIC responsibility.

## Key Points

- Design life of the PLANT is 25 years — Exhibit A, §4.1
- PLANT comprises: 1 NLQ topside (BI), 1 substructure, 1 bridge (BI-BJ), and brownfield integration works to existing BJ platform — Exhibit A, §3.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform full EPCIC: detailed engineering, procurement, construction, onshore pre-commissioning and commissioning, load-out, sea-fastening, T&I, hook-up, offshore commissioning — Exhibit A, §3.3
- SIMOPS dossier shall be APPROVED by COMPANY at least 2 months before beginning of offshore campaign — Exhibit A, §4.6.2
- All MARINE SPREAD shall be ISM certified, IACS classed, registered in OVID/OCIMF database; submitted for inspection at least 4 months before mobilisation — Exhibit A, §4.4
- CONTRACTOR shall operate a logistics base and WORKSITE in the State of Qatar — Exhibit A, §4.5
- Pile hammering shall not be conducted June–September to avoid Whale Shark Peak Aggregation season — Exhibit A, §4.6.1 / §2.3 App A1
- WP1: CONTRACTOR shall provide all project management, administration, cost control, planning, reporting, document control throughout CONTRACT — Exhibit A, §7
- WP2: CONTRACTOR shall submit an HSE safety plan with all activities defined; perform minimum 2 safety audits at WORKSITE, 1 per MARINE SPREAD vessel — Exhibit A, §8
- WP3: Engineering shall comply with DESIGN DOSSIER (Exhibit E), good industry practice, recognised codes; all design to be AFC status — Exhibit A, §9.1
- WP3: CONTRACTOR shall organise OPERCOM training sessions for pre-commissioning teams; all commissioning procedures ready 3 months before start — Exhibit A, §9.8, §9.9
- WP4: CONTRACTOR shall prepare all MTO, requisitions, TBE, and VDRL; all VENDOR documents submitted to COMPANY per Exhibit G — Exhibit A, §10
- WP5: Construction shall use OPERCOM methodology per SD-NOC-EC-600, 601, 602; ICAPS software mandatory for progress measurement — Exhibit A, §11.7
- WP6: Final T&I documentation to be MWS- and COMPANY-approved at least 4 weeks prior to planned installation date — Exhibit A, §9.10.2
- WP8: All commissioning procedures based on OPERCOM methodology per SD-NOC-EC-600, SD-NOC-EC-601, SD-NOC-EC-603; ICAPS mandatory — Exhibit A, §9.9
- WP12 Options: Option 1 — NLQ at A-Location; Option 2 — Logistics/Transportation for site construction; Option 3 — Offshore Accommodation Vessel — Exhibit A, §18
