## Summary

Exhibit A Rev C is the primary Scope of Work for the NLQ EPCIC 10 contract (Al Shaheen Oil Field, Qatar). It defines 12 Work Packages covering Management (WP1), HSE (WP2), Engineering (WP3), Procurement (WP4), Onshore Construction (WP5), T&I (WP6), Site Construction (WP7), Commissioning (WP8), Brownfield Integration (WP9), Startup Assistance (WP10), Final Documentation (WP11), and Options (WP12). The facility is one NLQ topside (BI), one substructure, one bridge (BI-BJ) with 25-year design life. CONTRACTOR holds full EPCIC responsibility. Rev C adds new §4.10 (Marine Classification), §4.11 (Applicable Standards), Option 4 (Helifuel System), Option 5 (Drinkable Water), and revises the SIMOPS dossier responsibility.

## Key Points

- Design life of the PLANT is 25 years — Exhibit A Rev C, §4.1
- PLANT comprises: 1 NLQ topside (BI), 1 substructure, 1 bridge (BI-BJ), and brownfield integration works to existing BJ platform — Exhibit A Rev C, §3.2
- CONTRACTOR shall perform full EPCIC: detailed engineering, procurement, construction, onshore pre-commissioning and commissioning, load-out, sea-fastening, T&I, hook-up, offshore commissioning — Exhibit A Rev C, §3.3
- **Rev C change — SIMOPS dossier:** The SIMOPS dossier is compiled by COMPANY; CONTRACTOR provides relevant input and approved documents at least 6 months prior to commencement of offshore works (Rev A stated CONTRACTOR approval 2 months prior) — Exhibit A Rev C, §4.6.2
- All MARINE SPREAD shall be ISM certified, IACS classed, registered in OVID/OCIMF database; submitted for inspection at least 4 months before mobilisation — Exhibit A Rev C, §4.4
- MARINE SPREAD above 20 years old before end of CONTRACT subject to extensive evaluation and may be rejected — Exhibit A Rev C, §4.4
- CONTRACTOR shall operate a logistics base and WORKSITE in the State of Qatar — Exhibit A Rev C, §4.5
- Whale shark aggregation period 1-Jun to 30-Sep impacts piling and all noisy activities; noise mitigation system required — Exhibit A Rev C, §4.6.1
- **Rev C new §4.10 — Marine Classification:** In conflict between COMPANY and Classification Society/Flag State/Qatari Authorities, most stringent applies; CONTRACTOR notifies COMPANY promptly — Exhibit A Rev C, §4.10
- **Rev C new §4.11 — Applicable Standards:** CONTRACTOR designs per FSS and Safety Concept (international standards); all other activities per EXHIBIT E Appendix E3 (NOC Company Standards) — Exhibit A Rev C, §4.11
- WP1: CONTRACTOR shall provide all project management, administration, cost control, planning, reporting, document control throughout CONTRACT — Exhibit A Rev C, §7
- WP2: CONTRACTOR shall submit an HSE safety plan; perform minimum 2 safety audits at WORKSITE, 1 per MARINE SPREAD vessel — Exhibit A Rev C, §8
- WP3: Engineering shall comply with DESIGN DOSSIER (Exhibit E), good industry practice, recognised codes; all design to be AFC status — Exhibit A Rev C, §9.1
- WP3: CONTRACTOR shall organise OPERCOM training sessions for pre-commissioning teams; all commissioning procedures ready 3 months before start — Exhibit A Rev C, §9.8, §9.9
- WP4: CONTRACTOR shall prepare all MTO, requisitions, TBE, and VDRL; vendor KOM within 15 days after Contract Award — Exhibit A Rev C, §10
- WP5: Construction shall use OPERCOM methodology per SD-NOC-EC-600, 601, 602; ICAPS software mandatory for progress measurement — Exhibit A Rev C, §11.7
- WP6: Final T&I documentation to be MWS- and COMPANY-approved at least 4 weeks prior to planned installation date — Exhibit A Rev C, §9.10.2
- WP8: All commissioning procedures based on OPERCOM methodology per SD-NOC-EC-600, SD-NOC-EC-601, SD-NOC-EC-603; ICAPS mandatory — Exhibit A Rev C, §9.9
- WP12 Option 1 — NLQ at A-Location; Option 2 — Logistics/Transportation for site construction; Option 3 — Offshore Accommodation Vessel — Exhibit A Rev C, §18.1–18.3
- **Rev C new Option 4 — Helifuel System:** Helicopter refuelling and fuel storage system; Helifuel Storage Package (2×100% transfer pumps, 2×2900-litre portable fuel tanks, deluge system) + Helifuel Dispenser Package (filter water separator, flowmeter, hose reel, bonding reel, 5×2900L portable fuel pods) — Exhibit A Rev C, §18.4
- **Rev C new Option 5 — Drinkable Water:** CONTRACTOR to assess Portable Water Maker (RO + UV + mineral) vs Piping & Tap System (full purification network); per NLQ FSS §18.2 — Exhibit A Rev C, §18.5
- App A10 Interface Management marked NOT APPLICABLE in Rev C — Exhibit A Rev C, §19
