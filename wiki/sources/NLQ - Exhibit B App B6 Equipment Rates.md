## Summary
Appendix B6 Rev A provides daily rates for construction equipment (including marine spread standby) applicable to Change Orders under NLQ EPCIC 10. Covers vehicles, generators, air compressors, cranes (crawler/truck/tower/hydraulic/gantry), forklifts, sandblasting/painting equipment. All rates in USD/Day.

## Key Points
- Covers WORKSHOP/WORKSITE rates and SITE (offshore) rates in separate columns — App B6
- Equipment categories: automobiles/trucks/minibus/pickup/flat-bed/kenworth; generators up to >300KVA; air compressors; crawler cranes up to >45mt; truck cranes; tower cranes; hydraulic cranes; gantry cranes up to >20mt; forklifts up to >15mt; sandblasting/painting/coating equipment — App B6
- Marine Spread standby rates also defined here per Exhibit B §3.3 — Exhibit B, §3.3
- Equipment rates include all PERSONNEL required for that equipment (except where explicitly stated as exclusive of personnel) — Exhibit B, §3.2.2
- No compensation for downtime, maintenance, repair, absence of operators — Exhibit B, §3.2, (iii)
