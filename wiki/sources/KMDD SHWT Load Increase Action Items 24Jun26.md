## Summary

Following the 22-Jun-2026 meeting between PVEP-KM, VSP (OCD+MED), and PMC on the SHWT (Solar Hybrid Wind Turbine) load increase, 9 action items were issued. Total platform 24VDC load is now 112,499.01 Wh/day — a +33% increase vs FEED — not including any additional 15% design margin. PMC proposed dropping the 15% margin. Several CPY decisions were still pending as of the 24-Jun email.

## Key Points

- **Meeting date:** 22-Jun-2026 (PVEP-KM, VSP OCD+MED, PMC)
- **Total 24VDC load:** 112,499.01 Wh/day (+33% vs FEED baseline)
- **15% margin status:** PMC proposes removing the 15% additional design margin (rationale: ICSS has 10%; CI Package uses highest bidder power figure)
- **Load list finalization deadline:** 25-Jun-2026 — PMC to finalize and send to SHWT bidders

### 9 Action items from 22-Jun meeting:

- **Action 4 (CPY to advise by 24-Jun):** Network backup BRA (Battery Rack Assembly) — include or not
- **Action 6 (CPY to advise by 24-Jun):** Confirm dropping the 15% margin
- **Action 8:** Check whether solar panels >380W require Ex (explosion-proof) certification
- **Action 9:** Vendor to confirm wind turbine metocean data compatibility

- **Chemical Injection Package impact:** 11% of total SHWT load growth attributed to CI package (confirmed per 20-Jun email context)
- **IRCD loads:** PPD pump IRCD must be remote (24VDC) per new P&IDs — not yet in load list
