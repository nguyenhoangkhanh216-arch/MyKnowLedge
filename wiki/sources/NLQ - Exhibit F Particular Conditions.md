## Summary

Exhibit F Rev A (4 pages) is the CONTRACTOR's declaration of Particular Conditions for the Performance of the Work. It is a TENDERER-completed document describing planned engineering offices, worksite facilities, marine spread vessels, and construction equipment. All fields are blank in the ITB version — TENDERER fills them in as part of the technical proposal submission.

## Key Points

- **Section 1 – Engineering Offices**: CONTRACTOR identifies engineering subcontractor (if any), engineering office location, and management office location — Exhibit F, §1
- **Section 2 – Worksite Facilities**: CONTRACTOR identifies plant/mill/fabrication facilities, test facilities/laboratories, storage area, and logistic base for offshore support — Exhibit F, §2
- **Section 3 – Marine Spread**: CONTRACTOR commits to 6 vessel categories: Transportation Spread (F3.1), Installation Spread (F3.2), Pre-commissioning/commissioning support vessel (F3.3), Survey vessel (F3.4), Crew boats (F3.5), any other vessels (F3.6) — Exhibit F, §3
- **Section 4 – Construction Equipment**: CONTRACTOR lists all construction equipment for WORKSITE and SITE — Exhibit F, §4
- All minimum facilities deemed included in the Initial CONTRACT PRICE; CONTRACTOR responsible for upgrading as needed — Exhibit F, §1, §2
- Marine Spread listing in Exhibit F does not constitute COMPANY acceptance — CONTRACTOR remains responsible for providing adequate equipment based on installation studies — Exhibit F, §3
