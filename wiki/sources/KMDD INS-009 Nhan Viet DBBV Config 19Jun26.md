## Summary
Nhan Viet ICS (McCrometer V-Cone bidder) provided SGS recommendation for PVT sampling and tracer injection configuration on 14-Jun; PTSC instrument engineer followed up 19-Jun with two specific questions on DBBV suitability for tracer injection at vertical 3-o'clock and sampling quill isolation.

## Key Points
- SGS recommendation (via Kate Nguyen, Nhan Viet ICS): DBBV for tracer injection OK at 3-o'clock vertical orientation; no sampling quill needed for DBBV sampling point
- DBBV sampling point requires: 1" NPT full-bore minimum 14mm internal diameter; position at 12-o'clock (top of horizontal pipe); or 6-o'clock (bottom) if no sand production — then retractable probe not required
- Current design has: 10mm full-bore DBBV with 1/2" NPT(F) connection — SGS confirmed this is NOT suitable for retractable probe (needs 14mm min)
- PTSC's Nguyễn Thanh Sơn raised follow-up questions 19-Jun: (1) suitability of 3-o'clock DBBV for tracer injection; (2) how pressure can be safely isolated during installation of retractable probe if no sampling quill
- V-Cone wet gas calibration: McCrometer confirmed factory calibration by water + wet gas correction is sufficient; 3rd party wet lab calibration NOT required for wet gas; CEESI 8" facility response indicated for 10" flowmeter
- PVT recommended for both well test and allocation flowmeters at 1st round before handover; frequency to be determined by end-user
