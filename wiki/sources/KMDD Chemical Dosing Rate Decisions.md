## Summary

Meeting on 13-May-2026 finalized design basis for all four chemical injection systems on KM-WHP and DD-WHP. PPD dosing rate was increased from FEED, Scale Inhibitor injection point moved upstream of choke, Corrosion Inhibitor optional 130 L/d at DD-WHP requested for TBE, and Methanol pump capacity massively increased (10× FEED values). Two TQs issued to COMPANY for formal confirmation. CI skid layout dispute still open.

## Key Points

### Chemical Dosing Rates Decided (13-May-2026 meeting):

| Chemical | FEED Design | DE Decision | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| PPD Wax Inhibitor | 300 ppmv (oil rate) | **500 ppmv** (oil rate) | Baker Hughes recommended 300–500; PTSC chose conservative 500 |
| Scale Inhibitor (SI) | 40 ppmv (total liquid, d/s choke) | **40 ppmv** (total liquid, **u/s choke**) | Rate kept; injection point moved upstream for better distribution |
| Corrosion Inhibitor (CI) | Base case: 50 L/d @ DD-WHP | Base: 50 L/d; **Optional: 130 L/d** | 130 L/d covers commingled KM+DD flow; optional case for TBE |
| Methanol | KM: 50 L/h; DD: 31.3 L/h | **KM: 507.8 L/h (30% start-up); DD: 560.8 L/h (50% start-up)** | Major change — ~10× increase |

### Go-by reference comparison (PMC study Apr-2026):
- PPD in go-by projects ranged 100–1000 ppmv; FEED 300 ppmv was "reasonable"
- Baker Hughes (PAO82004): recommended 300–500 ppmv
- ROMEX (RX-7025M): typical 200 ppm
- SI in go-by projects ranged 10–75 ppmv; FEED 40 ppmv was "reasonable"
- Baker Hughes (SRW82510): recommended 25 ppmv continuous, upstream of choke

### TQs issued (21-May-2026):
- KDP-OOO-03-TQ-B-0003-(P)-D0: PPD & SI injection system finalized design basis
- KDP-OOO-03-TQ-B-0004-(P)-D0: Methanol injection rate — KM 507.8 L/h, DD 560.8 L/h

### Open dispute — CI Skid Layout:
- PTSC relocated CI skid from original FEED position due to layout constraints
- PVEP-KM wants reverting to original FEED position
- PTSC position: reverting is a major change at this stage with significant schedule impact
- Status as of 14-May-2026: still being discussed
