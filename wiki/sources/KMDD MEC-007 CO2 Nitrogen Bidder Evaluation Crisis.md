## Summary
Võ Anh Kiệt's internal assessment of MEC-007 bidder status shows only 1 out of 5 bidders (TTE) is technically acceptable. The remaining four — DKE, FFE, PPL, Mermaid — all rated "Not Acceptable" due to missing critical documents and unresolved technical questions. TBC#02 Rev.A sent to TTE and FFE on Jun 3.

## Key Points
- **Overall result: 1/5 bidders acceptable** — TTE only.
- **DKE**: No response to TBC#1 Rev.A; has not confirmed Control Panel logic for CO2 snuffing, has not confirmed bottle quantities, no PCV sizing calc, no cert for bottles.
- **FFE**: BCM held 22-May; still missing Mark-up Datasheet, ITP, VDRL, Form T5, sub-contractor list, reference list, scope of supply markup, KTR markup, CO2+N2 calculation and drawing, PCV sizing calc; has not confirmed bottle quantities.
- **PPL**: Missing KTR, experience list, CO2/N2 catalogue & drawing, PCV calc; no response to TBC#1 RevA; no bottle cert.
- **TTE**: Acceptable — needs PCV calc clarification and bottle cert; TBC#1 RevC under review.
- **Mermaid**: No response to TBC#1 Rev.B; missing almost all docs; non-compliant with INS and PIP TBC; no bottle numbers confirmed.
- **Action taken Jun 3**: TBC#02 Rev.A sent to TTE and FFE; commercial team (Ngân) pushing to improve bidder quality.
