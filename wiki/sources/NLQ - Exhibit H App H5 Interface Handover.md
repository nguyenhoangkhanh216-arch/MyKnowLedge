## Summary

Appendix H5 Rev A (15 pages) defines the Interface Items Handover process for material and equipment transferred between contractors or from contractor to COMPANY In-Country Warehouse during NLQ EPCIC 10 execution. Three handover scenarios apply: Contractor-to-Contractor, LLI Supplier-to-Contractor (Not Applicable), and Contractor-to-COMPANY In-Country Warehouse.

## Key Points

### Interface Item Handover — Contractor to Contractor
- Supplying CONTRACTOR issues preliminary Interface Items delivery list at ED+2 months; updated monthly in Monthly Report — Exhibit H App H5, §3.1.1
- FAT/SIT/inspection before shipment: CIM of Supplying party invites Receiving CONTRACTOR 2 weeks in advance via Interface Management Software — Exhibit H App H5, §3.1.2
- Handover at INCOTERM location: physical inspection by all three parties (Supplying CONTRACTOR, COMPANY, Receiving CONTRACTOR) to check quantities and visible damage — Exhibit H App H5, §3.1.3
- Unique Handover Certificate signed by all three parties; Outstanding Work List (OWL) agreed and maintained by Supplying CONTRACTOR until all points cleared — Exhibit H App H5, §3.1.3
- LLI Supplier to Receiving CONTRACTOR (non-novated items): NOT APPLICABLE for NLQ — Exhibit H App H5, §4

### Interface Item Handover — Contractor to COMPANY In-Country Warehouse
- Two item categories: Category A (tagged items or items that may be damaged in transport, e.g., equipment); Category B (untagged, routine transport-safe items e.g., bolts, gaskets, flanges, pipes, cables) — Exhibit H App H5, §5.1.2
- Category A release inspection: at CONTRACTOR's In-Country Warehouse after trans-oceanic transport; 15 calendar days notice to COMPANY — Exhibit H App H5, §5.1.5
- Category B release inspection: before shipment from CONTRACTOR's WORKSITE during packing; 2 working days notice to COMPANY — Exhibit H App H5, §5.1.5
- Dispatch Dossier: Supplying CONTRACTOR creates per item/group using Unique Identification Number; compiled against COMPANY SAP "QC Codes"; COMPANY must APPROVE Dispatch Dossier before Release Inspection can be notified — Exhibit H App H5, §5.1.4
- IRN (Inspection and Release Note) issued by COMPANY after satisfactory Dispatch Dossier review and Release Inspection; IRN is prerequisite for transfer to COMPANY In-Country Warehouse — Exhibit H App H5, §5.1.5
- Items directly shipped from VENDOR to Qatar cannot be treated as Category B Items — Exhibit H App H5, §5.1.2

### Document Numbering
- Dispatch Dossier, Outstanding Work List, and Handover Certificate numbered per SD-NOC-MP-701: PPPPP-CCCCC-OOOO-RRRR-CRT-NNNNNN format — Exhibit H App H5, §6
- Reference documents: SD-NOC-MP-710 (Interface Management Plan), SD-NOC-MP-711 (Contractor Interface Management Specification) — Exhibit H App H5, §1
