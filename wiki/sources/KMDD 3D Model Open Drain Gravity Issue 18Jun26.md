## Summary
Bùi Lê Lương (PTSC Mechanical) raised a layout conflict found during 3D model review: Open Drain Tank has been placed on the same deck as the Closed Drain Vessel, making gravity drain from the CD Vessel to the OD Tank impossible. PMC is clarifying whether gravity drain is even required, since liquid can be transferred by the CD Transfer Pump.

## Key Points
- Issue: Open Drain Tank placed same deck as Closed Drain Vessel → gravity drain from CD Vessel to OD Tank is impossible
- Raised in MEC weekly meeting 18-Jun by Bùi Lê Lương
- PMC (CPY) is clarifying whether gravity drain to OD Tank is necessary — liquid transfer can be performed by Closed Drain Transfer Pump instead
- If gravity drain not required, pump-only transfer is the fallback; if required, deck relocation needed
- Contact: Bùi Lê Lương → Trương Minh Hoàng for technical advice
- Context: Part of 60% 3D model preparation ahead of 06-Jul-2026 60% Model Review
