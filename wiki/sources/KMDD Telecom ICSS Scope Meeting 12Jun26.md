## Summary

A meeting was held 12-Jun-2026 between PMC (Nguyễn Văn Tây) and VSP (Võ Việt Hải, Engineering Manager) to align on the Telecom/ICSS scope dispute that originated 11-Jun-2026. Three agenda items were addressed: Telecom/ICSS scope split, Engineering Execution Plan, and Ship Collision — with the Ship Collision item deferred to a separate email.

## Key Points

- **Topic A1 — Brownfield Telecom Synchronization:** Technically, the Telecom system for KM-WHP & DD-WHP (Greenfield) and BRA (Brownfield) must be designed as one synchronized interface — PMC's position is that this means synchronized interface design only, NOT a full telecom system for both Greenfield and Brownfield
- **Topic A2 — FEED→DE SoW change for HML/PVEP-KM Office equipment:**
  - FEED Block Diagram: OPC server at HML office, Display/OWS/FW at PVEP-KM office → classified as Telecom Package under PMC procurement
  - DE design: these items moved to ICSS (VSP procurement) for System Engineering convenience
  - PMC position: per contract, PMC scope is Package A (Greenfield) only; Brownfield package (Package B) is VSP scope — so the shift from Telecom to ICSS stays within VSP scope, no PMC cost impact
- **Joint resolution proposed to PMTs:** Align on SoW/SoS between VSP and PMC procurement teams for the FEED→DE transition; implementation to follow DE design technically
- **Ship Collision:** To be addressed in a separate email (agreed by both sides)
- **EEP (Engineering Execution Plan):** Minor edits aligned directly between parties
- **Meeting recording:** Shared via SharePoint by Tây to Hải on 13-Jun-2026
