## Summary
Vietsovpetro's Offshore Construction Division (XNCĐ) was selected as the vendor for ICSS and WHCP&HPU packages. XNCĐ submitted WHCP design documents (P&ID, Hydraulic Calculation, GA drawings) to PMC on 28-29 May 2026, but no review feedback has been received as of Jun 3. A separate bottleneck blocks vendor data submission: XNCĐ has not received the document code template or DCC contact from PTSC (XNXL).

## Key Points
- **Arrangement**: VSP selected their own subsidiary XNCĐ as vendor for ICSS and WHCP; agreed in 4-party meeting (PVEP-KM, PMC, XNXL, XNCĐ) 22-May-2026.
- **WHCP documents submitted**: 28-29 May 2026 — P&ID, Hydraulic Calc, GA + 3D model (Naviswork) for KM-WHP and DD-WHP both.
- **Blocked**: PVEP-KM hasn't sent CRS comment sheet. Reason: PVEP-KM never received documents via official transmittal (though they had their comments ready).
- **Document numbering gap**: XNCĐ still waiting for: (1) Document code (Doc. Code) for ICSS and WHCP packages; (2) Cover template for vendor docs; (3) DCC contact at XNXL.
- **Without these**: XNCĐ cannot submit vendor data formally; detailed design cannot advance.
- **Escalation**: Đào Đức Quang escalated to PM Đồng Văn Nhường and Engineering Lead Võ Việt Hải. PM confirmed coordinating with PVEP-KM PM Hưng to push for comments.
- **DCC of XNCĐ**: Lương Thị Vân Hà (haltv.me@vietsov.com.vn).
- **Forwarded to Khánh (user)** by VSP's Nguyễn Tuấn Cường for FYI — PTSC's Lương (Ngô Văn Lương) handling VDS workflow.
