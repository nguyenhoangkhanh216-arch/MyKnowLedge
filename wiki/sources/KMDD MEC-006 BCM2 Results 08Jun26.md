## Summary

BCM#2 for KMDD-MEC-006 Chemical Injection Package was held on 08-Jun-2026 (15:11 Vietnam time) with both bidders POS and DKE. CPY requested combining the Chemical Injection Skid (CIS) and Methanol Injection Skid (MeOH) into a single common skid. Both bidders confirmed feasibility but requested more time for layout, maintenance space, CoG, lifting, and GAD drawing. Both confirmed Triplex methanol pump. Deadline for combined response: Friday 12-Jun-2026.

## Key Points

**BCM#2 participants:**
- PTSC: Nguyen Su Tran Hoang (Mechanical Engineer); Tran Thi Khanh Ngan (Commercial)
- POS: Tran Duc Dung (Business Development Deputy Team Leader)
- DKE: separate thread
- CPY/PVEP-KM: Nguyen Khac Hung (requested combined skid)

**Key BCM#2 outcomes:**
1. **Combined CIS + MeOH skid**: Both POS and DKE confirmed feasible — will integrate Chemical Injection Skid and Methanol Injection Skid into a single common skid
2. **Additional time required**: Both bidders need time to complete layout drawing, maintenance access, CoG calculation, lifting analysis, GAD (General Arrangement Drawing)
3. **Methanol pump**: Both confirmed **Triplex** type (not Simplex as originally bid) — aligns with Chemical Dosing Rate finalization
4. **Electrical load re-check**: Both to recheck other chemical pump electrical loads (consistent with 24VDC demand issue)
5. **Delivery**: 42 weeks DDP; potential to accelerate if vendor data is approved faster

**Response deadline:**
- **Both POS and DKE to respond by Friday 12-Jun-2026** on:
  - All outstanding TBC items
  - Combined skid design (layout, CoG, GAD)
  - Updated cost impact

**Why CPY wants a combined skid:**

- Operational simplicity (single skid for all fluid injection)
