## Summary

Hoàng (Mechanical, hoangnst) raised a concern to Quân (Process) on 24-Jun: the optional PPD/Wax injection case (upstream of choke) requires pump design pressure of 265 barg, which would force a pump model change and +0.37–0.55 kW motor increase versus the base case methanol pump at 232 barg. Lương (Mechanical lead) instructed Hoàng not to send this to bidders yet — the optional upstream-choke case will only be evaluated after TBC#3 responses and GA drawings are received, to assess tank volume for the layout-constrained skid.

## Key Points

- **Issue raised 24-Jun (Hoàng → Quân):** PPD/Wax pumps P-5325 A/B and P-5365 A/B — if design pressure raised to 265 barg (for upstream-of-choke injection), pump model changes required
- **Motor power impact:** +0.37–0.55 kW increase
- **Hoàng's proposal:** Reduce requirement to 240 barg instead of 265 barg
- **Lương's instruction (11-Jun context):** Do NOT send to bidders yet — avoid confusing bidders before TBC#3 deadline. Optional PPD u/s choke case is not urgent (only an option, not base case)
- **Context (from 09-Jun meeting):**
  - Base case: PPD inject **downstream of choke** at 500 ppmv
  - Optional case: PPD inject **upstream of choke** — requires higher pump design pressure
  - Post-TBC#3: PTSC will evaluate bidder GA drawings and tank volume for the optional layout case, then issue TBC for the option
- **Electrical load list note:** Upstream-of-choke case load only considered for future expansion if required
