## Summary
Exhibit J Rev B contains the Company-approved subcontractors and vendors list with qualification criteria, new vendor onboarding process (18-item due diligence questionnaire + 23-item dossier), and Attachment J2 listing standardized equipment with mandatory vendors. Any deviation from listed vendors requires COMPANY APPROVAL with no guaranteed timeline.

## Key Points
- **Standardized equipment** (Attachment J2) — vendor is mandatory, cost deemed included in lump sum regardless of COMPANY selection: RFID Lockers = EMKA/Nordiqa (Qatar); Vibration Monitoring = Bently Nevada (USA); RFID Tags = HID Global (Germany); PDC/PLC = ABB (India); General Telecom Integrator = SEMCO (Singapore); **ICSS = Yokogawa (Qatar)** — Exhibit J Att. J2.
- Vendors labelled "Qualified" or "Qualified with Reserves": CONTRACTOR cannot use Vendor Name + Location + Category combination differently — any change = New Vendor — Exhibit J §1.
- New vendor approval: COMPANY has **no obligation and no timeline** to approve additional vendors — Exhibit J §2.
- **New vendor criteria** (minimum 18 items): 10 years experience, customer references, ISO 9001/OHSAS 18001/ISO 14001 certified, financially sound, CONTRACTOR pre-qualified, no sanctions exposure — Exhibit J §2 (i–xviii).
- **Due Diligence Questionnaire** (Att. J1) covers: ownership structure, ultimate beneficial ownership, UN/US/EU/UK sanctions compliance, anti-corruption policies, criminal record of officers, marine craft history if applicable — Exhibit J Att. J1.
- **Vendor qualification dossier** (23 items): includes profile, org chart, previous projects, fabrication capabilities, ISO certs, HSE KPIs, financial statements (3 years), anti-corruption policies — Exhibit J Att. J1 Part II.
- Technical assessment standards for new vendors include: SD-NOC-INS-706 (Instrumentation), SD-NOC-ELE-931/935/941/951/952/961 (Electrical), SD-NOC-PVV-950-956 (Pressure Vessels/Piping/Valves), SD-NOC-MEC-900-903 (Rotating Equipment) — Exhibit J Att. J1 §22.
- PVV Piping/PLR Pipeline vendors marked "2" in column: COMPANY may request additional NDT/destructive testing at no cost before material release — Exhibit J §6.
- COMPANY may require vendor audit at any time before approving new vendors — Exhibit J §4.
