## Summary

Appendix A2 (Rev B) defines the minimum document deliverables CONTRACTOR must prepare and submit to COMPANY across all project phases and disciplines for the NLQ EPCIC 10. Documents are classified Class 1 (Approval), Class 2 (Review), or Class 3 (Information). The list covers 12 discipline areas plus general project management.

## Key Points

- All CONTRACTOR documents shall be classified Class 1 (Approval), Class 2 (Review), or Class 3 (Information); CONTRACTOR shall mark each document with the applicable stamp — Exhibit A App A2, §1
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare and submit the Master Document Register (MDR) for COMPANY APPROVAL; documents shall progress to AFC and As-Built status — Exhibit A App A2, §1
- Documents originated by SUBCONTRACTORS shall be issued by CONTRACTOR to COMPANY with CONTRACTOR comments already incorporated — Exhibit A App A2, §1
