## Summary

Preliminary HVAC heat load calculation for the Instrumentation Shelter on KMDD WHPs. The shelter has 2 AC units (9,000 BTU/hr each = 5.275 kW total). FEED requirement: max 25°C room temperature. Without a ceiling the system marginally meets the requirement. COMPANY considering adding a ceiling to help with heat load; HVAC evaluated multiple ceiling height scenarios.

## Key Points

- **HVAC system:** 2 AC units × 9,000 BTU/hr = **5.275 kW** total cooling capacity
- **FEED requirement:** Max 25°C room temperature
- **Heat load scenarios:**

| Case | Occupancy | Fresh air fan | Ceiling | Room Temp | Load | Result |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Unoccupied | Off | None | 24°C | 4.5 kW | ✓ OK |
| 2 | Occupied | On | None | 28°C | 5.2 kW | ✓ Marginal |
| 3 | Occupied | On | 3.0 m | 26°C | 5.1 kW | ✓ Marginal |
| 4 | Occupied | On | 2.8 m | 24°C | 5.3 kW | ⚠ Slightly over |

- **Ceiling constraint:** Minimum ceiling height = door height = 2800 mm
- **Case 4 analysis:** 5.3 kW slightly exceeds capacity of 5.275 kW, but could be OK with 25°C setpoint tolerance
- **Proposed to CPY:** Which ceiling option to select; HVAC team waiting for COMPANY input
- **Status:** Open (decision pending) as of Mar-2026
