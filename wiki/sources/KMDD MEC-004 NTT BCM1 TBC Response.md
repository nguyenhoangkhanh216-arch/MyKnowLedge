## Summary

TBC#01 Rev.B for KMDD-MEC-004 (EODD Pump & Centrifugal Pump Non-API), with BCM#1 follow-up items included. Bidder is Nam Tien Thinh (NTT); their sub-supplier for centrifugal pumps is KZPC. NTT has excluded EODD pumps from their scope entirely, supplying only the four Non-API Centrifugal Pumps. BCM#1 closed with 5 items still open.

## Key Points

- **EODD pump excluded:** NTT/KZPC confirmed they will not supply the EODD (Open Drain) pumps (P-5110, P-5120); all EODD-related TBC items answered as "Bidder excluded EODD pump from scope of supply"
- **Centrifugal pumps supplied:** Diesel Transfer Pumps (P-5710/P-5720) and Service Water Pumps (P-6510/P-6520) — 4 units total
- **Motor upgrade confirmed:** Zone 2, IP56, Ex ec IIC T3 (was offered at Zone 1, IP55 initially — rejected)
- **Flow % BEP for Diesel Transfer Pump:** 64.6% — below 70–100% spec; deviation accepted if pump complies with process data and motor is optimized (Purchaser updated requirement in TBC)
- **Earthing bosses:** NTT offers Earth Lug instead of SS316 earthing bosses on baseplate — minor deviation
