## Summary

On 23-Jun-2026, Nguyen Minh Tuan (Process Safety Engineer, PVEP-Khanh My / CPY) formally approved the A/B train suffix convention for SAF equipment variants. The approval covers three pairs of Safety equipment where physically identical items on the same platform need to be uniquely identified: lifebuoys, safety showers/eyewash stations, and lifejackets.

## Key Points

- **Approver:** Nguyen Minh Tuan, Process Safety Engineer, PVEP-Khanh My (CPY)
- **Date:** 23-Jun-2026
- **Approval text (Vietnamese):** "Bên anh đồng ý với đề xuất bên dưới em nhé" — CPY agrees with the A/B suffix proposal
- **Approved tag variants:**

| Equipment Type | Variant A | Example Tag-A | Variant B | Example Tag-B |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Lifebuoy (RLB) | With lifeline + light + smoke signal | RLB-81-0201-A | With lifeline + light only | RLB-81-0202-B |
| Safety Shower / Eyewash (RSS) | Plumbed (permanent) safety shower + eyewash | RSS-81-0202-A | Portable eyewash station | RSS-81-0201-B |
| Lifejacket (RLJ) | 1 lifejacket on crane cabin | RLJ-81-0201-A | 15 lifejackets in cabinet | RLJ-81-0202-B |

- **Convention:** A = one equipment variant; B = the other variant on the same platform
- **Context:** Raised 19-Jun because standard single-suffix tagging could not distinguish two identical-function but different-specification items at the same location
