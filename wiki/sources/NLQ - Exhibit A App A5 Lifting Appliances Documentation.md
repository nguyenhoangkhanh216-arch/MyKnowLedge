## Summary

Appendix A5 (Rev A) specifies the minimum documentation requirements for lifting appliances (cranes and winches) that form part of CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENT. CONTRACTOR must submit these documents for COMPANY review and APPROVAL before the first inspection by a COMPANY-appointed lifting specialist.

## Key Points

### Scope (§1)
- This appendix details minimum documentation for lifting appliances as part of CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENT to be submitted for COMPANY review and APPROVAL prior to the first lifting specialist inspection — Exhibit A App A5, §1
- Must be read in conjunction with Exhibit A and Exhibit L — Exhibit A App A5, §1
- List is not exhaustive; additional requirements exist in Exhibit L and COMPANY Standards SD-NOC-STR-401, SD-NOC-STR-411, SD-NOC-INP-IM-305, SD-NOC-LH-003 — Exhibit A App A5, §2

### Minimum Requirements — For Each Crane (§2)
- Certified crane chart at working radius for each step — Exhibit A App A5, §2
- Certified last crane load test and last crane certificate — Exhibit A App A5, §2
- Crane operator certificate — Exhibit A App A5, §2
- Last crane NDT inspection report — Exhibit A App A5, §2
- Position of each wire (ID number) on the crane with installation date; all crane wire certificates; all crane wire socket certificates; last crane wire sample strength tests — Exhibit A App A5, §2
- All crane hook block certificates and NDT reports — Exhibit A App A5, §2
- All crane load cell calibration certificates — Exhibit A App A5, §2
- Last crane slew ring bearing grease analysis; rocking test; bolting torque control at 100%; bearing and bolting NDT; bearing and bolting replacement records — Exhibit A App A5, §2
- Crane maintenance record for the last year — Exhibit A App A5, §2

### Minimum Requirements — For Each Winch (§2)
- Last winch certificate; last winch load test including brake test — Exhibit A App A5, §2
- Last structure NDT inspection report — Exhibit A App A5, §2
- Last winch wire certificate; last winch wire socket certificate; last winch wire sample strength test — Exhibit A App A5, §2
- Last winch load cell calibration certificate — Exhibit A App A5, §2
- Winch maintenance record for the last year — Exhibit A App A5, §2
- Winch operators' certificates — Exhibit A App A5, §2
- Date of last inspection of lifting accessories on-board — Exhibit A App A5, §2
