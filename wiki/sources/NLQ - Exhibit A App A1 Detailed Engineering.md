## Summary

Appendix A1 Rev B is the 34-page detailed engineering scope within Exhibit A, covering all discipline-specific requirements for the NLQ BI Living Quarter platform. It defines CONTRACTOR's engineering obligations across 12 disciplines (Safety, Process, Mechanical, HVAC, Piping/Layout, Structural, Electrical, Instrumentation/ICSS, Corrosion, Architectural, Hook-Up) and mandates an approved software suite (AVEVA E3D, ETAP, CAESAR 2, PIPENET, PHAST, ICAPS, etc.). All deliverables must be AFC-approved and managed in AVEVA databases with live COMPANY access.

## Key Points

- CONTRACTOR shall perform all detailed engineering necessary for procurement, pre-fabrication, construction, load-out, sea-fastening, T&I, pre-commissioning and commissioning of the PLANT — Exhibit A App A1, §2.1
- CONTRACTOR shall develop a complete project database from AVEVA solution and ensure correct licenses and multi-user access for all disciplines — Exhibit A App A1, §2.1
- CONTRACTOR shall further develop and complete the tagging of equipment, valves, instruments, cables, monorails per SD-NOC-EC-106 — Exhibit A App A1, §2.1
- Safety: CONTRACTOR shall conduct HAZID/ENVID, HAZOP, and all applicable safety studies at early stage of Detailed Engineering — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- Safety: CONTRACTOR shall develop an Engineering Safety Plan to ensure key safety reviews, studies, calculations, and deliverables are reflected in the project schedule — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- Safety: CONTRACTOR shall perform Fire Risk Analysis using PHAST software for consequence modelling per DESIGN DOSSIER — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- Safety: CONTRACTOR shall perform QRA, EERA, Ship Collision Study, Natural Ventilation Study, Noise and Vibration Studies, CFD Smoke Dispersion Study as minimum required safety studies — Exhibit A App A1, §2.2
- Process: CONTRACTOR shall maintain and update Project Design Basis, develop PFDs, P&IDs, Utility Balances, Equipment Sizing Calculations, and Line Sizing Calculations — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- Process: All instrument/equipment/line numbering shall be per NOC Standard and generated by NNG tool — Exhibit A App A1, §2.3
- Mechanical: CONTRACTOR shall maintain the Master Equipment List in COMPANY-approved AVEVA format; update bi-weekly or monthly — Exhibit A App A1, §2.4
- HVAC: CONTRACTOR shall perform all HVAC detailed engineering through an APPROVED 3rd party subcontractor per Exhibit J; design in line with SD-NOC-HVA-802 — Exhibit A App A1, §2.5
- Piping: Computer-assisted piping stress analysis shall be performed using COMPANY approved software only (CAESAR 2 or AutoPIPE) — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- Piping: GRE piping stress and surge analysis shall be performed only by COMPANY's APPROVED GRE VENDOR — Exhibit A App A1, §2.6
- Structural: CONTRACTOR shall perform all in-service analyses using SACS, USFOS, MOSES, GRLWEAP and FEA software per Company standards — Exhibit A App A1, §2.7
- Structural: CONTRACTOR shall perform structural fire response analysis and design/locate PFP requirements per fire analysis — Exhibit A App A1, §2.7
- Electrical: CONTRACTOR shall perform power system studies using latest ETAP software covering load flow, short circuit, protective relay, motor starting, transient stability, arc-flash, harmonic analyses — Exhibit A App A1, §2.8
- Electrical: CONTRACTOR shall develop black start philosophy for NLQ (BI platform) considering all scenarios — Exhibit A App A1, §2.8
- INS/ICSS: CONTRACTOR shall follow IEC 61508/61511 methodologies for functional safety assessment — Exhibit A App A1, §2.9
- INS/ICSS: CONTRACTOR shall use AVEVA Instrumentation database per SD-NOC-INS-103; AVEVA is mandatory for all F&G I/O lists and datasheets — Exhibit A App A1, §2.9
- INS/ICSS: CONTRACTOR shall merge APPROVED ICSS Integrator (per Exhibit J) into Detailed Engineering organisation — Exhibit A App A1, §2.9
- Corrosion: Painting systems shall be high durability CX category for Superstructure and IM4 for immersed and splash zone per ISO/NORSOK/IOGP — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- Corrosion: Rigid steel caissons shall have 6 mm corrosion allowance for full length and IM4 coating — Exhibit A App A1, §2.10
- Architectural: CONTRACTOR shall model living quarters and technical rooms in 3D model to as-built status — Exhibit A App A1, §2.11
- Hook-Up: CONTRACTOR shall minimise offshore hot works; all work packs shall be managed through in-house database associated with PROJECT schedule tool — Exhibit A App A1, §2.12
- Software: Commissioning preparation shall use ICAPS (COMPANY software); firewater hydraulic calculations shall use PIPENET; piping stress via CAESAR 2 — Exhibit A App A1, §3
