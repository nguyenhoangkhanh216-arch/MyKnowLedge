## Summary

Instructions to Tenderer (ITT) for the New Living Quarter (NLQ) Project EPCIC 10, issued by North Oil Company (NOC) for the Al Shaheen Oil Field, Qatar (CFT ref. 10210491, ST-ITTS-2023 Ver.01 Rev.GH). The ITT governs the full Call for Tender process covering EPCIC of a 200-bed permanent accommodation platform at B-Location (with a like-for-like at A-Location as an option), and mandates specific submission packages (Contractual, Technical, HSE, Commercial) with strict deadlines, qualification templates, and evaluation criteria.

## Key Points

- **Project scope**: EPCIC of a New Living Quarter (NLQ) platform at B-Location (Al Shaheen field, ~60m water depth, ~70 km NNE of Ras Laffan, Qatar), plus optional like-for-like NLQ at A-Location; 200 beds capacity — ITT, §3
- **CFT reference**: CFT ref. 10210491; COMPANY = North Oil Company (NOC), operator under Development and Fiscal Agreement for Al Shaheen Oil Field — ITT, §1
- **Tender launch**: 26 June 2025; TENDERER must confirm/decline participation within 7 working days via e-SOURCING TOOL (SMART GEP) and submit signed Confidentiality form — ITT, §4, §5
- **Technical Closing Date**: 30 April 2026 (first wave) / 20 May 2026 (second wave) — ITT, §4
- **Commercial Closing Date**: 30 June 2026 (first wave) / 12 August 2026 (second wave) — ITT, §4
- **Contract Effective Date**: 30 September 2026 / 29 October 2026 — ITT, §4
- **Post-TBC Meetings**: 17 May 2026 and 21 June 2026 — ITT, §4
- **TENDERER's updated Technical Bid**: 31 May 2026 / 15 July 2026 — ITT, §4
- **Bid Bond submission**: original USD 500,000 Bid Bond due 31 August 2025; must comply with Attachment 15 form; issued by bank with S&P AA- or better (or URDG 758 Qatari bank) — ITT, §8.2.2, §9
- **Exceptions deadline**: Contractual exceptions (Attachment 6) due 27 February 2026; no Technical exceptions allowed after TECHNICAL CLOSING DATE — ITT, §4, §8.2.3.13
- **Key Engineering Deliverables compensation**: USD 800,000 lump sum paid to unsuccessful tenderers who properly complete deliverables (IFR → IFA → AFD, three revisions with CRS); awarded tenderer's compensation is absorbed into contract price — ITT, §7.3
- **Technical proposal types**: two options — (1) Conventional: new-built jacket+piles+topside; (2) Unconventional: new built or conversion of MOU/Jack-up Barge/SEP — ITT, §4
- **Two-section tender**: (1) TECHNICAL/CONTRACTUAL/HSE (Packages A, B, C) — no prices allowed; (2) COMMERCIAL; failure to keep sections separate causes disqualification — ITT, §8.1.5, §8.2
- **Tender validity period**: 180 calendar days from COMMERCIAL CLOSING DATE — ITT, Attachment 3A
- **Commercial submission**: all prices in USD; rates fully inclusive of taxes, duties, overheads; Exhibit B plus Appendices B1–B11 must all be completed — ITT, §8.3
- **ICV Plan**: In-Country Value (Tawteen/QatarEnergy initiative) mandatory; submitted in both Technical (unpriced) and Commercial sections per Attachment 17 — ITT, §2.3, §8.3
- **Commissioning**: mandatory OPERCOM® methodology and ICAPS© software (latest version) on all worksites; cost of licenses and training included in offer — ITT, §8.2.3.10
- **Governing law**: State of Qatar — ITT, §7.1
- **COMPANY audit rights**: COMPANY may audit/inspect TENDERER's and subcontractors' premises, facilities, workshops, marine spread at any time before CONTRACT — ITT, §7.4
