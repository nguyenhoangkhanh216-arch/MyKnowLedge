## Summary

Minutes of Meeting for the Early Detail Engineering (EDE) Kick-Off Meeting of the Nam Du-U Minh (NDUM) WHP and Pipeline EPCI project. Held 03-Jun-2026 at PTSC M&C office (hybrid). PTSC M&C (Contractor) and Jadestone Energy/JSE (Company) agreed on EDE scope, EMDR, key technical inputs, T&I strategy, procurement plan for the flexible riser package, and communication protocols.

## Key Points

- **EDE formal scope:** Detailed engineering for flexible riser package (ND-P1-PKG-PSF-05) to assess delivery lead times and support schedule concept selection
- **IFA target for all EDE documents: 15-Jun-2026** (CPY expects best-endeavour early issue; key technical items already discussed at KOM)
- **CPY preference:** Adopt FA Study basis (not BOD) for process design data — inconsistencies identified between BOD [1] and FA Study; CTR agreed to provide simulation results within 2 days without full report
- **Corrosion Allowance: 6mm confirmed** by CPY; formal TQ will proceed in parallel
- **CI assumption to be confirmed:** 95% availability, 99% efficiency — any change may require redesign
- **Line pipe:** CTR proposes SMLS over HFW; CPY confirmation pending via TQ
- **T&I strategy:** Early engagement critical — 3 key areas: HLV/Marine Spread, Hot Tapping Tie-in, Long-Lead Materials
- **Flexible riser installation concept:** on hold pending early engineering assessment
- **Procurement schedule set:** TR IFR 15-Jun → Bid Due 08-Aug → LOA 19-Dec-2026
- **Communication matrix issued:** JSE contacts per document type (Rob Laybourne TO for MOM/TQ/Engineering; John Falconer TO for TBC; Nguyen Trong Duy TO for commercial docs; Vuong Bao Thu TO for all engineering documents)
- **Monthly invoice protocol:** Draft MVC + invoice within 30 days of month-end → CPY comments within 7 working days → CTR issues official invoice → CPY pays undisputed within 30 days
