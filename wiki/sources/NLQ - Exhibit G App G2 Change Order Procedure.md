## Summary

Appendix G2 Rev A defines the Change Order procedure for NLQ EPCIC 10 per Article 22 of the AGREEMENT. It establishes the full process from Change Order Instruction (COI) issued by COMPANY to Change Order Request (COR) submitted by CONTRACTOR, through evaluation, negotiation, and execution of the approved Change Order. Three standard forms are provided as attachments: COI Form (G2-1), COR Form (G2-2), and CO Form (G2-3).

## Key Points

- **COI (Company-initiated)**: CONTRACTOR must respond within 7 days with: cost impact (per EXHIBIT B rates), schedule impact on critical path, ICV impact, and plant-value-to-be-imported impact — Exhibit G App G2, §2.1
- COMPANY reviews CONTRACTOR's evaluation within 7 days; may return for clarification (cycle repeatable at COMPANY discretion) — Exhibit G App G2, §2.3
- If COMPANY decides not to proceed, COI is withdrawn — Exhibit G App G2, §2.5
- COMPANY may instruct CONTRACTOR to proceed during evaluation phase — Exhibit G App G2, §2.6
- **COR (Contractor-initiated)**: must be submitted within 7 days of triggering event; requires full justification, cost breakdown, schedule impact, ICV revised plan — Exhibit G App G2, §3.1
- COR not considered without complete substantiation — Exhibit G App G2, §3.2
- COMPANY reviews COR within 14 days: may request withdrawal, clarification, or issue CO — Exhibit G App G2, §3.4
- WORK must not be delayed pending change order resolution — Exhibit G App G2, §3.6
- **Price evaluation priority**: (1) comparison with Exhibit B lump sums, (2) unit rates in Exhibit B, (3) time rates in Exhibit B, (4) analogy/reference to nearest rates, (5) actual PO cost for materials — Exhibit G App G2, §4.1.1
- **Schedule adjustment**: only granted if change is on critical path with zero float; CONTRACTOR must first attempt overtime/extra resources to avoid extension — Exhibit G App G2, §4.2
- CO executed in 3 originals; one returned to CONTRACTOR; CHANGE ORDER fully and finally settles all consequences of the subject change — Exhibit G App G2, §5
