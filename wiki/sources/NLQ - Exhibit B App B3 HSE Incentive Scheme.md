## Summary

Appendix B3 to Exhibit B (4 pages) defines the three HSE Incentive Schemes (IS1, IS2, IS3) available under the NLQ EPCIC 10 contract. All amounts are "TBD by COMPANY" at award; payment is at COMPANY's sole discretion based on demonstrated performance and APPROVAL.

## Key Points

- Three HSE Incentive Schemes with amounts TBD at award: IS1 (Lump Sum) + IS2 (Provisional Sum) + IS3 (Provisional Sum) — App B3, §2
- **HSE IS1** — WORKSHOPS/WORKSITES & CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENT Standards Improvement: CONTRACTOR develops Improvement Plan from COMPANY HSE self-audit matrix; covers worker safety behaviour, worksite practices, and tool/equipment conditions — App B3, §2.1
- **HSE IS2** — WORKSITE HSE Performance: CONTRACTOR reports monthly on 17 KPIs from Exhibit L Table 15.2; 100% achievement required each month for full duration until Completion Certificate — App B3, §2.2
- **HSE IS3** — Safety Leadership, Behaviour & Cultural Mindset Program: CONTRACTOR proposes recognition/reward program for exemplary individual and collective HSSE performance; may include additional worksite/equipment improvements beyond IS1 — App B3, §2.3
- **Payment terms for IS1**: 50% at 50% of APPROVED IS1 actions; remaining 50% after all IS1 actions completed — App B3, Table 2
- **Payment terms for IS2**: portion equivalent to overall project progress at last READY FOR TRANSPORTATION CERTIFICATE; remainder at final COMPLETION CERTIFICATE — App B3, Table 2
- **Payment terms for IS3**: full amount at issuance of COMPLETION CERTIFICATE — App B3, Table 2
- All incentive payments formalised through CHANGE ORDER; COMPANY is sole judge of whether target is achieved — App B3, §2.4
