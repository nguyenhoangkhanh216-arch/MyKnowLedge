## Summary

Appendix A3 (Rev A) defines COMPANY's independent Project Review (PR) programme for the NLQ EPCIC 10. Two milestone reviews are mandated: PR3 at 60% engineering progress and PR4 at 80% construction progress. Both are systematic audits covering design correctness, procurement, fabrication, installation readiness, commissioning, and operational readiness.

## Key Points

### General (§1)
- COMPANY policy requires independent Project Reviews (PRs) at different stages; PRs are in addition to technical audits which COMPANY may perform at any time — Exhibit A App A3, §1
- PR3 and PR4 ensure design intent from previous phases is fulfilled and "fit-for-purpose" objectives (value creation, risk management) are maintained — Exhibit A App A3, §1
- Project Reviews may be split into several partial audits (per WORKSHOP/WORKSITE) at COMPANY's discretion — Exhibit A App A3, §1

### PR3 — 60% Engineering Milestone (§2)
- PR3 is conducted by COMPANY at 60% progress of the detail engineering phase; objective: assess project readiness to move into construction — Exhibit A App A3, §2
- PR3 covers: correctness of design basis and engineering principles; identification of critical aspects of design/construction/installation; implementation of actions from previous reviews — Exhibit A App A3, §2
- PR3 review of AFC documents to verify intended design is properly implemented; review of deviation process — Exhibit A App A3, §2
- PR3 Procurement check: conformity of technical documents sent to vendors with latest AFC documents; inspection program review; FAT procedure review — Exhibit A App A3, §2
- PR3 Project Execution: assessment of project organisation and planning across all locations and phases; logistics preparation; HSE management systems review; QA/QC process verification for procurement and construction — Exhibit A App A3, §2

### PR4 — 80% Construction Milestone (§3)
- PR4 is conducted at 80% progress of the construction phase; confirms facilities constructed per approved design; verifies sail-away, towing/transportation, commissioning, and operations readiness — Exhibit A App A3, §3
- PR4 Procurement: inspection report review; non-conformance and corrective action review; FAT records review — Exhibit A App A3, §3
- PR4 Fabrication: verify in-situ resources; check correctness of fabrication documents; review welding/NDT procedures; check welder qualifications; check storage conditions; review safety procedures; conformity with AFC documentation — Exhibit A App A3, §3
- PR4 Mobilisation: review mobilisation manual; review construction manual for latest revision compliance; verify CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENT availability — Exhibit A App A3, §3
- PR4 Pre-commissioning: review onshore pre-commissioning procedures; compliance with corrective actions; verify pre-commissioning facilities and equipment availability; check pre-commissioning operations and records — Exhibit A App A3, §3
- PR4 Commissioning: review commissioning procedures and organisation; ensure PLANT can be started up and operated safely; assess adequacy of construction, pre-commissioning, commissioning, and start-up plans against COMPANY standards — Exhibit A App A3, §3
- PR4 Operational Readiness: review processes for transfer to operations; assess operating procedures; assess training and competency programs; review support services — Exhibit A App A3, §3

### CONTRACTOR Duties (§4)
- CONTRACTOR shall give COMPANY review team access to any documentation in its current status at time of review, including requisitions and purchase order documentation — Exhibit A App A3, §4
- CONTRACTOR's leaders/management shall be available to immediately answer questions and attend Project Review meetings at any time — Exhibit A App A3, §4
- CONTRACTOR shall implement COMPANY's review team comments as early as possible — Exhibit A App A3, §4
- If findings show PLANT to be unsafe, inoperable, or not maintainable, CONTRACTOR shall perform required modifications at own cost — Exhibit A App A3, §4
- CONTRACTOR shall maintain a live database of Technical Review findings and agreed actions with current status for COMPANY review — Exhibit A App A3, §4
- CONTRACTOR shall make available all detailed engineering documentation in latest AFC or As-Built issue, plus pre-commissioning, safety, and test reports — Exhibit A App A3, §4
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare documentation for each PR in quantities required by COMPANY: minimum 1 electronic copy (secured hard drive) and 3 paper copies included in the Lump Sum price — Exhibit A App A3, §4
- CONTRACTOR shall provide temporary space for PR team for duration of review: IT support (video conference, international desk phone, printers, internet), and lunch for 15 persons for 10 consecutive days per review (PR3 and PR4) — included in Lump Sum — Exhibit A App A3, §4
