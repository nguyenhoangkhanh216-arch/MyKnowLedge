## Summary

Survey questionnaire (Phiếu khảo sát hiện trạng) administered to PTSC M&C's Engineering Department (Phòng Thiết kế) as part of a Digital Transformation Strategy & Enterprise Architecture (EA) consulting project, implemented by FPT on behalf of PTSC M&C. The survey aims to document current business processes, IT application maturity, and identify digital transformation opportunities in the Engineering Department.

## Key Points

- **Project:** Dự án Tư vấn Chiến lược Chuyển đổi số và Kiến trúc Doanh nghiệp (Digital Transformation Strategy & EA Consulting)
- **Client:** PTSC M&C (Công ty TNHH MTV Dịch vụ Cơ khí Hàng hải PTSC)
- **Consultant:** FPT (Công ty Cổ phần FPT), contact: Lê Vũ Minh, minhlv18@fpt.com
- **Department surveyed:** Phòng Thiết kế (Engineering Department)

### Engineering Department Scope (from survey answers):
**Professional Engineering:**
- Offshore structures, oil & gas, floating facilities, renewables, civil/industrial engineering
- Basic design, detailed design (TKCT), construction drawings (BVTC)
- Design management; quality, safety, schedule control

**Bidding & Business Development support:**
- Prepare bid proposals and tender documents
- Approve technical requirements; assess design capability for market development
- Track revenue, cash flow, project reporting/settlement

**Procurement & Design Services management:**
- Prepare procurement requirements for goods and design services
- Manage subcontractors: selection, coordination, inspection, acceptance of design service contracts
