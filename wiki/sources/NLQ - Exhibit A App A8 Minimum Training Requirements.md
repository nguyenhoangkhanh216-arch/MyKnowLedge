## Summary

Appendix A8 (Rev B) specifies the minimum requirements for training of COMPANY operations personnel. CONTRACTOR must prepare an approved Training Program covering all disciplines, beginning during detailed engineering and completing prior to Commissioning. Training must be delivered in English by competent personnel using VENDOR Specialists where required.

## Key Points

### Scope and Duties (§1–§2)
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare a Training Program for APPROVAL and train COMPANY personnel based on the approved program — Exhibit A App A8, §2
- Training begins during detailed engineering phase and must be completed before Commissioning — Exhibit A App A8, §2
- Emphasis on START-UP, shutdown, and troubleshooting (least understandable from manuals); all disciplines included (instruments/controls, electrical, telecom, HVAC, galley appliances, mechanical) — Exhibit A App A8, §2
- Training must be completed before Transfer of Operation and Maintenance (TOM) — Exhibit A App A8, §2

### Training Plan Requirements (§2)
- Detailed technical training plan for COMPANY operations personnel submitted for COMPANY review and APPROVAL no later than 6 months post CONTRACT award — Exhibit A App A8, §2(i)
- Schedule shall include each VENDOR's specific equipment and training delivery schedule with agreed participant plan — Exhibit A App A8, §2(i)
- All training modules (syllabus, schedules, materials, competency assessment) shall be agreed in advance with COMPANY; materials available for review no less than 3 months prior to each course — Exhibit A App A8, §2(ii)
- Training schedule fully integrated with CONTRACTOR's overall delivery schedule; plan developed for each system, equipment item, and package — Exhibit A App A8, §2(iii)
- Classroom components: equipment description; normal startup/shutdown/operation; routine maintenance; specialist maintenance and troubleshooting per discipline — Exhibit A App A8, §2(iii)
- Demonstration/simulation components: VENDOR workshop familiarisation (key assembly/disassembly, equipment operation); Yard/Site familiarisation on facility — Exhibit A App A8, §2(iii)
- Training plan must include: duration; locations; course content; expected learning objectives; formal competencies and evidence of learning — Exhibit A App A8, §2(iii)
- Theoretical and practical phases; practical phase for operator familiarity with FACILITIES before start-up — Exhibit A App A8, §2(iv)
- All training delivered in English by competent personnel; training materials in English; format must be APPROVED and suitable for future editing — Exhibit A App A8, §2(v)
- CONTRACTOR must appoint a training manager/lead; provide documentation (attendees, objectives, content, learning outcomes) within one calendar week of each session to COMPANY — Exhibit A App A8, §2(vi)
- CONTRACTOR shall involve qualified VENDOR Specialists for completion of the training plan — Exhibit A App A8, §2(vii)
- Training Program shall integrate trained COMPANY personnel into CONTRACTOR Commissioning team for FACILITIES familiarisation — Exhibit A App A8, §2(viii)
- Training matrices for each category of personnel (management, section heads, supervisors, operators, maintenance) — Exhibit A App A8, §2(x)
- Training shall not conflict with any of the WORKS; training plan schedule submitted for APPROVAL 30 days before commencement — Exhibit A App A8, §2(xi)
- COMPANY offshore personnel work on 4-week rotation; CONTRACTOR shall arrange enough sessions for all personnel to attend — Exhibit A App A8, §2(xii)
- Upon completion, CONTRACTOR shall prepare a Training Close-out Report with training activities, attendance records, and competency evaluation — Exhibit A App A8, §2

### Minimum Training Schedule (§3 Table 3-1)
- ICSS (Safety Logic, C&E, Interfaces & Cybersecurity, F&G): 2 sessions × ½ day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance required — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Emergency Diesel Generator (EDG): 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance required — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Potable Water Maker: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Sea Water System (lift pump): 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Fire Water Pump Package: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Instrument Air Compressor Package: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Crane: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- HVAC system: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Galley Appliances: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- ECS Training, Protection Relays in Switch Gear & Power Management System: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- AC UPS: 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Telecoms (PAGA, Wifi): 2 sessions × 1 day; OFFSHORE; Vendor attendance — Exhibit A App A8, §3
- Maximum 10 trainees per session; VENDORs not based in Middle East give training during commissioning period — Exhibit A App A8, §3, Notes 1 & 2
