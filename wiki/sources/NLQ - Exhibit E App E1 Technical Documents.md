## Summary

Exhibit E Appendix E1 Rev C (Rev BC) defines the Technical Documents that form the Design Dossier for the NLQ EPCIC 10 project. It lists the TBE (To Be Endorsed) documents that CONTRACTOR must endorse as free from errors and omissions, and provides the framework for Technical Clarifications and Technical Exceptions raised during the Call for Tender.

## Key Points

- The Design Dossier Technical Documentation includes 6 key documents that CONTRACTOR must endorse (TBE): (1) NLQ Functional System Specification NLQ01-MPEN-ASBII-00-390001_rev00, (2) NLQ Safety Concept NLQ01-MPEN-ASBII-00-390002_rev01, (3) NLQ A-Location FSS NLQ01-MPEN-ASAGI-00-390001_rev00, (4) A-Location Safety Concept ALS-AY-CPDPA-SAF-0001_rev02, (5) key engineering deliverables in AFD status developed during EARLY WORK, (6) list of Technical Clarifications and Exceptions raised during CFT — Exhibit E App E1, §1
- TBE endorsement means CONTRACTOR confirms these documents are free from errors, omissions, and ambiguities per Article 13.4 of the AGREEMENT — Exhibit E App E1, §1
- Technical Clarifications (Queries) raised during Call for Tender shall be attached prior to EPC award — Exhibit E App E1, §2
- Technical Exceptions (Deviations) raised during Call for Tender shall be attached prior to EPC award — Exhibit E App E1, §3
