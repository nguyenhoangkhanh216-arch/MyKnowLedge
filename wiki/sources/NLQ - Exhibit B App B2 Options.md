## Summary
Appendix B2 Rev C defines the five Options available under NLQ EPCIC 10. Options are excluded from the initial CONTRACT PRICE and exercised by COMPANY via CHANGE ORDER. Option prices are fixed and firm. COMPANY confirms selection prior to Effective Date where possible.

## Key Points
- Option 1 (WU 12.1): NLQ at A-Location — same type/philosophy as B-Location NLQ; bridge-linked to AD Platform; studies sent via Tender Bulletin; deadline ED+7 days — App B2, §2.1
- Option 2 (WU 12.2): Logistics and Transportation between Qatar port and SITE — includes material transport and personnel transport — App B2, §2.2
- Option 3 (WU 12.3): Offshore Accommodation Vessel (OAV) — for hook-up and commissioning at SITE — includes initial and final debris survey, mob/demob of OAV and assistance vessels — App B2, §2.3
- Option 4 (WU 12.4): Helicopter refuelling and fuel storage system — per EXHIBIT C; battery limits per WU 12.4 EXHIBIT A — App B2, §2.4
- Option 5 (WU 12.5): Drinkable water from potable water — App B2, §2.5
- All Option prices: fixed and firm; COMPANY exercises at sole discretion per Exhibit C — App B2, §1.3
- Option 1 deadline: ED+7 days; Option 2 deadline: ED+XX months (TBD) — Exhibit C, Table 4-1
