## Summary

PMC-VSP meeting held 12-Jun-2026 (14:00–15:00 MS Teams; recording shared 13-Jun) to resolve two scope disputes raised by VSP on 11-Jun: (1) Telecom/ICSS scope and (2) Ship Collision Study. PMC clarified contractual positions; both items remain open at PMT escalation level. Telecom technical approach agreed (follow DE design); Ship Collision: VSP to send formal letter to PMT requesting PMC confirmation of obligation.

## Key Points

**Telecom/ICSS (Issue A):**
- VSP asserted telecom must be "complete system" for both Greenfield (KM/DD WHP topside) and Brownfield (BRA/HML office equipment).
- **PMC position (confirmed):** Contract limits PMC procurement to Package A (Greenfield) only. Brownfield (Package B) telecom procurement = VSP scope.
- **FEED→DE scope shift (A2):** FEED Block Diagram placed HML Office / PVEP-KM Office telecom under PMC-Telecom; DE moved these items to VSP-ICSS to facilitate System Engineering. PMC does not agree with the scope transfer.
- **Agreed resolution:** Technical teams agreed to proceed per DE design. Contractual/commercial alignment (SoW/SoS renegotiation) to be handled separately by procurement teams from both sides.

**Ship Collision Study (Issue B):**
- VSP position: Ship Collision is listed under QRA in HSMT Exhibit #2 (Package A); PVEP-KM confirmed separate SoW required (TBC#3 item 91). VSP cannot find any document showing PMC excluded it during bidding.
- **PMC position (MOM of DDE meeting):** PMC claims Ship Collision was excluded from SoW, but documentation not yet produced.
- **Lead time:** 1.5–2 months to complete the study.
- **Next step:** VSP (Vo Viet Hai) stated VSP engineering will formally request PMT to issue an official letter to PMC requiring confirmation of obligation to perform Ship Collision Study, and to begin immediately to protect project schedule.

**EEP (Issue C):**
- Technical updates to Engineering Execution Plan agreed directly between VSP and PMC technical teams at the meeting.
