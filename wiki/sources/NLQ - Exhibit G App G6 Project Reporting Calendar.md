## Summary

Appendix G6 Rev A defines the Project Reporting Calendar for NLQ EPCIC 10. The calendar format includes monthly cut-off dates, weekly and monthly report submission dates, and planned progress meeting dates across the full contract duration. The actual calendar dates are to be populated at contract award and agreed between the PARTIES. In the CFT version, the table structure is present but contains no pre-filled dates.

## Key Points

- Monthly cut-off dates referenced by Exhibit G §4.1.1 as the basis for monthly reporting cycle — Exhibit G App G6, §1
- Reporting calendar is a CCM Part 1 deliverable per Table 3-1 of Exhibit G — Exhibit G App G6, §1
- Calendar must show all week numbers, cut-off dates, weekly and monthly report submission dates, planned meeting dates, and all non-working days (weekends, public holidays) — Exhibit G, §4.1.2
- Weekly cut-off: each Thursday at close of business (fixed; not dependent on calendar) — Exhibit G, §4.1.1
