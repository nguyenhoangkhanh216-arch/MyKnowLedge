## Summary

Appendix G5 Rev A (16 pages) defines the Risk Management Procedure for NLQ EPCIC 10. It covers the full iterative risk process: identification, evaluation (6×6 risk matrix), mitigation, monitoring, and reporting. Key deliverables include the Risk Management Plan (ED+1M), quarterly risk workshops, monthly Top-15 risk reporting, and Planning and Schedule Risk Analysis (PSRA) using Deltek Acumen Fuse at each major project phase.

## Key Points

### Risk Management Plan
- CONTRACTOR submits Risk Management Plan within ED + 1 month; must cover methodology, roles, participation plan, opportunity optimization — Exhibit G App G5, §2
- Risk Management Plan requires COMPANY APPROVAL — Exhibit G App G5, §2

### Organisation
- CONTRACTOR must nominate a "Risk Manager" as primary COMPANY contact for all risk matters; CV subject to COMPANY APPROVAL — Exhibit G App G5, §4
- CONTRACTOR monitors risks in its own scope AND risks from COMPANY's activities that could affect CONTRACTOR — Exhibit G App G5, §1

### Risk Process
- Iterative monthly cycle: Identification → Evaluation → Mitigation → Monitoring → Reporting → Review Meetings — Exhibit G App G5, §5
- "Risk" (potential/uncertain event) must be clearly separated from "Issue" (already occurred event); Issues maintained in separate Issue Log — Exhibit G App G5, §6
- Risk identification methods: document review, lessons learned, brainstorming, interviews, workshops with subcontractors and COMPANY — Exhibit G App G5, §6
- Risk workshops: minimum quarterly; mandatory prior to each phase (Engineering & Procurement, Construction, Installation, Commissioning) — Exhibit G App G5, §2 (Table 2-1)

### Risk Evaluation (6×6 Matrix)
- Overall Impact = Probability Score × Severity Score — Exhibit G App G5, §7
- Criticality ranking: Minor (1–6), Significant (8–18), Unacceptable (24–64) — Exhibit G App G5, §7
- Probability categories: <2% to ≥80%; Severity assessed on 5 dimensions: Cost, Schedule, Production Loss, Reputational/Compliance, Environment, Human — Exhibit G App G5, §7
- Unacceptable risks require urgent action up to Project Manager level — Exhibit G App G5, §7 (Figure +7-2)

### Mitigation Strategies
- Avoidance, Transfer, Mitigation (reduce probability or consequence), Acceptance — Exhibit G App G5, §8
- Action Owner assigned for each risk mitigation; Risk Manager tracks status at each review — Exhibit G App G5, §8

### Risk Breakdown Structure (RBS)
- 9 Level-1 categories: Technical, External, Political, Contractual, Organizational, Technological, Compliance, Operational, HSEQ — Exhibit G App G5, §12.1 (Table 12-1)
- Each has 4–9 Level-2 sub-categories (e.g., Technical Fabrication & Construction, Operational Interface Management, HSEQ Quality Performance) — Exhibit G App G5, §12.1

### Reporting
- Monthly Risk Register Report: Risk event/cause/consequence, RBS level, risk owner, pre/current/post-mitigation scores, action owners, planned dates — Exhibit G App G5, §12.1
- Top 15 Risk Summary: formal appendix to Monthly Report; basis for risk segment of monthly meeting — Exhibit G App G5, §12.2
- PSRA Report content: probability/uncertainty assumptions, Risk-schedule mapping, results (P50 etc.), tornado graph, float consumption, criticality, cruciality, complete risk register — Exhibit G App G5, §12.3

### Planning and Schedule Risk Analysis (PSRA)
- Mandatory tool: **DELTEK Acumen Fuse** (latest version) — Exhibit G App G5, §10
- Triggered: upon COMPANY APPROVAL of DWTS; repeated prior to each project phase — Exhibit G App G5, §2, §3
- CONTRACTOR establishes separate L2 CPM schedule for PSRA; schedule critique confirms logic integrity, no constraints/broken logic/negative lags/open-ended tasks — Exhibit G App G5, §10
- Uncertainty assigned per activity as distribution (uniform/triangle/beta pert) with pessimistic/realistic/optimistic range — Exhibit G App G5, §10
- Dedicated PSRA workshop with COMPANY and stakeholders to validate inputs — Exhibit G App G5, §10
- CONTRACTOR must engage COMPANY-APPROVED qualified third-party or independent CONTRACTOR resource for PSRA — Exhibit G App G5, §3
