## Summary

Exhibit E Appendix E2 Rev C (Rev BC) defines the Al-Shaheen Existing Facilities and Brownfield Integration Scope for the NLQ EPCIC 10 project. It governs CONTRACTOR's full responsibility for brownfield integration at BH and BJ locations, from Effective Date through issuance of the Completion Certificate, and outlines the two possible execution scenarios depending on whether BH/BJ platforms are still under OTHER CONTRACTOR execution at Effective Date.

## Key Points

- B-Location and A-Location as-built documentation is available on iView at Effective Date; CONTRACTOR requests native files via the RFI process during execution — Exhibit E App E2, §1
- Brownfield integration work organisation and identification are within CONTRACTOR's scope; follow-up and performance of identified tasks are an integral part of the scope of WORK — Exhibit E App E2, §2
- Brownfield integration work management is CONTRACTOR's responsibility and covers all activities (engineering, procurement, fabrication/construction, pre-commissioning, T&I, hook-up, commissioning) from EFFECTIVE DATE until issuance of the COMPLETION CERTIFICATE — Exhibit E App E2, §2
- At CFT stage, platforms BJ and BH are under EPCIC execution by OTHER CONTRACTORs; after EFFECTIVE DATE, two scenarios: (a) CONTRACTOR carries out brownfield work with OTHER CONTRACTOR, or (b) CONTRACTOR works directly with COMPANY — scope of WORK is identical in either case — Exhibit E App E2, §2
- Brownfield integration scope details and engineering documentation are in Attachment 1 (NLQ – Brownfield Integration Scope), document CPPR1-MDM5-ASBJA-05-273123-0002_rev.04, updated and integrated within §6.5.5 of the FSS — Exhibit E App E2, §3
