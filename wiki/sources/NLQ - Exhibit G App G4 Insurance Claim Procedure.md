## Summary

Appendix G4 Rev A (5 pages) defines the Insurance Claim Procedure for the NLQ EPCIC 10 contract under the COMPANY's Construction All Risk (CAR) Policy. It establishes CONTRACTOR's obligations for immediate event reporting, evidence preservation, cooperation with Loss Adjusters, claim file preparation, and submission to COMPANY for onward submission to underwriters.

## Key Points

- CONTRACTOR must immediately take protective/salvage measures, obtain photographic evidence, and prepare an Insurance Incident Report with location, nature, description, damage extent, persons involved, repair estimate, sequential claim number — Exhibit G App G4, §3.1.1
- Damaged property must not be repaired without COMPANY and insurer prior APPROVAL (except emergencies) — Exhibit G App G4, §3.1.2
- No admission of liability or payment offer to any claimant without underwriter prior APPROVAL — Exhibit G App G4, §3.1.3
- COMPANY notifies broker/underwriters and appoints Loss Adjuster on receipt of CONTRACTOR's notification — Exhibit G App G4, §3.2
- CONTRACTOR must cooperate fully with Loss Adjusters: survey access, document access (daily logs, MOMs, inspection records), cost records — Exhibit G App G4, §4.1
- Repair/reinstatement method must be agreed with Loss Adjuster and COMPANY before proceeding — Exhibit G App G4, §4.1.ii
- Separate accounts maintained for each insurance claim — Exhibit G App G4, §4.1.iii
- Insurance claim file submitted by CONTRACTOR to COMPANY (not directly to underwriters); COMPANY is sole negotiating party — Exhibit G App G4, §6.2
