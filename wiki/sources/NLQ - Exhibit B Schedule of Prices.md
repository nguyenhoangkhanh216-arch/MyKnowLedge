## Summary

Exhibit B Rev C (16 pages + 12 appendices) defines the full pricing and payment framework for NLQ EPCIC 10. It establishes the lump-sum basis, Change Order rates, payment schedule (35%/60%/5% split), helicopter and service rates, Liquidated Damages (USD 200K/day), and the marine fuel adjustment mechanism. Prices are in USD, fixed and firm with no escalation.

## Key Points

- All Lump Sum prices are fixed and firm in USD for the entire CONTRACT duration; no escalation for any reason including currency fluctuations — Exhibit B, §1.2
- Lump Sum basis: CONTRACTOR is fully responsible for complete performance; any item left un-priced is deemed included in other Work Packages — Exhibit B, §2.1.2
- Standardisation: COMPANY controls vendor selection for items in Exhibit J Attachment J2; CONTRACTOR's Lump Sum covers all standardised items regardless of which vendor COMPANY selects — Exhibit B, §2.1.4
- Provisional Sums are not a COMPANY commitment; activated only via CHANGE ORDER; no extension to contract period will be considered for Provisional Sum works — Exhibit B, §2.2
- Customs duties in Qatar are reimbursable at substantiated cost in QAR or USD as agreed — Exhibit B, §2.3
- OPTIONS are excluded from Initial CONTRACT PRICE; exercised via CHANGE ORDER at pre-defined basis (App B2) at COMPANY's sole discretion — Exhibit B, §2.4
- Change Orders priced by correlation with Lump Sums where possible; if reimbursable, rates from App B4/B5/B6/B7 apply — Exhibit B, §3.1
- Procurement Services Fee for Change Orders: 10% on cost ≤USD 100K; 5% on cost >USD 100K; minimum 3 quotes required for items >USD 100K — Exhibit B, §3.4.1
- Helicopter cost: USD 750/person/one-way trip when CONTRACTOR manages bookings; charged also for 'No Show' unless documented medical reason; last cancellation call 12PM local Qatari time for next day flights — Exhibit B, §3.4.4
- Payment structure: 35% of Lump Sum on milestone achievement (App B9); 60% on actual physical progress; 5% on PAC issuance — Exhibit B, §8.2.1
- Max 8 invoices per month (4 in Qatar, 4 outside Qatar); one additional for customs duties — Exhibit B, §8.2
- PAYMENT SCHEDULE: WP1 (excl. WU1.5) = 100% physical progress; WU1.5 = reimbursable; WP2 = 100% physical progress (WU2.3 = App B3); WP3–WP9 = 100% physical progress; WP10 = reimbursable; WP11 = 100% on Final Documentation — Exhibit B, §8.3
- **Liquidated Damages for Late Completion**: CD1 (BI Substructure RFT) = USD 200K/day, max 10%CP; CD2 (BI Topside & Bridge RFT) = USD 200K/day, max 10%CP; CD3 (TOM Certificate) = USD 200K/day, max 10%CP; CD4 (Completion Certificate) = USD 100K/day, max 5%CP; if CD1+CD2 same day = USD 400K/day; overall LD cap = 10% of initial CP — Exhibit B, §9.1
- **Liquidated Damages for Key Personnel Substitution**: Level 1 personnel – USD 100K discovery + USD 10K/week until replacement mobilised; Level 2 – USD 50K discovery + USD 5K/week — Exhibit B, §9.2
- ICV Plan per Appendix B10; can only be amended by CHANGE ORDER — Exhibit B, §10
- Appendices: B1 (CP Breakdown/WBS), B2 (OPTIONS), B3 (HSE Incentive), B4 (Company Service Rates), B5 (Personnel Rates), B6 (Equipment Rates), B7 (Unit Rates), B9 (Milestone Payments), B10 (ICV Plan), B11 (Milestone Completion Certificate), B12 (Marine Fuel Adjustment) — Exhibit B, §11
