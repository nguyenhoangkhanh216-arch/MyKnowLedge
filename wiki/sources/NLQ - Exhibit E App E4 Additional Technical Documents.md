## Summary

Exhibit E Appendix E4 Rev B lists Additional Technical Documents that CONTRACTOR must rely upon for the NLQ EPCIC 10 project. The documents are categorised by location (B-Location and A-Location) and cover field layout charts, coordinates, geophysical/geotechnical survey reports, safety concept documents, ENVID reports, and HAZAN studies. These are NTBE (Not To Be Endorsed) reference documents that inform engineering but are not subject to CONTRACTOR endorsement.
