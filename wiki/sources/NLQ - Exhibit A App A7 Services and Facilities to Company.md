## Summary

Appendix A7 (Rev B) specifies the minimum quantity and quality of facilities and services that CONTRACTOR must provide to COMPANY across all work locations (Engineering Offices, Fabrication Site, Offshore SITE, Marine Spread, and other Worksites). All costs are included in Work Unit 1.5 of Exhibit B. Services standard shall be "first class" and all equipment shall be new.

## Key Points

### Duration Commitments (§4)
- Engineering Offices services: from Effective Date (KD1) up to 6 months after completion of 90% 3D Model review (up to 6 months after KD18) — Exhibit A App A7, §4(i)
- Fabrication Site services: from 3 months before start of pre-fabrication (first steel cut, KD17) up to 2 months after last READY FOR TRANSPORTATION CERTIFICATE (KD38 & KD45) — Exhibit A App A7, §4(ii)
- Installation Supervision Team offshore: from arrival of Marine Spread/Construction Equipment up to demobilisation — Exhibit A App A7, §4(iii)
- Hook-up/Commissioning Supervision Teams offshore: from arrival of Construction/Integration Marine Spread through demobilisation — Exhibit A App A7, §4(iv)

### Offices (§3.1)
- CONTRACTOR shall provide 24/7 building access to COMPANY personnel and nominated parties — Exhibit A App A7, §3.1
- Office types: Type I Large (≥18 m², single occupancy, 4-person meeting table, teleconference); Type II (≥12 m², single occupancy); Type III (≥18 m², double occupancy); Type IV (≥25 m², 5-person); Large Conference Room (≥25 m², ≥15 persons, video-conference, ClickShare/HDMI); Small Conference Room (≥8 persons, video-conference) — Exhibit A App A7, §3.1
- CONTRACTOR shall provide latest maintenance report confirming facilities are safe and fully operational prior to mobilisation — Exhibit A App A7, §3.1
- Exclusive sheltered parking for COMPANY; if unavailable, CONTRACTOR shall provide transport — Exhibit A App A7, §3.1

### IT Infrastructure (§3.12)
- Network: Cisco FPR-1120 firewall (outside Doha); Cisco C9300-48-E switches; Cisco Catalyst 9100 access points (within Doha) — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Internet: Primary 100 Mbps minimum (100 Mbps for 50–100 users, 150 Mbps for 100–150 users, 200 Mbps for 100–200 users); Backup 50 Mbps minimum — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Primary and backup ISPs must be different providers for redundancy; COMPANY may select ISP — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Wi-Fi: minimum 100 Mbps dedicated exclusively for COMPANY PERSONNEL — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Workstation: two Dell 24-inch FHD monitors (Dell P2422H equivalent or better); Dell Dock WD19S; keyboard, mouse, headset with MS Teams capability, security lock — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Printers: Canon iR-ADV C5850 equivalent MFP; individual A4 laser (HP P2055D equivalent); A3 colour laser (HP LJC 5525 equivalent) — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Technical room: ≥15 m², 2.5 m ceiling, 19-inch 42U racks, 19°C air conditioning, reinforced concrete walls, no windows, badge-controlled access managed solely by COMPANY — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Video conferencing: Microsoft Teams compatible, ClickShare, HD auto-framing camera, noise-cancelling microphone, touch panel — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- Hardware SLA: 4-hour response for critical issues; NBD for non-critical; faulty hardware replaced within 24 hours — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12
- UPS: minimum 30 minutes of operation during power failure — Exhibit A App A7, §3.12

### Other Services (§3.2–§3.18)
- Kitchen: fully stocked with mineral water (still and sparkling), hot/cold refreshments, Nespresso-type coffee, tea, soft drinks, biscuits, snacks, fruit — Exhibit A App A7, §3.2
- Messing: daily lunch provision equivalent to CONTRACTOR's management/engineering staff; multi-cultural cuisine; outside caterer if insufficient variety — Exhibit A App A7, §3.3
- Administration: assistance for permits, governmental authorisations, visas, car rental, domestic/international travel for COMPANY personnel, families, and nominated parties — Exhibit A App A7, §3.4
- First Aid: kit including defibrillator; replenished by CONTRACTOR; emergency notification procedure — Exhibit A App A7, §3.5
- Security: access control with badge system; video control system if deemed necessary; badge list managed by COMPANY — Exhibit A App A7, §3.6
- Vehicles: Type A (Toyota Land Cruiser 4WD, brand new); Type B (saloon, 1.8L, brand new); Type C (7-seat minivan, 1.8L, with driver 24/7); full insurance, fuel, licenses included — Exhibit A App A7, §3.7
- Telephone: Local SIM cards with unlimited 4G/5G; USD 100 credit/month; 20 hours/month (10 international + 10 national) — Exhibit A App A7, §3.8
- PPE: all necessary work equipment and PPE for COMPANY PERSONNEL including winter wear, hard hats, overalls, safety boots, marine jackets, waterproofs — Exhibit A App A7, §3.11
- VHF/UHF radios: intrinsically safe; ATEX if required; DTMF keypad; spare batteries and charging units — Exhibit A App A7, §3.13
- Cabins (offshore): Type 4 (single), Type 5 (double), Type 6 (four occupancy); with electricity, heating/AC, water, telephone, internet, satellite TV — Exhibit A App A7, §3.18
- Prayer room: shared multifaith room; routine cleaning by CONTRACTOR — Exhibit A App A7, §3.17
- Miscellaneous services: COMPANY may request additional services; CONTRACTOR must respond within 7 days with cost and schedule impact — Exhibit A App A7, §4.2
- Monthly report: Services and Facilities to COMPANY section (quantity and cost) must be included in monthly report — Exhibit A App A7, §5
