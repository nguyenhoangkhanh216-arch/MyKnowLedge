## Summary

PTSC M&C and VSP are finalizing the KMDD Subcontract (PTSC as subcontractor under VSP's EPCI contract with PVEP-KM). A face-to-face (F2F) meeting was held 05-Jun-2026 in Ho Chi Minh City. Multiple exhibits have been agreed; key open items remain in Exhibits 3 (Compensation/Payment), 8 (Schedule), and the LD Milestone for RFSA.

## Key Points

- **AGREED at F2F 05-Jun:**
  - Warranty period: 30 months from RFSA (Readiness for System Acceptance)
  - Exhibit 4 (Admin): Temporary works for load-out (4.3, 4.4, 4.5) included; IT facilities (PTSC to provide own); vehicles/laptops NOT included
  - Exhibit 2 (Scope): Defined Package A content for both WHPs; temporary works for transportation and installation agreed
  - DCC support: 2 personnel from PMC DCC + 1 from TK (Thư ký?)

- **OPEN ITEMS (as of 13-Jun):**
  - **LD Key Milestone (RFSA):** VSP holds position that RFSA by 12-Jul-2027 with NO GRACE period; PTSC position not yet confirmed in thread; this is a commercial risk item
  - **RFLO (Ready for Load-Out) milestone:** 30-Jun-2027 (linked to project schedule)
  - **Exhibit 3 (Compensation):** Monthly payment terms not yet agreed — still in discussion
  - **Exhibit 8 (Schedule):** Key dates and milestone dates list not finalized; requires alignment with engineering and construction plan
  - **Payment forms (Exhibit 6/Annex):** Contractor data forms pending

- **CPY Document Template (13-Jun):** Châu sent "KDP PMC Template for Document Rev 1" to Nam (PTSC) for review — 3 specific points flagged: (1) cover page format, (2) "subcontractor" wording preference, (3) reference to Malaysian law (Sarawak seat)

- **Exhibit 1 (General T&Cs):** Largely agreed; warranty 30m from RFSA is firm

- **Context:** VSP signed the main EPCI contract with PVEP-KM with specific project milestones; subcontract must mirror those milestones so VSP can flow down liability to PTSC
