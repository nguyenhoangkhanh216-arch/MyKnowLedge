## Summary

Appendix G1 Rev A defines the mandatory structure and content for CONTRACTOR's weekly and monthly progress reports across all 12 Work Packages of the NLQ EPCIC 10 contract. The weekly report covers WP1–WP12 in full; the monthly report has the same structure but adds Executive Summary, Risk Management section (with PSRA), and progress photographs.

## Key Points

- Weekly cut-off and submission governed by Exhibit G §4.3.2; monthly by §4.3.3 — Exhibit G App G1, §1
- **WP1 – Management, Project Control & Administration**: general management narrative, progress table (plan vs actual by WU), productivity factors, S-curves, schedule Level 4+, critical path float analysis, QA/QC inspections and 6-week look-ahead, TQ register by discipline, interface highlights, services/facilities to COMPANY — Exhibit G App G1, §1.1
- **WP2 – HSE**: HSE statistics (LTIF, TRIR cumulative), lagging/leading indicators, LTIs, near misses, corrective actions, alert notices, special training — Exhibit G App G1, §1.2
- **WP3 – Engineering**: narrative on achievements, progress on major drawings and key documents, outstanding items by Work Unit — Exhibit G App G1, §1.3
- **WP4 – Procurement**: status on purchasing, expediting, inspection, forwarding and shipping — Exhibit G App G1, §1.4
- **WP5 – Onshore Construction**: NDT statistics (structural/piping), reject weld identification, rolling 4-week NDT past performance, construction critical path analysis, shop drawing progress by discipline, fabrication/assembly progress by facility and deck, FAT punch list clearance — Exhibit G App G1, §1.5
- **WP6 – Transportation & Installation**: marine spread mobilisation/demobilisation narrative; shipment status — Exhibit G App G1, §1.6
- **WP7 – Site Construction Works**: same detail as WP5 + integration works by location; FAT punch list clearance — Exhibit G App G1, §1.7
- **WP8 – Commissioning**: ongoing activities and areas of concern; onshore and offshore commissioning progress by location — Exhibit G App G1, §1.8
- **WP9 – Brownfield/Integration**: maps to WP3–WP8 requirements by sub-Work Unit — Exhibit G App G1, §1.9
- **WP10 – Assistance to Start-up and Training**: narrative on activities and areas of concern — Exhibit G App G1, §1.10
- **WP11 – Final Documentation & Contract Close-out**: achievements, critical points, areas of concern, resources, action list review — Exhibit G App G1, §1.11
- **WP12 – OPTION NLQ A-LOC**: same structure as WP1–WP11, same level of detail — Exhibit G App G1, §1.12
- **Monthly additions**: Executive Summary (decisions, milestones, critical points, resources, activities for next period); Risk Management (Top Risks, PSRA) — Exhibit G App G1, §2
- **Document attachments table (Table 3-1)**: MDR weekly (monthly as summary), Document Control Report weekly, Requisition/Procurement/Traffic Register weekly, Cost/Invoice/CO Registers monthly, 6-week Look Ahead weekly, Level 3 schedule monthly, KPI Report monthly — Exhibit G App G1, §3
