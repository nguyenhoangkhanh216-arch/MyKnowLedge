## Summary

PTSC issued TBC#1 Rev.A1 for NLQ (New Living Quarter, Ruya B1 Project) MEC-019 Fixed Foam package to C&C Offshore Engineering Pte. Ltd (Singapore). Response deadline 26-Jun-2026. C&C had raised questions about approved vendor lists for foam pumps and proportioners, and clarified that foam monitors, spray nozzles, and water guns are outside their scope of supply.

## Key Points

- **Package:** NLQ-MEC-019 Fixed Foam (Ruya B1 NLQ Project — offshore Qatar, NOC client)
- **Bidder:** C&C Offshore Engineering Pte. Ltd (Singapore, contact: tlchuah@cnc-offshore.com)
- **TBC#1 Rev.A1 issued:** 24-Jun-2026 by Trần Ánh Linh (Commercial); directed by Võ Anh Kiệt (Mechanical)
- **Response deadline:** 26-Jun-2026
- **C&C scope of supply (their confirmed scope):** Fixed Foam Skid Package only
- **Items NOT in C&C scope:** Foam Monitor, Foam Spray Nozzles, Foam Water Gun
- **C&C proposed brands for foam pumps:** Grundfos or Viking (UL-qualified)
- **C&C proposed brands for foam proportioners:** Skum, Viking (UL-qualified)
- **Approved vendor list (project AVL) status:**
  - Foam Firefighting Systems (32G): no approved vendors listed
  - Foam Concentrate Bladder Tanks (32G2): no approved vendors listed
  - Water/Foam Monitors (32F5): DESAUTEL, A.I. GROUP, TYCO (qualified)
  - Foam/Water gun (3225-03): A.I. GROUP (qualified)
  - Foam/Water Spray Nozzles (32F6): TYCO, BETE, Grinnell, GW Sprinkler, Angus Fire, DRAGER, SOLAS MARINE (Qatar)
  - Deluge valves (32F4): INBAL, CLA-VAL, TYCO-Fire, KSB (qualified)
- **Offshore location:** Fabricated in Vietnam; installed offshore Qatar (~70 km from Ras Laffan, 60m water depth)
- **Project scope:** Base case 1×NLQ (200 POB); Optional case 2×NLQ
