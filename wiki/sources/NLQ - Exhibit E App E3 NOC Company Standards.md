## Summary
App E3 (Rev B) lists all 610 NOC Company Standards (SD-NOC-*) and guidelines (GM-NOC-*, PL-NOC-*, RL-NOC-*) that are mandatory references for the NLQ EPCIC 10 project. All are issued at CFT stage and are "Not to be Endorsed" (NTBE) by CONTRACTOR — they are mandatory governing standards, not endorseable commitments.

## Key Points
- 610 documents total across all engineering disciplines; all classified as Level 3 Standards or Level 4 Guidelines; all issued at CFT stage (not added post-award). — Exhibit E App E3, §Header
- **Key discipline groupings (SD-NOC-*):** COR (corrosion, ~100 standards: painting, cathodic protection, PFP, coatings), ELE (electrical: ETAP studies, switchgear, cables, UPS, generators, VSD), HVA (HVAC: design criteria, AHU, ACU, dampers, duct insulation), INS (instrumentation: ICSS, SIS, control valves, PRVs, analysers, F&G, metering), MEC (rotating machinery packages: 60+ standards covering turbo generators, pumps, compressors, API 610/674/616/617), PLR (pipeline: design, installation, testing, coatings, flexible pipes), PVV (piping and pressure vessels: material classes, stress, fabrication, valves), SAF (safety: layout, HAZARD AREA classification, HIPPS, ESD, fire systems), STR (structural: jacket, topside, FEA, fabrication, loadout), TEL (telecom: PAGA, CCTV, radio, satellite, fibre optic). — Exhibit E App E3, SD-NOC blocks
- **Key SD-NOC standards referenced elsewhere in Exhibit A:** SD-NOC-EC-600/601/602/603 (pre-commissioning/commissioning), SD-NOC-INS-103 (E&I Database Management / AVEVA INS), SD-NOC-HVA-802 (HVAC documents for Basic/Detail Engineering), SD-NOC-PJC-501 (Quality Management requirements for CONTRACTOR), SD-NOC-EC-106 (Equipment Tagging and Facilities Numbering), SD-NOC-SAF-009 (HIPPS Design). — Exhibit E App E3
- All COMPANY Standards take precedence over international standards only if explicitly stated in Project Documentation (Particular Specifications). — Exhibit E App E3 / Exhibit E §2
