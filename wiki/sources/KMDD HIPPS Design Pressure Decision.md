## Summary

Design pressure downstream of HIPPS on both KM-WHP and DD-WHP was confirmed at 133 barg (900# section) in March 2026. The original FEED had 150 barg for some components. The reduction to 133 barg was driven by the SS316 piping material limit at 900# class, and ensures consistency across both platforms.

## Key Points

- **Decision:** d/s HIPPS design pressure = **133 barg** (both KM-WHP and DD-WHP)
- **Scope:** Includes Pig Launcher and CI injection pump systems on both platforms
- **Reason:** SS316 piping at 900# class has maximum allowable working pressure ~133 barg @ 80°C; running higher design pressure would require upgrading to Class 1500 or changing material
- **Background:** DD-WHP original discussion was to keep 150 barg for equipment but only reduce SS316 piping to 133 barg. The final agreed position brings both platforms to 133 barg consistently
- **HIPPS implication:** HIPPS safety time calculation uses 133 barg as the downstream threshold (weakest point in the system)
- **P&ID update:** Process team to update P&IDs and all related documents to reflect 133 barg
- **No concerns raised:** MEC team confirmed no concerns from mechanical perspective
