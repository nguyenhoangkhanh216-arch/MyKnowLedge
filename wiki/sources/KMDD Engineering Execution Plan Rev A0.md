## Summary

Engineering Execution and Quality Assurance Plan for KM-WHP & DD-WHP (KMDD Project), document number KDP-000-03-PS-G-0001, Rev A0 (Inter-Discipline Check). Prepared by PTSC M&C (Topsides Subcontractor); circulated to VSP for review by Nguyễn Hoàng Khánh 30-May-26. VSP (Nguyễn Tuấn Cường/OCD) returned comments 12-Jun-26; meeting held same day to align. Document governs engineering execution strategy, organizational structure, document control, quality assurance, and discipline-specific execution across all KMDD engineering scope.

## Key Points

- **Project parties:** VSP = Contractor (CONTRACTOR); PTSC M&C = Topsides Subcontractor; NIPI/OCD = Jacket/Pipeline Subcontractor
- **PTSC M&C scope:** Topsides engineering for KM-WHP and DD-WHP (greenfield WHP topside structures)
- **Brownfield scope:** Handled separately by OCD/NIPI; Hold 1 in Rev D0 = "Nomination of Brownfield Engineering Sub" (open at D0 issue 28-May-26)
- **Engineering org roles:** Contractor Engineering Manager (VSP), Topsides Subcontractor Engineering Manager (PTSC M&C), Jacket/Pipeline Subcontractor Engineering Manager (separate)
- **Document issue statuses:** IFR (Issued for Review), IFA (Issued for Approval), AFC (Approved for Construction), IFI (Issued for Information)
- **IDC process:** Self Check → Single Discipline Check (SDC) → Inter-Discipline Check (IDC) → Project Approval for IFR/IFA/AFC/IFI
- **Revision history:** Rev D0 issued 28-May-26 (Inter-Discipline Check); Rev A0 is the comment version under VSP review
- **Table of contents (57 pages):** Engineering Management/Control Plan, Detailed Discipline Execution Plan (Process, Mech/HVAC, Piping/Layout, INS/Telecom, Electrical, STR Topsides, STR Jacket, Technical Safety, Pipeline, Brownfield), Engineering QA and Design Control
- **Document control:** Workflow for vendor data review under VSP team procedure — to be incorporated into EEP
- **Appendices:** Contact list, org charts, responsibility matrix, EDR/MDR philosophy, engineering schedule, software register, templates
