## Summary

Appendix H3 Rev A (9 pages) defines the four Inspection Levels and maps each to equipment and material categories for the NLQ EPCIC 10 project. Inspection levels are directly correlated to criticality ratings from App H4: Criticality 1 = Level I (Full Stage), Criticality 2 = Level II (Occasional), Criticality 3 = Level III (Final), Criticality 4 = Level IV (Document only).

## Key Points

### Inspection Level Definitions
- Level I — Full Stage Inspection: continuous full-time inspector presence during entire manufacturing process; includes material traceability review, qualified welder verification, all NDT witness, pressure and performance tests; applies to Criticality Rating 1 items — Exhibit H App H3, §3.1
- Level II — Occasional Stage Inspection: predefined hold and witness points + occasional monitoring; includes FAT, integration tests, final release; applies to Criticality Rating 2 items — Exhibit H App H3, §3.2
- Level III — Final Inspection: inspection on completed item prior to delivery; final condition or as-built; applies to Criticality Rating 3 items — Exhibit H App H3, §3.3
- Level IV — Document Inspection: review of manufacturer's certification and fabrication records only; applies to Criticality Rating 4 items — Exhibit H App H3, §3.4

### Surveillance Activities by Criticality Rating (Table 3-1)
- Criticality 1: QMS audit of vendor prior to commitment; resident inspector at vendor; systematic engineering document review; all Hold Points; full FAT witness; QC records intermediate review — Exhibit H App H3, §3.4 Table 3-1
- Criticality 2: Review of vendor Quality Manual; part-time inspector; selective engineering document review; witness of FAT and specific ITP stages — Exhibit H App H3, §3.4 Table 3-1
- Criticality 3: Inspector visits only; review of purchasing documentation; attendance at coordination meetings — Exhibit H App H3, §3.4 Table 3-1
- Criticality 4: review of final QC and certification dossier only — Exhibit H App H3, §3.4 Table 3-1

### Equipment Inspection Level Allocation — Key Items (Table 4-1)
- Pressure vessels ≥80 barg: Level I; 30–80 barg: Level II; <30 barg non-sour: Level III; sour/lethal: Level I — Exhibit H App H3, §4 Table 4-1
- API 610 pumps, emergency generators, air compressors, ESD safety system PLCs: Level I — Exhibit H App H3, §4 Table 4-1
- Switchgear, MCCs, cranes, all permanently installed lifting equipment, audio/visual alarm systems: Level II — Exhibit H App H3, §4 Table 4-1
- Structural steel Special and First Category: Level II; tertiary/non-structural: Level III — Exhibit H App H3, §4 Table 4-1
- Instrumentation: Safety PLCs (ESD, FG) Level I; control panels/analysers Level II; transmitters/gauges Level III/IV — Exhibit H App H3, §4 Table 4-1
- Deluge/CO2/fixed firefighting skids: Level II; fire fighting portables: Level III — Exhibit H App H3, §4 Table 4-1
- HV cables (≥1kV): Level II; LV/control cables: Level III — Exhibit H App H3, §4 Table 4-1
