## Summary

Exhibit C Rev C (10 pages) defines the Work Time Schedule (WTS) for NLQ EPCIC 10. It specifies 25 Key Dates (KD1–KD25), 23 Milestone Dates (MD1–MD23), and 4 Completion Dates (CD1–CD4) all stated as calendar days/months after Effective Date. It also defines the 5-period window notification mechanism for the two offshore installation campaigns and lists major equipment with PO placement dates TBD before award.

## Key Points

- PLANT consists of: (1) LQ Platform Topside (BI), (2) LQ Platform Substructure, (3) Bridge (BI-BJ), (4) Offshore Integration WORKS — Exhibit C, §1
- All offshore activities are 7-day/week, 24-hour/day with 2 shifts — Exhibit C, §1.2.1
- Environmental restriction: pile driving NOT allowed inside Whale Shark aggregation area 1 June to 30 September every year — Exhibit C, §1.2.9
- OAV: COMPANY provides OAV as base case; 50 POB dedicated to CONTRACTOR at B location — Exhibit C, §1.3
- COMPANY submits Level 3 Engineering/Procurement schedule for APPROVAL within 45 days of ED; full Level 3 WTS including hook-up/commissioning at Level 2 within 3 months of ED — Exhibit C, §1.2
- Document review/APPROVAL period: 15 days by COMPANY and MWS (if submitted on time per MDR) — Exhibit C, §1.2.4
- Factory inspection notice: 14 days minimum — Exhibit C, §1.2.5

### BI Substructure Installation Window (Table 2-1)
- At ED+7d: COMPANY notifies Period 1 window (ED+29m to ED+32m)
- ED+12m: COMPANY narrows to 6-week window within Period 1
- ED+18m: COMPANY narrows to 3-week window within Period 2
- 60d before Period 3: CONTRACTOR selects 2-week window within Period 3
- 30d before Period 4: CONTRACTOR selects 7-day window within Period 4
- 7d before ID1: CONTRACTOR confirms Installation Date 1 (KD40) within Period 5; 4d before ID1: COMPANY provides Access Date 1 — Exhibit C, Table 2-1

### BI Topside and Bridge Installation Window (Table 2-2)
- At ED+7d: COMPANY notifies Period 1 window (ED+31m to ED+34m)
- ED+18m: COMPANY narrows to 6-week window within Period 1; CONTRACTOR confirms BI-BJ Bridge and BI Topside installation sequence
- ED+24m: CONTRACTOR selects 4-week window within Period 2
- 90d before Period 3: CONTRACTOR selects 2-week window
- 30d before Period 4: CONTRACTOR selects 7-day window
- 7d before ID2: CONTRACTOR confirms Installation Date 2 (KD46); COMPANY provides Access Date 2 within 4d — Exhibit C, Table 2-2
