## Summary

PVEP-KM (Nguyen Minh Tuan, CPY) formally confirmed on 15-Jun-2026 that KDP-OOO-00-PS-G-0003 Rev B2 is the approved Equipment Tagging and Numbering Procedure for the KMDD project. Key confirmation: "01" in the tag number = KM-WHP equipment, "02" = DD-WHP equipment. Tags include 2 additional platform-identification digits to distinguish between platforms (e.g., RLR-81-**01**01 for KM-WHP vs RLR-81-**02**01 for DD-WHP).

## Key Points

- **Document confirmed:** KDP-OOO-00-PS-G-0003 Rev B2 — Equipment Tagging and Numbering Procedure
- **Confirmed by:** Nguyen Minh Tuan (CPY / PVEP-KM)
- **Date of confirmation:** 15-Jun-2026

### Tagging Convention
- **"01" in tag** → equipment on KM-WHP (Khanh My Wellhead Platform)
- **"02" in tag** → equipment on DD-WHP (Dam Doi Wellhead Platform)
- The two-digit platform code is embedded within the equipment tag number
- Example: `RLR-81-0101` = KM-WHP relief valve no. 01; `RLR-81-0201` = DD-WHP relief valve no. 01
- This allows all equipment across both platforms to be uniquely identified within the same project tag numbering system

### Significance
- COMPANY (PVEP-KM) confirmation is required before PTSC can finalize equipment tags in deliverables (P&IDs, datasheets, MEL, instrument index)
- The Master Equipment List (MEL) issued same day for IDC (KDP-KMA/DDA-03-LI-M-0001-D0) uses this confirmed convention
- Resolves any ambiguity about platform identification in instrument/equipment tags
