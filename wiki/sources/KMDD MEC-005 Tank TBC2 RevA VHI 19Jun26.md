## Summary
MEC-005 (Open Drain Tank) TBC#02 Rev.A issued to VHI on 19-Jun-2026 with deadline 23-Jun. Three key DKE-raised technical items (from 30-May) are incorporated: coalescer/weir plate position inconsistency, Nozzle N4 dip pipe requirement, and maintenance hatch at coalescer.

## Key Points
- TBC#02 Rev.A sent to VHI (Bidder), deadline 23-Jun-2026
- DKE technical items raised 30-May now formally in TBC:
  1. Calming Baffle/Coalescer/Weir Plate position: HC 10% calculation and GAD show inconsistency
  2. Nozzle N4 (oil outlet) risk: needs dip pipe ~150mm from bottom; outlet elevation must be ≤ Weir Plate elevation
  3. Maintenance hatch required at Coalescer position for access
- Manufacturer restriction: China and India origin not acceptable for tank fabrication
- Open drain tank options (PPS response 19-Jun, see separate source): 4 options being evaluated for inlet basis and dimensions
