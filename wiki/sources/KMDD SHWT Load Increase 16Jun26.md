## Summary

Nguyen Dinh Manh (PTSC PMC) flagged a critical SHWT (Solar Hybrid Wind Turbine) power system load increase to VSP and PVEP-KM on 16-Jun-2026. Based on the 15-Jun-26 load review cut-off, the 24VDC load has increased by approximately 33% compared to the FEED assumption. ICSS load is unofficially estimated at nearly double the FEED value, with official confirmation still pending from VSP. A meeting to align on load basis and way forward was requested for 17-Jun-26 at 9:00 AM.

## Key Points

- **Load increase magnitude:** ~33% increase in overall SHWT system capacity vs. FEED basis (excluding ICSS)
- **ICSS load (unofficial, DD-WHP):** 3,045 W estimated by working team vs. FEED assumption of 1,567 W — almost double
- **Load cut-off date:** 15-Jun-2026 (latest review)
- **Main load drivers (updated/corrected):**
  - WHCP (Well-Head Control Panel)
  - Chemical Injection Package (MEC-006)
  - DEG (Diesel Electric Generator related loads)
  - Telecom / CCTV
- **HVAC assumption challenged:** FEED basis is 8-hour operation; actual continuous 24-hour operation during platform operation being considered → major contributor to load increase
- **Impact risk:** Existing FEED-based SHWT configuration may not accommodate the increase without major impact to:
  - Solar panel / wind turbine arrangement
  - Equipment sizing
  - Platform layout and weight
- **PMC position:** Changes affecting SHWT must be strictly controlled; changes consistently aligned throughout project to minimize impacts on layout, weight, and schedule
- **Documents sent 16-Jun:** DD-WHP 24VDC Load List Calculation (16-Jun-2026 version); draft ICSS + WHCP power consumption calculations
- **HVAC sizing calc:** Promised by PMC for morning of 17-Jun-2026
- **VSP request (Nguyen Hai Son, OCD Design Dept):** 24VDC load list + HVAC sizing calc for Instrumentation Shelter AC units
- **Meeting:** PMC requested meeting 17-Jun-2026 at 09:00 AM to align on official load basis and way forward
