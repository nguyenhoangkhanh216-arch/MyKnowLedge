## Summary

HAZID study report for Khanh My Wellhead Platform (KM-WHP), conducted per ISO 17776 during KMDD Detailed Engineering phase. Identifies 42 hazardous scenarios, confirms 7 Major Accident Hazards (MAHs), and raises 3 recommendations. Document KDP-KMA-03-RP-X-0001-D0, issued for Review (IFR) 03-Jun-2026.

## Key Points

### Study Summary
- **Total hazardous scenarios identified:** 42
- **Major Accident Hazards (MAHs):** 7 confirmed
- **Risk distribution:** Low = 23, Medium = 11, High = 8, Very High = 0
- Scenario H-08.10 (boat transfer) evaluated but excluded from MAH classification

### Primary Hazard Categories Identified
| Hazard ID | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| H-01.02 | Hydrocarbons in formation (well intervention, drilling) |
| H-01.06 | Hydrocarbon gas from production header, test header, FWS export |
| H-06.02 | Overhead objects at main deck, wellhead service deck, cellar deck |
| H-07.01 | Objects under tension (cranes, cables, slings, winches) |
| H-08.04 | Boat collision to vessels and structures |
| H-31.01 | Fire and explosion from hydrocarbon gas |

### Recommendations (3 total)
1. **EPCI — Diesel bunkering hose:** Consider removing to eliminate connected-hose bunkering while Standby Vessel maintains position (B2 risk; COMPANY responsibility)
2. **FEED pending — WAT analysis:** Perform analysis of live Wax Appearance Temperature (WAT) based on actual gas/liquid production (COMPANY responsibility)
3. **FEED pending — PPD test:** Test pour point depressant (PPD) chemical on condensate after initial production (COMPANY responsibility)
