## Summary

Internal KMDD design thread (Apr–Jun 2026) covering temperature monitoring for the cold vent line on the vent boom. INS team to configure thermowell/thermal element installation on the vent boom; STR team to design the structural access stairway and landing required for maintenance of the removable thermal element. Vent boom: 21.5m length, 6.5 MT additional structural weight.

## Key Points

- **Topic:** Cold vent line temperature monitoring — thermowell/thermal element on the vent boom
- **Why temperature monitoring:** Cold vent can discharge at very low temperatures (Joule-Thomson cooling during depressuring); temperature sensor needed to detect extreme cold conditions and trigger alarms
- **INS scope (Trịnh Xuân Cảnh):** Build temperature monitoring for the cold vent line — includes: mounting configuration for thermowell, sensor position along the vent boom (how far up the tip), and cable routing to instrument junction box
- **STR scope (Trần Nguyên Giáp):** Design access stairway + landing at vent boom for removable thermal element maintenance; also provide 3D Model with access way
- **Piping/Process scope (Trần Đức Trung):** Perform MTO (material take-off) for the vent boom access area
- **Vent boom dimensions:** 21.5m total length; stair/landing adds 6.5 MT to structural weight
- **Drawing reference:** `KDP-DDA-03-DW-S-VentBoom-Concept.pdf` (DD-WHP vent boom concept drawing)
- **Lead:** Nguyễn Hải Nam (Process/Lead Engineer, Engineering Dept)
- **Timeline:** STR concept provided Apr 20; STR design actioned Jun 1; INS temperature monitoring requested Jun 13
