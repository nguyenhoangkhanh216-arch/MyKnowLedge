## Summary

Appendix A9 (Rev A) is the detailed pre-commissioning and commissioning specification for NLQ EPCIC 10. It governs the OPERCOM/ICAPS methodology, organisational requirements, pre-commissioning engineering and execution, commissioning engineering and execution, functional tests, operational tests, punch list management, piping reinstatement, Transfer of Care and Custody, and Transfer to Operations.

## Key Points

### Execution Plan and General Requirements (§3)
- CONTRACTOR shall obtain COMPANY APPROVAL for all Systems and Sub-systems to be performed during each Hook-up and Commissioning campaign — Exhibit A App A9, §3.1
- CONTRACTOR shall select OPERCOM methodology and an APPROVED SUBCONTRACTOR to develop Pre-commissioning/Commissioning Procedures, ICAPS database, and Operational Test Procedures — Exhibit A App A9, §3.1
- Commissioning Manager (Key Personnel) shall be mobilised at ED+2 months; shall not have participated in Engineering/Construction/Pre-commissioning operations — Exhibit A App A9, §3.1
- CONTRACTOR shall select APPROVED SUBCONTRACTOR from Exhibit J for N2–He Leak Tests and flange management — Exhibit A App A9, §3.1
- COMPANY shall have full access to ICAPS database at all stages for all pre-commissioning and commissioning activities — Exhibit A App A9, §3.1
- CONTRACTOR shall acquire all ICAPS software licenses; design ICAPS network for daily progress and backup on all worksites — Exhibit A App A9, §3.3
- Pre-commissioning and Commissioning SUBCONTRACTORS for chemical cleaning and lube oil flushing shall be APPROVED SUBCONTRACTORS — Exhibit A App A9, §3.1

### Execution Plan (§3.2)
- Execution Plan shall cover all phases onshore/offshore; include proposed start-up sequences; define all means to achieve timely TOM — Exhibit A App A9, §3.2
- Preservation scope must include construction equipment preparation, commissioning resources for utilities, maintenance/inspection plan for temporary equipment, preservation and de-preservation plan — Exhibit A App A9, §3.2
- Execution Plan shall define pre-commissioning/commissioning organisation: org charts, individual job descriptions, mobilisation/demobilisation plans, roles and responsibilities — Exhibit A App A9, §3.2
- Organisation charts for Pre-commissioning team and Commissioning team shall be separate — Exhibit A App A9, §3.2

### Pre-commissioning Engineering (§4.1)
- Pre-commissioning activities shall be planned at least 3 months before execution start — Exhibit A App A9, §4.1
- SYSTEMS and SUB-SYSTEMS definition (using latest CAD P&IDs, D&IDs, SLDs) submitted to COMPANY for APPROVAL — Exhibit A App A9, §4.1
- ICAPS Pre-commissioning data base (Status Index) shall be an exhaustive list arranged per SUB-SYSTEM, discipline, and type of equipment — Exhibit A App A9, §4.1
- 100% of Ex equipment installed in potentially hazardous areas must be delivered with proper Ex certificate; RFID tags installed and information uploaded before initial detailed inspection — Exhibit A App A9, §4.1
- All PSVs shall be calibrated at WORKSITE with third-party witness prior to onshore commissioning; PSVs for packages calibrated by Package Vendor with third-party witness before FAT — Exhibit A App A9, §4.1
- Time between PSV certification and TOM handover shall not exceed 6 months — Exhibit A App A9, §4.1
- Fragile devices (ESDVs, SDVs, BDVs, XVs, MOVs, PSVs, rupture disks, critical manual valves, in-line instrumentation) shall be removed before hydro testing and cleaning — Exhibit A App A9, §4.1

### Pre-commissioning Execution (§4.2)
- 100% initial detailed inspection of Ex equipment using COMPANY APPROVED Ex Inspectors prior to RFC issuance (disciplines: Electrical, Instrumentation, HVAC, Telecom) — Exhibit A App A9, §4.2
- COMPANY APPROVED Third Party Inspector shall witness initial detailed inspection of at least 30% of each Ex equipment type prior to RFC — Exhibit A App A9, §4.2
- Critical Valve Leak Test: systematic low-pressure passing test on critical valves upon reception and before installation; tested in closed position both directions at 5 barg N2 — Exhibit A App A9, §4.2
- Enclosure integrity test (door fan test per NFPA 2001) shall be carried out on each E/I Room — Exhibit A App A9, §4.2

### RFC Dossier Content (§4)
- Ready for Commissioning dossier per SUB-SYSTEM must include: RFCC; SUB-SYSTEM description and marked-up drawings; updated punch lists; specific procedures; Pre-commissioning Status Index; Check Sheets and Test Sheets by discipline; As Pre-commissioned drawings; QC reports; modifications list; VENDOR shop test reports and documentation — Exhibit A App A9, §4

### General Commissioning (§5)
- CONTRACTOR shall maximise commissioning activities prior to shipment from WORKSHOP/WORKSITE — Exhibit A App A9, §5
- N2–He leak test on all SUB-SYSTEMS with hydrocarbon lines: performed Onshore and repeated Offshore after hook-up — Exhibit A App A9, §5
- Diesel Generator shall be load tested at 100% capacity (Active and Reactive Power); impact and rejection tested — Exhibit A App A9, §5(vii–viii)
- UPS and Battery set shall be load tested at 100% capacity — Exhibit A App A9, §5(ix)
- ATEX equipment: specific PTW (+JRA) required for any intervention when platform is under Hydrocarbon — Exhibit A App A9, §5

### Commissioning Engineering (§5.1)
- Commissioning procedures must be issued at least 3 months before commissioning execution starts — Exhibit A App A9, §5.1
- PLANT START-UP sequence finalised and agreed with COMPANY — Exhibit A App A9, §5.1
- Operational Test Procedures (OTPs) with acceptance/rejection criteria shall be submitted for COMPANY APPROVAL — Exhibit A App A9, §5.1
- CONTRACTOR Authorised Fluids: Corrosion Inhibitor (downhole/topside), Demulsifier, Biocide, Antifoam, Hypochlorite, Nitrogen Bottle Rack (back-up), Diesel (storage), Inergen — Exhibit A App A9, §5.1
- COMPANY-Only Fluids: Purge Gas, Gas Lift, Water Injection, Crude Oil, HP Flare, Wet Gas, High Voltage Electrical Network — Exhibit A App A9, §5.1

### Commissioning Execution (§5.2)
- Functional Tests: carried out on each and every loop; random checks NOT allowed; individual logics (ESD, F&G) and intercommunication links — Exhibit A App A9, §5.2.2
- Random Inergen release test on 2 rooms per platform per SD-NOC-EC-603 and NFPA 2001 — Exhibit A App A9, §5.2.2
- Operational Tests (OT): each SUB-SYSTEM subject to at least one OT; COMPANY to witness with minimum 3 days advance notice — Exhibit A App A9, §5.2.3
- Punch list categories: A (cleared before RFC); B (cleared before AOC); C (cleared before Completion Certificate) — COMPANY approves category ratings — Exhibit A App A9, §5.2.5
- Carry-over of WORK from WORKSITE to SITE shall be completed at SITE by CONTRACTOR at no additional cost — Exhibit A App A9, §5.2.5
- AOC Dossier must include: AOC certificate; system description and marked-up drawings; updated punch lists; commissioning status index; check/test sheets by discipline; As Commissioned drawings (P&IDs, SLDs, TSLDs, Safety Logic, Alarm/Trip/Set Point, Electrical, Mechanical, Inhibit/Overrides); isolation register; preservation/maintenance records; spare parts; first fill list; O&M manual — Exhibit A App A9, §5

### Transfer of Care and Custody (§6)
- At predefined stage, PTW management switches from CONTRACTOR to COMPANY (Transfer of Care and Custody — TCC) — Exhibit A App A9, §6
- Additional transfers at TCC: Electrical LOTO Management; Instrument Inhibits and Overrides Management; Mechanical Isolation Management — Exhibit A App A9, §6

### Transfer to Operations (§7)
- CONTRACTOR responsible for operation, care, and maintenance of started-up systems until TOM to COMPANY — Exhibit A App A9, §7.1
- CONTRACTOR must demonstrate facilities have not suffered from use and are in brand new condition prior to handover — Exhibit A App A9, §7.1
- TOM Dossier: includes TOM certificate; TOM corresponding System/Sub-system List; corresponding AOC dossiers and AOCs — Exhibit A App A9, §7.2
- Commissioning spare parts and special tools shall be handed over to COMPANY prior to TOMC signature — Exhibit A App A9, §7.2

### JSA/RA (§8)
- CONTRACTOR shall develop JSA and risk assessment in all execution procedures — Exhibit A App A9, §8
- Specific safety training program for all commissioning/pre-commissioning PERSONNEL (including SUBCONTRACTORS, VENDORS, COMPANY) covering PTW requirements — Exhibit A App A9, §8
- Electrical certification of PERSONNEL per international standards, COMPANY requirements, and local regulations — Exhibit A App A9, §8
