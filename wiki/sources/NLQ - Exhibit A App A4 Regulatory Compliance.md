## Summary

Appendix A4 (Rev A) defines CONTRACTOR's regulatory compliance duties and deliverables for the NLQ EPCIC 10. It supplements Article 23 of the AGREEMENT and Exhibit A Clause 4.8. CONTRACTOR bears full responsibility for all regulatory and environmental aspects of the WORK, including developing and implementing a Regulatory Compliance Plan (RCP).

## Key Points

### Scope and Objectives (§1–§2)
- PLANT must be designed, constructed, installed, and commissioned incorporating regulatory and environmental requirements — Exhibit A App A4, §2(i)
- PLANT must comply with SPECIFICATIONS and all applicable government regulations, statutes, and permit requirements — Exhibit A App A4, §2(ii)
- WORK must be performed consistently with COMPANY standards (Exhibit E): environmentally sound execution; personnel protection; management leadership accountability — Exhibit A App A4, §2(iii)
- Full compliance with applicable local and international laws must result in: no non-compliance notices from governing authorities; no environmental incidents — Exhibit A App A4, §2(iv)

### CONTRACTOR Duties (§3)
- CONTRACTOR is responsible for all regulatory and environmental aspects of the WORK, including SUBCONTRACTORS — Exhibit A App A4, §3(i)
- CONTRACTOR must comply with all relevant government regulations and laws regarding performance of the WORK — Exhibit A App A4, §3(ii)
- CONTRACTOR must develop, document, and implement regulatory and environmental policies consistent with COMPANY standards in Exhibit E — Exhibit A App A4, §3(iii)
- CONTRACTOR must identify and obtain all necessary approvals, licenses, and permits required to execute the WORK — Exhibit A App A4, §3(vi)
- CONTRACTOR must meet all conditions of government agreements and permits, including rectifying inconsistencies between different permits prior to start of WORK — Exhibit A App A4, §3(vii)
- CONTRACTOR must conduct environmental analyses, studies, and assessments for all relevant phases per regulatory and COMPANY requirements — Exhibit A App A4, §3(ix)
- CONTRACTOR must develop and conduct regulatory and environmental training programs for supervisory and workforce personnel — Exhibit A App A4, §3(x)
- CONTRACTOR must prepare and issue monthly regulatory and environmental reports to COMPANY as part of the monthly progress report — Exhibit A App A4, §3(xii)
- CONTRACTOR must develop, implement, and maintain Regulatory and Environmental Management Plans; must monitor performance against these Plans — Exhibit A App A4, §3(xvii)
- CONTRACTOR must evaluate effectiveness of Plans and make appropriate modifications when necessary — Exhibit A App A4, §3(xviii)

### Regulatory Compliance Plan (RCP) Content (§4)
- RCP shall describe the processes CONTRACTOR will have in place to ensure all applicable laws and regulations are followed during performance of the WORK — Exhibit A App A4, §4(i)
- RCP scope and objectives: describe methods, processes, timing, and actions to limit non-compliance incidents and schedule impacts — Exhibit A App A4, §4(ii)
- RCP shall identify regulatory compliance organisation including staffing requirements; clearly define and document roles and responsibilities for site management, supervisory personnel, and workforce — Exhibit A App A4, §4(v)
- RCP shall include process for identifying relevant laws and regulations (local, national, international) and associated permits, licenses, approvals, notifications — Exhibit A App A4, §4(vi)(a)
- Permits and approvals listed in RCP shall include but are not limited to: permit to operate as oil service contractor; permits for service boats; import of equipment/materials; work permits and visas; communications; Navy notification; industrial consumer (fuel storage) license; effluent discharge permits — Exhibit A App A4, §4(vi)(b)
- Applicable government regulations shall include but not be limited to: labour laws; tax laws; contracting laws; customs laws; laws governing offshore operations; occupational health and safety; emissions/discharges; waste disposal — Exhibit A App A4, §4(vi)(c)
- RCP shall include process for monitoring compliance and timely submittals of permit applications/notifications — Exhibit A App A4, §4(vi)(d)
- RCP shall describe processes for monitoring/auditing operations for regulatory compliance; identifying and reporting non-compliance incidents; developing learnings and transmitting to organisation — Exhibit A App A4, §4(vi)(g)
- RCP shall include description of qualifications and additional training required for personnel responsible for regulatory compliance — Exhibit A App A4, §4(vii)
