## Summary

Communication matrix issued by Jadestone Energy (JSE) to PTSC M&C on 04-Jun-2026 for Early Detailed Engineering (EDE) works on the NDUM Flexible Riser package. Defines who at JSE receives which types of documents: engineering documents, MOM/TQs, letters, technical emails, interface data, TBCs, procurement documents, commercial documents, PAFs/timesheets, and invoices.

## Key Points

- **Engineering Manager / Primary Technical Contact:** Rob Laybourne (rlaybourne@jadestone-energy.com) — TO for MOM/TQ, technical clarification emails, engineering docs; CC for most other categories
- **Project Manager:** Graham Korbosky (gkorbosky@jadestone-energy.com) — CC for most; TO for LOA/PO approval
- **Project Engineer / Interface:** Do Tung Lam (dtlam@jadestone-energy.com) — TO for interface data requests; CC for engineering docs, TBC, procurement
- **Pipeline Engineer:** John Falconer (jfalconer@jadestone-energy.com) — TO for TBC (pipeline); CC for procurement
- **Contracts Engineer:** Nguyen Trong Duy (ntduy@jadestone-energy.com) — TO for letters, commercial docs review, PAFs
- **Interface Engineer:** Nguyen Le Kim Cuong (nlkcuong@jadestone-energy.com) — CC for engineering, TBC, procurement
- **Document Controller:** Vuong Bao Thu (vbthu@jadestone-energy.com) — TO for engineering documents and transmittals
- **Rule 1:** All Letters → addressed to PM (Korbosky) but emailed to Document Control (DC)
- **Rule 2:** Discipline technical emails arranged directly between JSE and PTSC engineers; any changes with design/schedule/cost impact → formal TQ or MOC submission to Engineering Manager
