## Summary

Internal PTSC M&C email thread (Jan–Mar 2026) covering early preparation to bid the LDV-B Early Engineering contract. Murphy Oil sent an EOI on 23-Jan-2026. PTSC's Engineering and Commercial teams benchmarked MTO/weights against LDV-A to prepare a cost estimate. A legal risk was also flagged: doing Early Engineering may limit eligibility to bid the subsequent EPCIC.

## Key Points

- **Trigger:** Murphy Oil EOI received 23-Jan-2026; BoD asked team to evaluate feasibility and provide preliminary EPCIC price estimate
- **Early Engineering duration:** ~4–5 months (Trần Anh Kim assessment)
- **Expected EPCIC ED date:** Q1–early Q2 2027 if Early Engineering starts Jul-2026
- **PTSC resource:** Sufficient if no major concurrent project (MM project risk noted)
- **High-level benchmark factors:**
  - LDV-B Topside = 0.8 × KLWB Topside [BLB2]
  - LDV-B Jacket = KLWB Jacket [BLB2]
  - LDV-B Pipeline = per Benchmark Factor_PLR_LDVB file
- **Benchmark priority:** LDV-A (mandatory) → other completed projects (if LDV-A factor >1.5) → recent bid projects
- **Benchmark scope per discipline:**
  - STR bulks: weight in tonnes + spec/grade
  - Piping: weight per material type (CS, SS, DSS, CuNi, etc.)
  - E&I: factor per material category
  - Equipment: factor per tag item (not per package)
- **Legal concern:** PTSC needs to verify legal eligibility to bid EPCIC after performing Early Engineering (conflict of interest under Vietnamese procurement law)
- **Packages missing benchmark (Mar-Apr 2026):** Piping (Bolts, Gaskets, Pipe Support, Insulation, Flexible Hose, Flame Arrestor, etc.), E&I cables/glands/trays/JBs, Instrument packages (ICSS, WHCP, AOV, PSV, flowmeters, F&G), Safety (DIFFS), Structure (Fab Aids)
