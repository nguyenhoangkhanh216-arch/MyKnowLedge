## Summary
Exhibit E (Rev D) and its appendices (E1–E4) define the Design Dossier for the NLQ EPCIC 10 project. It establishes the hierarchy of governing documents, specifies which documents CONTRACTOR must endorse (TBE), and lists all additional technical and geotechnical references CONTRACTOR must rely upon during execution.

## Key Points
- Document precedence order: (1) Qatar law → (2) Project docs & Particular Specifications → (3) International standards → (4) COMPANY standards → (5) Other codes → (6) Industry practice. If conflict remains, CONTRACTOR applies the most stringent requirement at no extra cost. — Exhibit E, §2
- Design Dossier comprises: (a) CFT Technical Specifications (FSS, Safety Concepts), (b) Al-Shaheen existing facilities documentation, (c) Additional technical documents to rely upon, (d) COMPANY Standards. — Exhibit E, §4
- **App E1 (TBE documents):** CONTRACTOR must endorse (TBE) 4 key documents: NLQ FSS (NLQ01-MPEN-ASBII-00-390001), NLQ Safety Concept (NLQ01-MPEN-ASBII-00-390002_rev01), NLQ A FSS, A-Location Safety Concept (ALS-AY-CPDPA-SAF-0001_rev02). List of Queries and Exceptions raised during CFT to be attached prior to EPC award. — Exhibit E App E1, §1
- **App E2 (Brownfield integration):** B-Location and A-Location as-built documentation available on iView at Effective Date; native files requested via RFI process. BH and BJ platforms' brownfield integration work is fully within CONTRACTOR's scope from Effective Date to Completion Certificate. TWO possible scenarios at Effective Date: coordinate with OTHER CONTRACTOR or directly with COMPANY. — Exhibit E App E2, §§1–3
- **App E4 (Additional documents):** Key rely-upon documents include geophysical survey (DC03/DC04/DC08 well locations), geotechnical report (Q-NOC-QAT-0689 Rev4), HAZAN study (ASBYY), ENVID report, Environmental Permit (CTC), Safety Concept as-built (ALS-BY-HIMR-SAF-0003), Third Party Toxic Assessment (ARC), and MA WHP Relief/Cold Vent study. A-Location adds Safety Concept ALS-AY-HIMR-SAF-0003 and geotechnical reports for AS-AD platform. — Exhibit E App E4, Tables 1-1 through 2-3
