### An Phu Ferrite Spec Dispute (Ongoing)
- **PTSC spec:** 45–55% ferrite content in duplex stainless steel (DSS) castings
- **Italian foundry (Technical Srl) position:**
  - Standard range is 35–55% ferrite (per NORSOK M-630 and industry norms)
  - 45–55% range is unusually narrow and contradicts low-temperature toughness requirements
  - For good low-temp impact toughness, austenitic structure is favored → ~40–42% ferrite is optimal
  - Requests Customer (PTSC/PVEP-KM) clarification — foundry will not commit to 45-55% without formal CPY endorsement
- **Status:** Dispute remains open; PTSC must respond with either an updated DTS or formal clarification letter to resolve
