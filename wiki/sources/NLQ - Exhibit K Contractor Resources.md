## Summary
Exhibit K Rev A defines minimum CONTRACTOR organisation, facilities, and resources requirements including 12 Key Personnel positions (Level 1 and 2), 5 Engineering Key Personnel leads, and specific mobilisation periods for each role. Key Personnel substitution triggers liquidated damages per Exhibit B §9.2.

## Key Points
- **12 Key Personnel positions** with Level 1 (most critical) or Level 2 designations — Exhibit K Table 3-1.
- **Level 1 Key Personnel** (highest LD exposure): Project Manager (CONTRACTOR REP), HSE Manager, Engineering Manager, QA/QC Manager, Procurement Manager, Project Control Manager — Exhibit K Table 3-1.
- **Level 2 Key Personnel**: Lead Architectural Engineer, Interface Manager, Commissioning Manager, Digital Information Manager, Transport & Installation Manager, Construction Manager (each yard = 1 Key Person if multiple yards) — Exhibit K Table 3-1.
- Most Level 1 personnel required **ED to PAC** (full contract duration) — Exhibit K Table 3-1.
- T&I Manager required from **ED+3 months to last Marine Spread Release Certificate** — Exhibit K Table 3-1.
- **5 Engineering Key Personnel** (full-time, Level 2): Lead Architectural Engineer, Lead Structural Engineer, Lead HVAC Engineer, Lead Electrical Engineer, Lead Naval Architect Engineer — all from ED to 100% detail engineering finalised; part-time acceptable after DE — Exhibit K Table 4-1.
- **Construction Key Personnel**: Yard Superintendent per fabrication yard — 3 months prior to first steel cut through loadout — Exhibit K Table 7-1.
- **Commissioning Site Lead** (Level 2): 6 months before commissioning start through Offshore Commissioning Completion — Exhibit K Table 8-1.
- CONTRACTOR must submit org chart with names, positions, responsibilities, mobilisation/demobilisation dates for APPROVAL — Exhibit K §2.1.
- **COMPANY interview right**: COMPANY may interview all nominated Key Personnel — Exhibit K §5.1.
- CONTRACTOR org includes: PMT, Engineering+ICSS Integration, Procurement, Substructure/Topside/Bridge Construction, Onshore Fabrication, Offshore Hook-up+Commissioning, Marine, Pre-commissioning, Onshore Commissioning, QA/QC, Interface Management — Exhibit K §2.3.
- All Key Personnel resumes to be submitted to COMPANY for APPROVAL before mobilisation — Exhibit K §2.2.
