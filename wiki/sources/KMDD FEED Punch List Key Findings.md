## Summary

FEED Verification Report is a contract obligation: submit IFR within 30 days of Effective Date (deadline 09-May-2026) and final version within 60 days. Failure to raise issues within 30 days means losing the right to raise Change Orders. PMC (PTSC) identified four critical FEED findings with potential cost and schedule impact that must be logged in the FEED Verification Register.

## Key Points

- **Contract obligation:** 30 days after ED = IFR FEED Verification Report must be submitted to COMPANY (09-May-2026 deadline); 60 days = final version
- **Change Order right:** If issues not raised formally within 30 days, contractor loses the right to claim change orders for those issues later
- **Parties contributing:** PMC (PTSC), VSP (OCD), NIPI (subcontractor to VSP)
- **Report structure:** Introduction, Methodology, FEED Verification Register Summary, FEED Punchlist Summary, Critical Findings with Cost/Schedule Impact
- **Four critical FEED findings flagged by PMC:**
  1. **Generator capacity shortfall:** Power cuts in FEED → insufficient capacity for key equipment during start-up (Closed Drain pumps, Methanol pumps, WHCP pumps) — ELE team owner

- **NIPI commitment:** Will submit FEED verification register by 07-May-2026
