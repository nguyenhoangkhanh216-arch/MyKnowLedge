## Summary

Appendix H1 Rev A (7 pages) defines the Conformity Assessment requirements for the NLQ EPCIC 10 project, specifying when Registration/Classification, Certifying Authority (CA), Basic Certification (BC), Quality Surveillance (QS), or Quality Control (QC) is required for each type of installation and equipment across all project phases (Engineering, Procurement, Installation, Commissioning).

## Key Points

### Conformity Assessment Levels — Hierarchy
- Five levels of conformity assessment from highest to lowest: Registration/Classification (RG), Certifying Authority (CA), Basic Certification (BC), Quality Surveillance (QS), Quality Control (QC) — Exhibit H App H1, §5
- CA must be accredited third-party; BC is non-accredited third-party; both contracted by CONTRACTOR as ancillary services — Exhibit H App H1, §3
- Marine Warranty Surveyor (MWS) contracted by COMPANY (not CONTRACTOR); where CA and MWS scope overlap, both must perform — Exhibit H App H1, §2

### Conformity Assessment by Equipment Type — COMPANY Minimum Requirements
- Floating and marine systems (hull, cargo, mooring, offloading): Registration (RG) at all phases E/P/I/C — Exhibit H App H1, §6
- Safety and lifting equipment (safety detection, HIPPS, functional safety, SIL verification, firefighting, evacuation): CA at all phases E/P/I/C — Exhibit H App H1, §6
- Main bearing structure (foundations, jacket, topside primary structure including flare, bridge): CA for E/P/I; QC only for C — Exhibit H App H1, §6
- Transportation systems (pipelines, flexible lines, umbilicals, risers): CA for E/P/I; QC for C — Exhibit H App H1, §6
- Critical Production Equipment (rotating machines, valves, subsea equipment, HP vessels/exchangers): CA for E/P; QC for I and C — Exhibit H App H1, §6
- Other structure and topsides (onshore/offshore structures, brownfield, utility systems): BC for E; CA or QC for P; QC for I and C — Exhibit H App H1, §6

### Regulatory Compliance — Qatar Law
- CONTRACTOR must comply with Qatar Decree Law No. 4 of 1977 on Preserving Petroleum Resources — Exhibit H App H1, §7
- CONTRACTOR shall prepare a Plant Certification Scope of Work with the CA to comply with this law — Exhibit H App H1, §7
- CA must issue 4 certificates of conformity (Design, Procurement/Fabrication/Construction, Installation, overall PLANT) from CA branch based in Qatar — Exhibit H App H1, §7
- Monthly PLANT Certification meeting: organised by CONTRACTOR with CA and COMPANY — Exhibit H App H1, §7

### CA Reporting Requirements
- CA issues monthly reports to CONTRACTOR and COMPANY covering: progress of conformity assessment, outstanding matters, problems — Exhibit H App H1, §8
- CA issues close-out report covering their own scope at CONTRACT completion — Exhibit H App H1, §10
- CONTRACTOR maintains a continuously updated certification dossier of all third-party records; COMPANY may request full/partial copy at any time — Exhibit H App H1, §9
